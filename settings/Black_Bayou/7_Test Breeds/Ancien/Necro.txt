</FONT><FONT  COLOR="#800000" SIZE=5 PTSIZE=16><I>Necromancer Spells<BR>
</FONT><FONT  COLOR="#000000" SIZE=5 PTSIZE=16><BR>
</P><P ALIGN=LEFT></FONT><FONT  SIZE=3 PTSIZE=10><B></I>Necrosis<BR>
</B>     By touching the limb of a victim, the necromancer can cause it to wither and shrivel, becomming useless.  <BR>
     <I>To Purchase: 6 points.  </I>[<I>system: this is a resisted spell.  Duration: one night.  the limb slowly grows back over a week's time, regardless of breed.  Range: touch.</I>]<BR>
<BR>
<B>Feign Death<BR>
     </B>By casting this spell, the necromancer can force the target into a state that resembles death.  Their skin pales, wrapping tightly against their bones.  Their eyes sink in.  Their lips curl back over their teeth in a hideous grin.  Their bodily functions slow to the point that a heart monitor would not even register a heart beat.<BR>
     <I>To Purchase: 4 points.  </I>[<I>system: this is a non-resisted spell with a target number of 12 to succeed.  It can not be used on an unwilling subject.  Duration: 10 minutes.  Range: touch.</I>]<BR>
<BR>
<B>Dark Channels<BR>
     </B>This spell enables the necromancer to contact the souls of the recently dead.  The target must not have been dead for more than twenty-four hours prior to the casting of this spell.  <BR>
     <I>To Purchase: 2 points.  </I>[<I>system: this is a non-resisted spell with a target number of 12 to be successful.  The necromancer get to ask a number of questions equal to the number of points scored over 12.  The spirit can only answer questions about it's things it knew in it's pervious life.  The answers must be answered honestly, but can be as cryptic as the spirit wants.  Duration: Instantaneous.  Range: touch.</I>]<BR>
<BR>
<B>Darkness<BR>
     </B>This spell creates an area of inky-blackness that can not be seen through, even with night vision or cat's eyes.  Sonar, however, still operates, as does scent of sight.  The inky blackness seems to come from the very shadows itself, but is unaffected by shadow manipulation.  It extends from a center point to a radius of fifty yards.<BR>
     <I>To Purchase: 4 points.  </I>[<I>system: this is a non-resisted spell with a target numeber of 14 to be successful.  Duration: 5 Minutes / 10 rounds.  Range: sight.</I>]<BR>
<BR>
<B>Touch of Death<BR>
     </B>By casting this spell, negative forces fill the necromancer's limbs with eerie black electrical energies.  This allows the necromancer to cause damage simply by touch.  When used in conjunction with a punch or kick, it causes extra damage.  A loud popping sound is heard as contact is made.<BR>
     <I>To Purchase: 6 points.  </I>[<I>system: this is a non-resisted spell with a target number of 16 to be successful.  Damage from touch, or added to a hand-to-hand attack, is 2.  Duration: 2 rounds.  Range: touch</I>]<BR>
<BR>
<B>Shroud<BR>
</P><P ALIGN=JUSTIFY></B>     By concentrating, the necromancer can create  a thick dark green fog to surround him, forcing attacks to cause 1/2 their normal damage rounded down.  Thus, attacks causing 1 point of damage will cause 0.<B><BR>
     </B><I>To Purchase: 6 Points.  </I>[<I>system: this is a non-resisted spell with a target number of 14 to succeed.  Duration: 5 minutes / 10 rounds or if the caster physically attacks.  Range: Self.</I>]<BR>
<BR>
<B>Spirit Form<BR>
     </B>The body of the necromancer, upon completion of this spell, takes on an etherial quality.  He becomes transleucient, physical objects having absolutly no effect on him.  He can pass through solid objects as if they were not there.  The only things that can effect him are fire and the electrical currents of tasers.  Althought the necromancer in spirit form can not manupulate physical object, he can cast spells that do not require touch.<BR>
     <I>To Purchase: 6 points.  </I>[<I>system: this is a non-resisted spell with a target number of 14 ro succeed.  Duration: 10 minutes.  Range: Self.</I>]<BR>
<BR>
<B>Soul Coffin<BR>
      </B>This spell allows the caster to trap the soul of the recently dead and trap it in a physical container.  The target creature must not have been dead for longer than two hours.  The soul is converted into a think, black, tar-like substance that when embibed, restores 4 Soul points to the necromancer, or any Lamia who drinks it.  This bonus will never increase the caster's Soul points beyond their normal Base Soul.<BR>
     <I>To Purchase: 6 points.  </I>[<I>system: this is a non-resisted spell with a target number of 16 to succeed.  Only PC souls, or approved NPC souls may be trapped and used in this manner.  A Host must be present to cast this spell.  Duration: Instantaneous.  Range: Touch.</I>]<BR>
<BR>
<B>Rigormortis<BR>
     </B>With an outstretched hand, the necromancer appears to be clutching something.  Symbolically, the victim's heart.  The victim feels the cold grasp of fingers crushing it's heart.  A deep black aura of crackling energy envelopes the necromancer's hand.  the victim's limbs become numbingly cold and stiff.  Within seconds, the victim is completely paralyzed, unable to move, or even use mindspeak.  The necromancer must concentrate to continue the effects, and can not attack nor cast other spells.  He may only move.<BR>
     <I>To Purchase: 4 points.  </I>[<I>system: this is a resisted spell.  Duration: 5 minutes / 10 rounds.  Range: sight.</I>]<BR>
<BR>
<B>Plague<BR>
     </B>Upon touching the victim, the necromancer can cause intese sickness.  Boils, lesions, seeping abscesses, etc. will appear all over the victim's flesh.  This causes intense weakness and the victim will suffer -3 to their Body stat.<BR>
     <I>To Purchase: 6 points.  </I>[<I>system: this is a resisted spell.  Duration: the rest of a night, unless healed mystically by an angel.  Range: touch.</I>]<BR>
<BR>
<B>Gnade</B><BR>
     The necromancer preforms a ritual which includes boiling a live cat, then skinning it.  This is then dropped into a large pot, with a variety of ingredients.  a severed finger, the head of a venemous snake, dirt from a grave, etc.  The end result is a very deadly poison which, when ingested, initiall causes 2 points of damage to Body.  Every ten minutes thereafter, the victim suffers an additional 1 point of damage to Body as the poison rips through their veins.  The only cure is and angel's healing powers or Major Medical skill treatment.  If Laying on Hands is used, the victim will be saved, but the healer will take the effects immediatly.  The victim will see visions, being intense halucinations, and can feel the boiling of their blood.<BR>
     <I>To Purchase: 6 points.  </I>[<I>system: this is a non-resisted spell with a target number of 16 to be successful.  Duration: Instantaneous.  Range: touch.</I>]<BR>
<BR>
<B>Trap The Spirit<BR>
     </B>Occasionally, a spirit being dealth with is accidentily set free.  This allows a potentially dangerous spirit the freedom to effect the world of the living.  Trap the Spirit is a ritual of thirty minutes, designed to trap the spirit in a circle of power so that it may be banished.<BR>
     <I>To Purchase: 4 points.  </I>[<I>system: this is a resisted spell.  Duration: 2 hours.  Range: Sight.</I>]<BR>
<B><BR>
Banishment<BR>
</B>     In dealing with numerous spirits of many different types, the necromancer occasionally has the need to banish them.  A ritual of one hour is required with an apporpriate sacrifice, depending on the level of the spell being used, and the destination of the spirit.<BR>
     <I>To Purchase: 4 points for level 1, +4 points for level 2, + 6 points for level 3 and +8 points for level 4.  </I>[<I>system: this is a non-resisted spell with a target number depending on the level being used.  Level 1: 14, Level 2: 16, Level 3: 18, Level 4: 20.<BR>
<B>Level 1: </B>This ritual will cause the spirit to be banished from the realm of the living.<BR>
<B>Level 2: </B>This ritual will cause the spirit to be banished to purgatory.<BR>
<B>Level 3: </B>This ritual will cause the spirit to be banished to the fiery depths of hell.<BR>
<B>Level 4: </B>This ritual will cause the spirit to be utterly destroyed.<BR>
<B>Special Note: </B> In order for the spirit to be banished, it must first be successfully trapped using the Trap the Spirit spell.</I>]<BR>
