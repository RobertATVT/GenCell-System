Physical Description

A lithe figure standing at a graceful 5'9", her well muscled body sculpted from the purest marble, yet not losing any femininity in her poise. If anything, watching her at a distance, she would remind you of a lioness watching the herds, sleek, feral, with a graceful power in every step. A tumbling mass of silver curls dances down her back like a raging waterfall in the moonlight. Eyes of mercury quicksilver flash warily over everything, taking note of every nuance, every person, every movement, the eyes of a killer. Lips of perfect cerise, often curling into a smile with some secret knowledge hidden behind it, like the Cheshire cat mocking Alice. Often found in her preferred color of the shadows, clad in supple leather in the darkest black. Boots curving up to her thighs, no heel or sole, in their stead, an extra layer of thick leather hushing her every step. A dagger of platinum always present in an intricately worked scabbard on her thigh, hidden almost by the top of the boot, it's hilt wrapped in black leather as well, perfectly fitted to her hand, a single emerald carved into a scarab embedded in the top of the hilt. A cashmere cat-suit of dove gray hugs every curve like a glove, a vest of matching ebony leather cinched around her, holding various accouterments of her trade.
Background
Arriving in Volletta, unconscious under a tree outside of the Facade, left for dead by her attackers with a gash on her temple, and no memory, she stumbled into the Facade. Two dark shadows, their names Nuit and Billaud, take her in, coaxing her into the back room with promises of tending her wounds. Immediately, Billaud feeds upon her like a helpless lamb in her weakened state, leaving her to forget his attack and attribute her wounds on her neck to her earlier attack. Confused, she befriends and falls in love with Sky, who, after revealing his true form during an attack, during which she shielded him with her own body to protect him, the terror and incomprehension of his form breaking through the amnesia. Sending her reeling and into the brink of madness, she allows Nuit to take her, forever ending her mortal bonds. Haunted with ghosts and nightmares of long dead friends, marks, and family, the night her only solace. Now remembering her past, she constantly wars between the killer she once was, and the gentle, naive women who had forgotten everything, and now, had become the dark angel she would always be.
