Healing - 0 Soul to use. (##Heal##)
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs. TN 20 / Instant; takes fifteen minutes to cast.
Effect/Description: When this spell is successfully cast, the Lamia will heal at an incredible rate. Upon successfully casting this spell, the Lamia heals 1 Body for each point over 20 they make on the activation roll, unless the wounds were created by fire or salt water. Wounds created by salt water cannot be healed with this spell. Fire wounds heal at the rate of 1 Body per day and only when the character sleeps. If the character is woken up before the day is over, the healing process is disrupted and they do not regain their point (which means they cannot role-play more than the casting of this spell until the damage is healed). If fire wounds aren't healed with this spell they will take twice as long to heal than normal. The spell takes a fifteen minutes to cast and the Lamia must be undisturbed for the entirety of the casting. Any distractions will force the Lamia to start over a gain. The Lamia must role-play the fifteen minute casting of this spell in the main room.

Meditation - 0 Soul to use. (##Meditation##)
System/Duration: NA/NA / Instant; takes 15 minutes to activate.
Effect/Description: In order to center themselves and replenish their mystical energies after spell casting, a Lamia needs to shut themselves off from the world around them and meditate. To accomplish this, they must be sitting alone and relaxing, concentrating on a candle flame, or some sort of talisman - perhaps chanting. The meditation must be role-played for the full 15 minutes in the main room. Background

Aara Dominique Kindel was born on November 1, 1973, in New Orleans. She and her parents, Maria (American) and Tomas (Italian), lived in Monaco, France. Maria and Tomas were wealthy and prominent, and they were involved in business and politics.
Aara grew up very down-to-earth, in a very loving environment. Her parents provided her with everything, as in a story-book life: love, support, a castle, the best schooling and tutors, her own piano...even an arranged marriage that Aara was not aware of. This arranged marriage, to her childhood friend and sweetheart, Christopher, was to form an alliance of political and financial power.
Aara studied to be a musician and artist in Paris. She also traveled around Europe.
Her dream was to open a shop where she could sell her drawings, paintings, and photographs, and to eventually become a concert pianist. She hoped, after her marriage to Christopher, this dream would also come true.
After a weekend modeling trip to Milan with her friends, Aara came home to find her parents brutally murdered in their library and Christopher gone without a trace. It was if he never lived there, never existed. She buried her parents and tried to find Christopher and his family, but they had vanished. Aara's great-grandmother, whom she called Gigi, sat her down and explained to her that she was inheriting everything- the castle, money, everything. She also told Aara that she thought her parents were killed because of their connections and most likely it was Christopher and his family. Gigi, being the eccentric woman that she was, started speaking of vampires and how she thought Christopher was one, and he probably killed Maria and Tomas. Gigi told her to be weary of everybody. All of this struck Aara hard and she chose to leave France. She was going to keep the castle, but she needed to leave for a while. Being a citizen of France, Italy, and the United States, Aara finally decided to travel to the US to see art and find her grandfather, whom still lived in New Orleans. She packed up her portfolios, her laptop, a few pictures, some clothes, and headed off.
She arrived in DC and went to the museums. She had never been to DC and the museums were a way to cope with everything that occurred. With some of her money, she purchased a car, an Eclipse, along with new camera and art equipment, and a cell phone. Aara took pictures wherever she went and while in DC, even did some drawings. After spending some time there, she started driving to LA. Finding her grandfather wasn't going to be easy, but she vowed to. Aara knew that he, being eccentric himself, would be able to help her. She found Voletta on accident...she wasn't paying attention while driving through LA, and drove past New Orleans, right into Voletta. She was intrigued by the darkness and knew that this would be the place for her to stay for a while. She would be able to draw and photograph very interesting things here...maybe she could get a job as an artist or photographer, work as a teacher, model like she had, work as a musician, or even open up the dream shop...the possibilities for her new life are endless. Aara knows she needs to find herself, find her grandfather, and eventually find Christopher, but, this must be done one step at a time.
