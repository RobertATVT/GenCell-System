Background


Elisabeth Kylie Langfield was born in Texas on her parents ranch. Her mother (Eve) and father (Adam) were one of those rare couples who actually loved one another when they married and got along quite well. Elisabeth was an only child and her parents devoted most of their time to her. Needless to say, she developed a sort of "I'm better than everyone else" attitude and was selfish about many things. This carried on into adulthood. Her years on the ranch taught her how to care for the livestock, ride and tame horses as well as other animals, the easy points of bargaining, and to get what you pay for. Her experiences on her family's ranch also left her knowing more about how to take care of herself and less about how the world works. Elisabeth doesn't always look at things through various angles and basically only sees things her way and believes everyone else should too. However, her vision, charisma, magnetism, beauty, and charm are hard to resist and throughout high school she had many boyfriends. Those relationships meant nothing to her and had it not been for her parents caring and devoted relationship, Elisabeth probably wouldn't have believed that true love actually existed. And because she never settled for second best, few guys were good enough for her. She often times avoided romantic attractions. Elisabeth only attended the thirteen mandatory years of school. Her grades were only average. After graduating, Elisabeth stayed on her parents ranch. That's when Brielle came to stay with them. Brielle was 17. She was an only child and her mother had raised her her entire life. Brielle's mother had been best friends with Elisabeth's mother Eve for as long as Elisabeth could remember. But until then, Brielle and Elisabeth had never met. So when Brielle's mother passed away, Eve was happy to take custody of Brielle. Elisabeth and Brielle developed a close friendship almost as soon as she arrived. They trusted each other with everything and never before had Elisabeth felt so close to anyone. Although Brielle's feelings ran deeper than Elisabeth's. Brielle saw Elisabeth in a romantic sense to be her true soul mate. Elisabeth was not at all attracted to Brielle, but enjoyed the time they spent together. Brielle taught Elisabeth so much about sensitivity. Through it all Elisabeth never intended to leave the ranch. She had too much going for her there. But when Elisabeth turned 20, the ranch started making less and less money and her parents thought they'd have to sell it. Elisabeth didn't want to stick around to see that, so she packed her few belongings and hit the road leaving Brielle heartbroken. Brielle vowed never to forgive Elisabeth for running away from the problem and leaving her behind. She lived out of her suitcase for about a year. Many times she'd make stops with strange guys who'd promise her room and board for a while. In return she would have to do what she was told. Elisabeth tried to be good, to give them what they wanted but money was the only matter on her mind and she daydreamt most of the time. She dreamt of horses. She finally made a stop in a little island Lousiana town where she decided to stay.
