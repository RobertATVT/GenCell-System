Background


Tellen Sinclaire was born into a wealthy Baltimore family. His father was a high-ranking offical deep with in the National Security Agency. Because of this agency's secrecy, Tellen was unaware of what his father did for a living until after his father had died. When Tellen was 17, his father was leading an investigation of a bombing of an American nuclear launch site in Turkey. The terrorists who had planned the bombing broke into Tellen's parents house and burned it to the ground. Tellen's mother and father were killed. Tellen lived with his girlfriend's family until he went away to school. He went to Westpoint, and served in the military for 4 years, before someone noticed him, and had him transfered to Intelligence. He worked there for 7 years, before he made the decision to follow in his father's footsteps and had himself transfered into the NSA.
The NSA uses its field agents for missions too dangerous and to risky for both the FBI and the CIA. Tellen worked in the middle of crisis all over the world for 2 years before being transferred to Voletta. Today he packed up his desk and went home for the two day break before his transfer takes effect. His buddies from the agency who have made it out of Voletta alive have been calling him to tell him their horror stories. Tellen, now 33, almost drools over the idea of the chance to see some real intense action.
