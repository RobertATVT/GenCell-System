Luck - 0 Soul to use. (##LuckRe-roll##)
System/Duration: NA / Instant; takes 0 rounds to activate.

The Gift - 0 Soul to use. (no room command needed)
System/Duration: NA / NA
Effect/Description: Everyone has that little something they just seem naturally gifted at. Whether it be juggling, mathematics, or knife throwing, it's there, and it's just waiting to be found. This skill must have an * by its name to denote which skill is the Gift. Should you purchase an Occupational Training type that grants a non-combat skill bonus that you have chosen for your Gift, the bonus levels add to the ones granted by the Gift. Background


The young man's name, Blathazxar. Balthazxar Quintana. At age 0 when he was born and currently age 23 he wouldn't be considered very old by any standards other then a five year olds. An attractive man, though extremly pale. His hair flows down to his shoulders in straight jet black locks, beautiful black eyes piercing in nature. Born and raised in south Florida in a predominantly Cuban family he holds much to his heritage and finds any other race of people of "lower standards and class".
His mother was a high priestess in the art of Wicca and had a child simply for the purpose of raising him to take her place. Balthazxar never went to school, he knows very little about getting along with people outisde his family. His entire life was made of sitting with his mother and learning the art of balck/white magic, communing with the spirits and that razzle. Consequently he got little to no sun, and looks much paler then any live man should.
Now finally, his mothers death... he steps up to the plate. As knowledgable as he can be for one with little experience he has his eyes set on a little town his mother and he visited when he was young. A town by the name of Voletta. He figures he'll start his take over small, a small town with enough problems as it is. Surely he could find a few people to enter his coven there... if he could only figure out how to talk to them...
