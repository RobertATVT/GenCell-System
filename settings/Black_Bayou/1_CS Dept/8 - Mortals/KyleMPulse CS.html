Background: I was born in Berkeley County West Virginia. I led a pretty normal life, a lot of friends, a lot of fun. Everything was great until I turned 18, and my parents told me that it was time for me to get out on my own. I tried a few different jobs, but none of them really paid the bills. I was left with very few options. I decided that I should join the Army. After all, most of my expenses would be paid, and I could get out of my hometown.
I enlisted as soon as I got the chance, and despite my inherent rebellious nature, I did pretty well in my training. I advanced a little in the ranks, and was looking to possibly make the Army my life instead of just a way out of West Virginia. However, two weeks after I transferred out, and moved to my new station in Nova Scotia, I got an emergency telephone call from home. I was being notified that my fiance was dead.
I applied and was granted a stay of service while I returned for the funeral. Upon my homecoming, I was more properly informed on the details of her death. It had been ruled a suicide, but I thought differently. After reading the police reports, and doing a little of my own research, I found something that didn't add up. While I was going through her phone records, I found a call placed from her home several hours after she had reportedly killed herself. Upon dealing with the phone company, I found that the call was made to a one Darren Masterson, of New York, New York.
I went to New York. I went after this Darren Masterson. I spent most of my money for information, trying to locate him. I was now AWOL from the Army, and nobody knew where I was. Finally, I did track down Darren Masterson. My last bit of money loosened his toung just enough to learn that Ariel (my fiance) was murdered, and that the man responsible had fled to a town know as Isle Voletta, LA.
I followed them...

After a short stay in Voletta, and meting with some of the locals, I began to piece together what was happening. It seemed as though my previous outlook on life and death was ill-conceived. I learned that there are things beyond my understanding. After meeting with Jade Fortea, and her husband Daren, I opened my mind to other possibilities. My brother James came to Voletta to deliver a message to me. It was hard news to take, but I believe him. My fiance, Ariel's body had come up missing...
