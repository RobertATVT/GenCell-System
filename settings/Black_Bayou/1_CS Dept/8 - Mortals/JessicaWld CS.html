Background


Background: Born in Jacksonville, FL, Jessica Weilds lived a regular middle-class life. Her parents, Melissa and Joeseph Weilds, were both employees of a small life insurance firm. Times were never rough, nor were they extremely prosperous. She was never a cheerleader, but her school life was still avarage.

She went to college at Georgia Southern University and studied to become a CPA. While there she met John Hill, and as often does she fell in love. They were married when she was only 20 years old. In the beginning, it was a wonderfull relationship, but by the second year, things had started falling apart. John had become progressively abusive, to the point of one night putting her in the hospital. They were divorced a short time later.

It was a nasty proceeding, and left with nothing, Jessica fled to Isle Voletta. There she met a few friends, and learned of a completely different life; the life of the pack. A curious individual, John BlackCloud, promised to one day make her a part of his pack. She was, of course, enthralled by the idea. She started putting the pieces back together with the help of her friends Javin Winter, Rya Winter, and of course mother Syn.

Shortly before the act was supposed to take place, another tragic event occured. Her brother Ricky had died in a drunk driving accident. Crushed, Jess left to heal yet another wound.

Afraid to return to Voletta so soon, Jess went back to college and got her other degree. But something gnawed at her soul. After staying with a rather large firm in Savannah, GA for only three months, she quit and made the long trek back to Voletta.
