Physical Description


Annabelle has light skin from so many spending the cold summers and winters of Germany and Russia indoors. Wearing light makeup and her golden hair being cropped short about to her ears. Annabelle wears semi-casual clothes. Her pants, tied by a drawstring, are dark and loose, swaying whenever she moves. A silk blouse adorns her chest, hiding within it her muscles from years of training in the fighting arts. Her personality is somewhat secluded, she will often put on a bit of a psedo-person should someone get too nosey for her liking. Rarely she will open herself up completely, letting them know of her distraught past. At first glance she would seem as a person that just maybe had a semi-bad day, the facial expression locked off from almost everyone. When she does talk it is in a happy tone, and heavily accented with mainly German but occasionally it will be mixed with German; but you could definitely tell she wasn't born and raised in the United States just by speaking to her.

Name: Annabelle Victoria Karenia
Breed: Mortal
Age and/or birth date: 25/December 23, 1973
Eye Color: Deep Forest Green. The center dotted with golden specks.
Hair: Short and as golden as the sun.
Height/weight: 5'8'/ 125lbs
Occupation: Since her family had plenty of money to take care of her, she simply didn't need a job so she spent her time studying and learning; this made her a scholar of some sort.
Equipment/weapons: On her sixteenth birthday her brother bought her an ancient samurai sword; she keeps this within her house or in her car no matter what. Living in the Russian society was rough this called for some sort of protection for just everyday living...a Barrette 9 mm was just enough.
Appearance (clothes, etc.): Dark clothes are her fashion. She normally fits into loose pants with drawstrings. A loose silk blouse covers her muscular arms and medium chest. A small fleece jacket is in order if it is cold out.
Background: Annabelle grew up in Moscow, Russia only a small part of her life, but her family had close ties into the Russian Mafia and prospered because of it. Annabelle had a very close tie with her father and older brother. Her brother protected her throughout her life. In 1979 when Annabelle was only six her father was found brutally murdered. It was a disaster that would continue to haunt her and her family for years to come. Her family still, even without her father, had money; the money of her mother's family and some from savings. Vladimir (who now was taking care of most family matters), her brother, watched as the communist party was starting to give-way around 1980-81; transferring all money to Swiss accounts for financial safety he then turned to the family. They had to leave so late one night in December 1980 they hurried onto a train and left the USSR to East Germany. East Germany was still in communist hands and Annabelle's family was well known with the communists there and so were well off for now. Annabelle continued to study as much as she could in the restricted atmosphere of East Germany. Wanting to learn more things and go to a university somewhere she slipped away in 1987 after months of careful preparations and planning to West Germany. Not many people made it across the wall alive and she took the chance for a better life. Leaving a note with Vlad she took some money and just left. Her bother, caring for her no matter what, sent her money once a month to live on and save Incas she may need it sometime. Taking what money she had she started to attend a local school, learning about as much as she could. When the wall fell in 1989, Annabelle went to East Germany to find Vlad or her mother. Upon reaching the house that she once lived in she found both her brother and mother dead from poisoning. Upon reading the letter of apology and good-bye she learned that her brother had poisoned himself after poisoning their mother. He said that with the fall of Communism he didn't want to live anymore, he believed that strongly in it. On a separate card two Swiss accounts were given each containing two million dollars. Slipping the card into her pocket she lit a cigarette and dropped it onto the carpet igniting it into flames. Calmly Annabelle walked out looking back to the growing flames in the window. Her face was hardened and showed little if no emotion about the entire incident. She went back and finished the German equivalent of high school and then started to attend the University of Berlin. She took various subjects just wanting to learn whatever she could. Most of her late teens and early twenties were sad ones often going back to where the old house in Eastern Germany once stood. Sometimes when Russia wasn't in too much turmoil she went and visited her father's grave at St. Peter's Cathedral. Annabelle took little money out of the accounts in Switzerland. Around 1997 she moved to New York, working a bit on the runway modeling clothes to keep up the look of a struggling woman in New York. She didn't want people to know the past that was still vivid in her mind. Rarely opening up to anyone she lived a bit of a secluded life from almost everyone. Wanting to see more of the country she went to New Orleans for Mardi Gras in 1999. Hearing a bit about an Isle Voletta not that far from here she bought a jeep to drive there and headed on out...
