Background


Background: Born to Judy and Gary Eldritch. Brad's mother was a surgeon, an outgoing and energetic woman who was loved by all. Brad's father, however, was a different story. Gary Eldritch was an anti-social loner who ran an occult book store in a small town called Isle Voletta. Gary was always either working at the store or writing memoirs, insisting that there was more to the world around than meets the eye. Gary's work was his passion, but his obbsesion to find the "truth" worried his wife. By the time their only son, Brad, was five, Gary and Judy had gone their separate ways. Brad and his mother left for Pennsylvannia, while Brad's father stayed behind to run his bookstore and continue his search for the "truth". 18 years later, Brad recieved a letter stating that Brad's father had passed away, and had left the bookstore to Brad in his will. While Brad's mother urged him to sell the store, Brad was always fascinated by his father's work, and packed his bags and moved to Isle Voletta.
