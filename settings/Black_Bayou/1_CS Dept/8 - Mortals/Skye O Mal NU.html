Background


Skye was born and raised on a ranch about 50 miles outside Texas. She had a loving home with her mother and father, who had bought the ranch just after leaving Ireland ( They were both werewolves and were trying to escape pursicution). She had a happy, content childhood untill the day of her 16th birthday, when a band of outlaws, who were running from the police broke into her home and killed her parents after brutally beating them. Skye was only spared because of the fact that she had escaped out her 2nd floor window and hid in the barn for 3 days, when the outlaws were chased off. She stayed only long enough to finish high school and sold the ranch and began to roam the country, taking with her only some clothes and the four last gifts her parents gave her....Jemm, a black arabian horse, a set of ruby encrusted daggers, a medalion with a wolf on it, and her truck. She went from state to state, finally returning to texas to become a Texas Ranger and look for the evil men that had destroyed her family. She found them, killed the leader and put the rest in prison, but not before being charged with murder for the death of the leader. She was found innocent, but was fired from the Rangers. She began to wander around the country again, and stopped in at a place called Isle Volleta to rest her weary soul for awhile....
