Background


Philip Okobayashi was born in Los Angeles on one crisp November 16 back in 1977. His parents, Japanese immigrants, raised him to believe their life had only truly begun in the US. For eighteen years, they struggled to make ends meet, and out their only child through school. As he grew, he found himself caught between the physical past of Japan, the intellectual future of the modern world, and the stagnation of American culture.
