Background


Duncan Avery is the only child of Mythrandyr and Elizabeth Avery, two semi-renowned circus performers. He was raised on the circus circuit, never in one place for more than a few weeks at a time. Consequently, he is very well travelled, and speaks at least a smattering of several languages. However he never developed a sense of home, either. He has no real attatchments in his life, and since his mother's death, he has been drifting. His father, Mythrandyr, has only spoken to him twice since Elizabeth's death, once to say goodbye, and two years later to say that he was moving to Isle Voletta, LA. It has been close to a year since that last message, and Duncan has finally decided that he should reunite with his father. Although a strange man, Mythrandyr is still his father, and deserves a second chance.
