Background

Raven Gareth, aka DJ Digi was born in a small poor neighborhood, where
him and his mind were the only important things to him. When he was four,
he went to sleep, and woke up a year later in a hospital. His parents, dead...
His home, gone... Too young to know what happened while he was asleep,
he went off to live with his uncle, a famous DJ for a rap group called Young
Triggers. His uncle tought him the moves to be a DJ, and in time, Digi was a
prodigy on the turntables. When he was 11, depression led him to join a
deadly street gang called The Hot Bloods. When his bestfriend was killed in
a gang war, Digi got out. As he grew, he practiced more and more on his DJ
skills, and is now the DJ for the popular hip-hop group The Kryptonicles.
