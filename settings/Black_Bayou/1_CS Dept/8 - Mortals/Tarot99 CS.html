Background

Bailey Grant is from Chicago Illinois and went to college at the University of Southern California after catching the attention of many computer specialists at a trade show in Detroit. One of these specialists was Alison Morrey, the professor of advanced computer technology. They fell in love and got involved. That's when she mentioned her husband and convinced Bailey to help her funnel money from the university so they could run away together. They embezzled a lot of money but eventually her husband found out about their relationship and he tried to kill Bailey. He made a run for it with all of Alison's money, proof that she'd set him up for the fall and all the evidence linking her to the missing college funds. For a year Bailey's been on the run from Alison and her husband, at the same time blackmailing them both to this plot of extortion and deceit. Now Bailey has made his way here to Isle Voletta, Louisiana.
