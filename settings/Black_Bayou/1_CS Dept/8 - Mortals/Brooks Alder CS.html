Background


Brooks (NMI) Alder was born on 7-19-1971 to Peter and Mary Alder in the small town of Valencia, CA. His father was a contracter who mainly worked in Los Angeles, so Brooks didn't get to see much of his father.

His early years were quite uneventful; however, when Brooks was 17, both of his parents were brutally murdered while heading to a party that they were invited to. Deeply depressed, and having nothing else to do, Brooks went to a 2-year Community College to get an Associates degree in Accounting. He had always been good with numbers.

Ironically enough, after graduation, his uncle Stephen Alder phoned him and asked him to come to New York City. He had seen pictures of Brooks, and believed that he was a well built man. He gave Brooks a job on the NYPD as a detective. Brooks had the time of his life for the next few years.. but the stress of a job in a big city really got to him. Deciding to head to greener pastures, Brooks decided to stop by Isle Voletta, LA and see if they could use any help.
