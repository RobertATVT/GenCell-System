Background

The boy never knew his parents; He was raised by an old man whom he called "grandfather" somewhere in Texas. Grandfather taught him "the old ways," lessons in spirituality, archery, and tracking. Grandfather also taught him of his people, and the "outside world," and how to read and write. This went on until age 15, when grandfather died.
on his death bed, grandfater pronounced the boy "wise wolf," and told him that he must go to the city, where he would take a the name of the outsiders, for they would never understand that which he had done.
The boy took the name of Ice, and the second name of his grandfather, Sanchez. When he reached the city, he was forced to pass a citizenship test, and then to work for the $ to put himself through schooling. With the help of his spirit guides, Ice adapted quickly and easily (though never fully) to the outside ways.
At age 18, Ice entered the US Marine Corps for six years, as the spirits dictated. Armed with these new skills, Ice went south, to the Isle Volleta, where the spirits told him he was needed.
Recently, Ice has become a professional bodyguard, and the propriater of a pawn shop, which he uses to collect old weapons and manuscripts of warfare, as well as get some income. he lives in a small apartment over his shop. But the spirits still speak to him, and he knows somethig's out there...
