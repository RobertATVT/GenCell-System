Background

Born Aug 21, 1962 at Voletta General Hospital, the only child of Susan and Roger Mitchell. When Lynn was 5, the family moved to Washington, DC, where Lynn grew up and lived until now. After high school, she was accepted into the premed program at George Washington University, and stayed there throughout medical school. Since then she has done her internship and residency at DC Memorial Hospital, and eventually gained a permanent position there as a trauma surgeon. Lynn loved the fast paced life and felt her best when the stress was highest. She was well on her way to becoming a 'star' in the medial field, and was even on track to be promoted to Director of Emergency Medicine at the hospital - one of the youngest in the nation to ever be considered for such a position. But just before her 37th birthday, she lost a patient, a young boy who should have been relatively easy to save. For some reason the death of this one boy did what the unavoidable deaths of other patients couldn't. She began to lose it - badly. At the suggestion from the head of her department she took a leave of absence to sort things out, and decided to take this time to revisit her dimly remembered birthplace of Voletta.
