Background


Born in Arkansas, JC (Jacob Caleb) Crimson was a big baby. His mother was a stripper, his father was not around at all.

(Note: I'm keeping this really short and sweet). He's never been married. He played High School Football, using his size to become an All-State linebacker. however, instead of attending college, he chose to join the Navy. What seemed like a career prospect quickly soured, and he began to search out greener pastures after 6 years of service, at the age of 24. It was then he took a "temporary" job as a bouncer at the "Hot Kitty" strip club in New Orleans. Th job fit his size, and his tempermant.

The girls started to come to him with problems, and before he knew it, 10 years had passed with him working at various joints. In those ten years, he had become a big brother to most of the working girls, ejecting unruly customers often violently. But his force left much unused. His hobbies grew to include books, working on his classic 1956 Ford Truck, and.....cross-stitching. Yep, Cross-stitching.

With life in the big city becoming more dangerous, he loaded up his worldly possessions and headed into the small town life of Isle Voletta
