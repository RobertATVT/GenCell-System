Background



Background: Derrick Oshman, adopted at the age of four by a loving Caucasian family in the Greater New Orleans area. Derrick was raised with his two sisters, youngest an asian orphan, the eldest the couples natural born daughter. Derrick through early childhood to his later teen years lead a very normal life in and out of schooling. He had been attending LSU Baton Rouge until events deterred him. Life did not get intresting for Derrick until he placed his birth place to a sparse localization of Oshawaw Tribe Indians, somewhere along the plains of Colorado. (Tribe is fictional for freedom of RP.) Gathering what little knowledge he could from the now depleted, near nonexistent tribe, through extensive research and funding from a supportive family, he began combining this new found knowledge of herbs and mystical healing with his education of current medicine. Combining the two-forms of medicine has allowed Derrick an interesting life the last two years. His practices were noticed by family friends who happened to be among social elite. Allowing Derrick new experiences he never dreamed possible, also giving him an affinity if not obsession for the Asian culture in which most of his time has been focused in for new practices to increase his repertoire. Upon his increasing uses for medical treatment a social "friend" he had been treating for an aledged lycanthropic disease mentioned strange happenings in a town somwhere near his hometown of New Orleans. Social circle soon to find new means of treatment, as fads often come and go, he travels back towards his home, to Isle Voletta, hoping that at least the mysterious association with voodoo could be useful with more research if not his reputation.
