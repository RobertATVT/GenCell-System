On the other hand, the user may need stop the use of the mastery to defend themselves. Background

 She was born on a blustery autumn morning on 1972. Her name, however, was quite the controversial issue. Her mother and father never agreed on too much of anything. They did love one another though, very much. Her father, Daryl, wanted to name her Darylyn after himself. Her mother, Kennedy, wanted to name her Alana, after her mother. The solution, compromise! They chose Anna. Anna Alana Darylyn Blaul. Her life was absolutely perfect.. when she was growing up. Her mother dressed her in frilly little dresses and pink buckle shoes. She was put in "Little Princess" beauty pageants and "Search for the Stars" shows where her mother could flaunt her daughter's beauty. The perfect little girl on the outside. Inside, however, she was tormented. It wasn't till she was about 13 years old that her mother finally noticed something was wrong. Anna had an anxiety attack. She spent about 3 months in a mental hospital for teens. A place where they denied her of anything that might assist her in harming herself and she felt like a prisoner. When she was released, she swore up and down she was fine. But her inner torment continued. She took up writing poetry and playing guitar. When she was 16 she ran away. She spent a few nights in her car on the side of the road and was eventually found and returned home. Her mother didn't care that she was feeling pain. She didn't want anyone to know that the president of the PTA's daughter was an unhappy deviant. So, Anna looked for new ways to express her pain. She started having sex with numerous people and was soon known as the school skank. She did drugs and smoked and anything she thought would help shield her from the pain. As Anna's reputation fell so did her mother's. Her home life suffered because of it. Kennedy started fights with Daryl and Anna every chance she got. When Anna was 17, her parents divorced and Anna moved in with her father. She spent a few years at home where she could reflect on her past problems and develop herself into a better person. She took up kick boxing as a hobby and then joined a band. For a while, she played in clubs, making her living that way. But, she tired of that easily and decided to move out of her fathers house to make herself a life. At 24, she moved to South Carolina and met a man named Joe. He was a lawyer and made good money. He thought her musical talent was great and promised her the world. They married and lived happily till she found him sleeping around with his secretary. She divorced him and swore never to get involved with a lawyer again. Anna packed her things up and moved down to Lousiana. She spent a year in Baton Rouge then moved to Isle Voletta.
