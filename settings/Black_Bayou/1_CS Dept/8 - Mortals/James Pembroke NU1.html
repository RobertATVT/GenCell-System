Physical Description


 Justin stands six-foot one-inch. His hair is a light brown and is cut simple and short. His eyes are hazel in color. Justin is twenty-nine years old and weighs around one hundred and ninety pounds. Favoring jeans and a faded tee as casual wear he also likes to wear a shoulder holster with a Beretta 92F. In the colder months of the year a black leather trench style coat will cover the sidearms from view, in the warmer months he favors a lightweight vinyl jacket such as those made by Adidas and Nike.

Background

Justin James Pembroke, a twenty-nine year old, was born in the outskirts of Cleveland, Ohio. His parents, Andrew Ryan Pembroke and Samantha Crystal Clear-Pembroke, were wonderful role models to him his whole life. His father a Sheriff's Deputy for the Portage County SD in Ohio, his mother a Prosecutor for the state of Ohio. His only other sibling is a younger sister, Angeline Sarah Pembroke, she's five years younger then him.
He was your typical boy growing up, big into sports, and the physical violence of young boys divulge themselves into. All through out Junior and Senior High Justin was on the Hockey and Wrestling teams. Justin was a well rounded student, not only was he involved in sports but he was an honor student, graduating tenth in a class of fifteen hundred.
After high school Justin headed off to Penn State for college, there he earned a masters in Criminal Justice with a Bachelors minor in psychology. While at school he started going by James due to his freshman year room mate's name being Justin as well. From then on he stuck with James. Very few of his friends these days call him Justin, only those from back in high school. Retuning home after college he found himself an apartment a couple miles away from home and was hired by the Cuyahoga County Sheriff's Department in Ohio. His father at this was elected to Sheriff of the Portage County SD. Justin went through many job changes during his years with the department. Such jobs included standard patrol work, undercover work, and a bit of investigation. The Sheriff, Matthew Devens, of the Cuyahoga SD was a good friend of Justin's, an old class mate back in high school.
One night when Matthew and Justin were off duty they had just left an Indians game and were headed to the parking lot where the car was parked. A fight broke out only yards away from them, shots were fired and they pulled their own sidearms, badges clipped to their shirts, the raised the guns and started ordering out commands to the juveniles as other Deputies and PD Officers arrived on the scene. Bullets came from behind the two, firing into the crowd of juveniles when they did not lower their weapons. However, two shots were fired into the back of the Sheriff and one into the leg of the Deputy. Sheriff Devens was rendered paralyzed from a bullet to the spine, his lower body no longer mobile. Deputy Pembroke was released from the hospital in hours with minor surgery to his leg. After that night Justin turned in his badge and resigned from the force. After going through a year of psychological treatment Justin is out on his own once more, this time he's headed away from Ohio and down to Louisiana and a small isle known as Isle Voletta. Living off savings and funds from family he's looking for a job, and a nice place to stay.
