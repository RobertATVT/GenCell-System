Background


 A tall man who was once very friendly, however the world has scorned him into a killer, angry at the world and everything around. He learned of a race of such things as werewolves and came to Voletta. There seemed to be many a vermin there. He grew up in Seattle and led a normal life. He went to collage at the Academy and has stayed in the military since. He was transferred base to base until they decided to send him out of town for "Peace Keeping." It was here he learned of Werewolves as one had ravaged his base. Anthony was one of the few survivors. The only one left with his sanity. The FBI flew in the next day and talked to each of them. Any who seemed to have any recollection of what *really* happened was shot. Anthony saw this and told them what he needed to stay alive. Which was absolutely nothing? He left for home the next day and stayed in a Psychiatric ward in the Los Angeles County Hospital, the FBI insisted he stayed there until further testing was done. So once he was released into the world again he asked to be relocated closer to Louisiana. He knew that Werewolves existed there, he had gotten the info from one of the feds. He was transferred quickly and here he is now.
