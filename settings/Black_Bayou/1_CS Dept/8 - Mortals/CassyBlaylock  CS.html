Physical Description



Standing at 5'7" and weighing 120 she moves in timid yet daring fashion. She is often found in slightly baggy pants and a lose fitting t-shirt. Almost always slung over her shoulder is a backpack in which she carries her journal and pen so that she misses nothing always ready to jot it down. Her hair is cut short framing her face nicely with her light perscription glasses. Though she may give the appearance of being shy at first a closer look would tell people that she is merely taking in atmosphere and the people around her into he thoughts dwelling upon them.


Background


Born in Birminham AL. in the later part of the year 1977, Cassandra Blaylock was born into a good family. Not to rich and not to poor. Just right in the middle. She was an only child, infact the only child her mother would ever be able to bear.

She grew up steadily with her loving family making average grades in her class and having a few friends her and there. Slowly though it was realized that Cassandra was never able to be found to close to reality. She could often be found floating around with her own thoughts day dreaming about the would around her.

Slowly as she made her way through High School, Cassandra's parents began to wonder if what they had thought might be just a show of creativity would actually be one of their worst fears. They feared that Cassandra was slowly becoming incapable with interaction with others as she preffered to be alone thinking to herself than with others of her age. As she seperated herself from the world around her they consulted a psycologist.

By her senior year with no signs of improvement Cassandra was going to be sent away to a mental institution to help her become associated with the life around her once again. Cassandra found this out and ran away quickly leaving her parents heart broken.

Cassandra began to travel with what little money she had making the best of what she thought was a happy life. In the process she eventually once again began to make friends her and there in her travels.

This wasn't however her only asperation. She still wanted to learn what she could. She still wanted to look at life at every possible angle. She still continued to write in her journals. For her the journals weren't just ideas and oppinions. They were a story waiting to be told. She will show no one her stories until she feels they are complete.

This is why young Cassandra Lea Blaylock has come to Volleta. Maybe it's just another chapter in her story...Or maybe it will be a whole other story in the making.
