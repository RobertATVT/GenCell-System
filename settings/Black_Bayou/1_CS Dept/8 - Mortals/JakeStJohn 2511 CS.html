Background


 Jake St.John was born on August 30th 1975 to Helen and Peter St.John in Ann Arbor Michigan. He lived the good life there only to find out later in life why. His father, Peter, was a good cop gone bad. Using his job he made various connections to the drug trade and other various crimes. Instead of shying away from this fact when he learned of it, Jake embraced it with open arms. He used the money and power all through High School and then on to college. He always had the best money could buy. His 6 foot 8 frame always had the best clothes, and he always drove the best cars. When he was 20 it was decided that he would travel down to the South to expand his father's business under the cover that he was a P.I. It was agreed this job would cover his need for his 9mm and would allow him to become close to the police establishing a working realationship so to speak.
Living in the South he found the weather to be quite nice so you can usually find him wearing a white t-shirt and khaki cargo pants.
