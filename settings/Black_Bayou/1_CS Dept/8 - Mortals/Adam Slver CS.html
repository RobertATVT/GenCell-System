Background

Background: Born in and raised in Indiana, Adam always felt removed from his peers, isolated. He never seemed to have anything in common with those around him, and while his personality was naturally outgoing, these odd feelings led him to spend most of his childhood in isolation. Quiet and reserved, he slipped through life with a quiet sullenness. Things may have gone rather downhill for Adam, and had indeed begun to by the time he reached his teenage years.
Salvation can come in many forms. For Adam, it came in music.
Introduced to drums by a friend, they quickly became his obsession. He practiced obsessively, day and night, hour upon hour, honing his skills with a fervor he had found in no other part of his life. He finally found a peerage he sought in high school music activities, from marching band to a rock band of his own, and his emotions finally had a throughput. He also took up martial arts, specifically Kendo, the japanese art of competitive swordplay. It seemed whatever he plyed his hand to, he gave the same boundless energy and fervor, and always managed to be successfull at it.
After graduating from high school, he poured himself into music more then ever before, learning the guitar, and piano, and practicing them as ferverently as the drums, every day enriching and honing his talents further. At 23, he has hit the road, seeking out a place where he can look for that illusive "big break", forming a band that can take him to the top. Deciding to meet some old friends and fellow musicians, he heads to where, amazingly, five of those he knew in high school have moved to. Amazing, because it's just a small, out-of-the way town, deep in the Louisana bayou. A town called Isle Voletta.
