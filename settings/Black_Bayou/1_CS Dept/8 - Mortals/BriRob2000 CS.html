Background


Remington Stephen Hawethorne was born on April 21st 1975 to Daniel and Gabriella Hawethorne. He was raised in Voletta in a moderately nice home and had a decent childhood. Daniel Thorne was an author and enjoyed writing scary novels on the supernatural in wich he though was only fiction. Remington took after him and wrote himself a few stories though they were small and messy as he was only 6 yrs. old. He had little friends as he spent most of his life to himself. Writing and drawing pictures of scary monsters as it became his passion. In his early teens his writing got better..and he began to write bigger books and draw more artistic pictures. Though he still kept himself out of friendships. Then one night was walking and witnessed the murder of his wife. The criminal could not be found but Rem felt as if he knew him. This inspired Rem into police academy and after graduating it became his dream of work. He wanted to help people and bring justice to those who do crimes. He waits for the day to find the man who killed his mother. At the age of 24 his father died of natural causes and Rem then took over his inheritance. Taking in a good sum of money, the house, and the riviera. He will live on the inheritance until he gets his job.
