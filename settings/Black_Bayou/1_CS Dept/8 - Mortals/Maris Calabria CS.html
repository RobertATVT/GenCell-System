Background


Maris Calabria was born early morning, on February the 16th. The firstborn, and only child of Anthony and Isabelle Calabria had all money could give her.

She called Atlanta, GA her home. The most expensive of toys were offered to her, then as she reached her teen years, electronics and clothes replaced the toys.

Her schooling was the most expensive. Private schools, etiquette classes. All these managed to give her an outer sheen, but never penetrated the person inside.

Maris was the one behind all the trouble. When everyone got straight A's, one semester, Maris was the brains behind it. Although, two other girls were found to be guilty - and when their accusing fingers pointed to Maris, the teachers just laughed.

She's always had a knack for computers. With a few pointers picked up from various places at University - Notre Dame, of course - Maris' knowledge grew even more.

She graduated with a degree in Computer Science. However, Maris knew long before that she wasn't interested in working at the.. typical desk job.

Petty robberies began to be reported in Atlanta, ever summer that Maris came home. But these weren't petty at all. The thief wasn't targeting the norm, electronics and expensive gadgets. One item at a time was being taken. Small valuables - something each family cherished.

This could be a photo album from years and years ago. Or the Monet bought from an exclusive sale in New York.

But Atlanta has lost some of its appeal, and so Maris has moved on, and has no found herself stuck (briefly she hopes) in a little town, with mysteries and quirks of its own.
