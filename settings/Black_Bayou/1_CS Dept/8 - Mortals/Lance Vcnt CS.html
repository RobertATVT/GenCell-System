Background

Being born into a family of a computer programmer and a stunt coordinator, Lance has always been one to use his mind. His father taught him the basics to programming with BASIC, VISUAL BASIC, C++, c+++, PROTOCAL, and several other comper programming languages. Though taking interest, he spent most of his time learning how to use makeup, mixing boards, and other devices used for special affects.
Around the age 15, Lance got into hacking, from changing passwords on his friends computers to looking up adresses of people who were unlisted in the phonebook. This continued, and as he learned to program, he got better at manuevering around watchdogs and fire walls.
Attending college at Yake for a Masters at age 20, Lance excelled at using special affects equipment and was getting better at programming, and hacking. His parents wanted him to come back to Los Angeles to work for a studio of some sort, but he refused, and found out about Fairlight, a company based in Isle Voletta, Louisianna.
This led him to research more about the city, and found out some very interesting things. There was several growing companies, such as Omsferd Investigations, Myacron Managing Inc, Fairlight, and even a porn video chain, a even better reason to move their. Using money from doing hacking jobs and crashing certaint internet sites for "friends", he bought a small house on the beach. (Security and description in Vincent house SL)
