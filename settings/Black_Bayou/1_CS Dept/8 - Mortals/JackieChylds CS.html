Background


 Jackie was born to a wealthy family in Beverly Hills, CA. He wanted to be a lawyer from the time he was ten, and he followed his dreams, going through law school and graduating near the head of his class. He worked in Los Angeles when he finished school, and was a fairly successful lawyer. Life was going pretty darned good for Jackie. But when he went got a better job in New York, things got a bit hairy.
He was doing very well in New York, and was even up for a partnership with the firm. But he stumbled upon a huge embezzlement scam, which involved the theft of over 100 million dollars. When went to the police with the evidence he had collected, he was asked to testify in court. He did, and it led to the conviction of several members of the firm. After having his life threatened in the court room, Jackie decided to change his name and move to a small town in Louisiana he had heard of from a past client. That town, of course, was Isle Voletta.
