Physical Description

Standing at about 6 foot even, this man is imposing. Dark hair surrounds the pale face, framing the icy eyes that can pierce souls. Muscular, but not large, he would appear to be very quick and lithe. Has a noticeable look of disdain or indifference on his face.


Background
 Paul Beuregarde was born in New York City on August 21, 1974. He was the child to proud parents Jon and Lillian Beuregarde. He had a fairly normal childhood, even through his father's occupation. Paul did not know it then, but his father was the head of a large corporation in Japan, whose main game was the elimination and erasure of important political figures. This is why the trouble started.
On his sixteenth birthday, Paul came home from basketball practice. It was dark, and as he entered, he saw black shapes moving from the house. He turned on the kitchen light, and saw the sight that would scar him forever. His parents were both lying on the floor, nude save for ropes tying their wrists. They had been beheaded by some unknown party, and Paul vowed to find out who it was.
Paul joined his father's corporation as an assassin. He was taught the arts of ninjitsu. For this reason he still wears a shadowy trenchcoat, and carries a katana and a Beretta. He worked for his father's superiors for nine years, before he finally found the strength to leave the dread occupation for good.
This is not his first visit to the Bayou... He has lived there before. It was there that he found out that a government operation to remove assassins had been the cause of his parents' deaths. After various unpleasantness in the dark city, he left. He vowed never to return. Something strong calls him back... He has found the truth. No one ever leaves the Bayou for good ... at least with their souls still intact.
