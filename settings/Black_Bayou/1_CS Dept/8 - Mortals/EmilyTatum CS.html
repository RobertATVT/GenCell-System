Background

Emily Anne, was born 26 years ago in Tulsa OK. Her mother Diane, was a single mom working as a waitress in a local bar to make ends meet. Naturally, Emily has been diggin the bar scene since she was a little girl. She would stay up late, and do her homework at the bar. But her main interest was the local band that played every saturday night. They called themselves "Tennesee Whiskey". Each and every saturday night, Emily would scoot right up front and watch them. The front man, Jimmy, later taught her how to wail on the guitar. By the time she was 15 or so. Emily was good enough to play along with them, and even head her own little shows for the bar, when the Band went on the road.
At the age of 17, Em graduated High School and moved straight on to college. She worked her way through school, waiting tables at the bar. And sometimes playing a little bit on a saturday night. She majored in music, and Journalism.
About a year ago, Em's grandmother died of a massive heart attack. She was left a very hefty amount of money. Totaling to about 1.5 million. Her grandmother had been saving money, and investing it for over 70 years. Though the money was a blessing, Em was heartbroken to loose her last remaining grandparent.But she took this chance to pick up and leave. Finally breaking away from the everyday life of Tulsa OK. To move to Voletta ,La.
