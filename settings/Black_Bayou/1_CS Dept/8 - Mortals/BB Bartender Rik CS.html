Background

Background: Rik Ohmsand was born in Hartford Connecticut at 2:04 A.M. on a cold New Year's Day. Son of Shawn and Marie Ohmsand, he became the first and only child the couple had. Once Rik turned 7, his mother had an affair so him and his father moved to New Orleans, Louisiana. He went through Junior High with average grades, due mainly to the fact that he didn't know what he wanted to do in life. Then, once he reached High School he met a girl named Jessica that changed his life forever. One day he met her parents. Jessica's father mentioned he was a bartender. Rik ponderded that career for a while, and quickly decided that is what he wanted to do. During school Rik was getting A's and B's, was the starting QB of the football team, and was holding a part-time job with Jessica's father. During the summer of graduationRik took time to practice the bartending tricks (ie: cathcing bottles behind the back and mixing drinks to get an odd result). He soon discovered after watching a movie that he needs a bigger build. He soon began lifting weights and as a result he had gotten much larger tips from the ladies, allowing him to buy a new Harley-Davidson. Two years later he got married to Jessica and now is moving to Isle Volletta in hopes of starting his own bar and retiring early.
