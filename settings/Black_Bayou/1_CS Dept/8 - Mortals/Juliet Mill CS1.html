Juliet Mill (formerly Daria Juliet Mill, but I'll get to that) was born on July 22nd in 1974. Growing up she was the eldest of three children, a younger sis named Josephine and little brother. When she was 17 her parents were killed by a burglar when they tried to protect the family. Soon afterwards they (the kids) were all placed into a foster home while social services processed them. Being 17 she wasn't young enough to be of interest to anyone and was separated from her sister and brother. Placed in an orphanage, she was abused and called a '"trouble maker" and did every chore they could find. Luckily, 6 months later, she became eighteen. Getting out of the orphanage she thought about petitioning for custody for her siblings, but couldn't tear them away from there new family. She disappeared from her "family" and went to work as a waitress in Baton Rouge...where she was "discovered." Before her career took off, she went to find her sister and brother. Arriving in Isle Voletta she searched for a while before she found her sis, then learned that her brother had died from cancer. After that time flew by and she had to leave her sis for Modeling. At the urging of her agent she dropped her first name "Daria" and now goes by Juliet Mill. She soon afterwards got a part in a local play, and started acting. She leads a successful career but misses her sis, and is moving back to Voletta
