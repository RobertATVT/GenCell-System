Background


Helena has lived a fairly "normal" life - born in Glenmora, Louisiana on August 7, 1977 to a moderately well-off family, she did very well in her schooling. She graduated High School with a 3.91 GPA, and after a grand vacation (visiting sights in California, New York, England, Scotland, and Australia) went to a local college (of her own choice - her parents, while willing to help Helena out with non-school-related things, wanted her to put herself through college). She is just one semester from graduation, and holds a GPA of 3.89 with a double major in Dance and Art, finding herself on the Dean's List each semester (though she is anything but a bookworm, as some would imply). She is the vice president of a sorority, having been a sister since her first year of college. The sorority prides itself on being fairly elite, its requirements being rather high GPAs, etc. Helena has worked very hard to give herself the things she wants; people wonder how she has time, what with so much college work. Recently, things in her life seem to have gone stale, and she finds herself tired of what's going on at college, her sorority, the surroundings, etc. She's decided to take at least a semester off and just get away from the general area. Her parents have, oddly enough, supported her in this decision. They even struck a bargain with her: so long as she doesn't leave the state, they would pay for her housing, furnishings, etc. She used the old-fashioned method of choosing her "getaway" location (namely, to touch a pencil to a random part of a map of Louisiana), and that resulted in her choice: Isle Voletta. Helena managed to get her parents to agree to allowing her to go - at least that way, she'd be away from the hustle and bustle of the life she'd gotten too used to.
