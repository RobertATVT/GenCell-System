Background
Father Richard Hansen was born into a religious family on November 9, 1975. But the young brown haired boy was not born at a hospital, but in the basement of his house, and doused in holy water the moment he was brought into the world. Over the next few years his father literally beat the Lord's ways into him. The Bible became his best friend and his father's belt would always be known as punishment for him. During his elementary school years he learned of the opposite sex, and was caught by his father with a girl in his backyard. His father then beat him while chanting the Lord's prayer. After that, Richard was sent to a boy's only religious school and taught all about sin. At the age of 20 Richard became the priest of a local parish. One day, he overheard one of the parishioners speaking of a place of pure evil and sinners everywhere. Richard's ears picked up as he heard the name "Voletta". The next day he packed his bags and took the next plane to Voletta to stop the evil he heard of. He now has a mission, and nothing will stop him from achieving it.
