Background
Charlotte Spindler was born on a brisk January morning. Her childhood was normal up till about highschool. Her mom had developed breast cancer and her father had stopped coming home at night. He'd stroll in at all hours, lie to Charlie and her older sister Josephine, or Joey about where he'd been, then walk out again leaving them to tend to their sickly and near death mother. Well, of course, several months later Charlie's mother died. And, of course, they could not turn to their father for only weeks earlier he had been arrested for drug charges. Since she was only 15 at the time, Joey...who was 18 took charge. They worked hard to keep things running smoothly. Charlie finished highschool in her small eastcoast town with nothing more than dreams of escaping it. So, it was no surprise that at graduation, she did just that. She headed south to Louisiana to go to college and attended U of L for four years. Then, after graduation headed to a little town where her sister had moved about a year ago. It seemed like a nice place to start a new life. Isle Voletta, Lousiana.
