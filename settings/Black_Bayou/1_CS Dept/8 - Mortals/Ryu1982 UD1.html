Background


Ryu Hotoro, twenty-five years old, born on October 13, 1974, on a Friday. He was orphaned at the age of five. Ryu lived in Enzan, Japan since he was five, or at least he could remember. He only remembers something's about his family. He had a mother, a father, and even a little sister. In his dreams he remembers a day deep in the forest where he use to live. It was a bright day, then it turned black. Blood splattered across his face. Turning his head, he could see the silhouettes of men killing his family. Slitting his father's throat, raping and killing his mother. He couldn't find his sister, maybe they took her. He hide behind some trees, crying and wishing the bad men went away. The struggle stopped, and he peered around the tree's trunk. He found his mother and father slain on the green grass. His mother the only one left with any breath in her body, looked at her son and told him, "Find...your sister...Lina..." With that her head fell back, her eyes wide open. Ryu clasps her hands and feels something inside of it. A locket... Inside the locket was a picture of his family. He held onto the locket for his mother's sake. "Is this all a dream?" Come back into reality! All that he dreamt was real.

Years passed, and now one has reported the missing Hotoro family. No one knows of the wandering Ryu. Ryu managed to get by, do odd jobs and other types of employment. One particularly in a martial arts dojo. Ryu had no real training, and needed an edge to punish those who took his sister. He hated guns, and didn't care much for any other type of weapon. But the human body was the perfect weapon. So, he taught himself various types of martial arts on his search, and has proven to have beaten many black belt masters. He learned English from his mother before she die. Very intellectual person, shy, and always quiet. He has not met an opponent he could not handle.

Twenty-five years of age, this Japanese young man is still searching for his sister in the US Still no sign of her. He can't bear to sleep sometimes, the visions ever so horrid and gothic. Ryu gets a lead on his sister's whereabouts where lead him to Voletta, LO. He wanders into Voletta, Louisiana wondering if he can find a job and his sister. Will he find his sister, if she's still alive...
