She disappeared shortly after learning of him and his family.
The Agency took her in. The Agency was a godsend.They taught her. The Agency was a corporation that took in people and made them something,they looked for the lost and the helpless.Lauyrn M. didn't really understand everything that was going on,but she finally felt loved and had people that understood her,even if she didn't understand them. They said that they needed her, since she was such a little fireball. She was 19 when she finally agreed. She took on the identity of Ken's younger sister. It was only later that she found out that the Agency intended to kill Ken's real sister. Lauryn M. had to stand there and watch as they slowly killed this young and innocent child for no other reason then being related to Ken. The incident only made her tougher.
She finally located Ken in Voletta. Now it is her task to confront him and do as the agency says.
