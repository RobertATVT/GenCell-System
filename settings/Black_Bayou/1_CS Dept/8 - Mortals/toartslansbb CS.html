Background

Timothy Sloans, or Toar Sloans, was born in the early 70's to a well put together family. 3 siblings, all older than him by considerable years also lived in his rather large house. Conneticut was your average state, not having too much to offer, but also nothing to complain about at the same time.
His oldest sister got married at age 26, when he was only 10. His parents were rather old to be raising a son of such a young age. They weren't like other parents, trying to raise him the same way they raised their otehr children over a decade ago. This led to tension between him and his parents, especially in his teen years.
Watching the older siblings leave in their twenties, Timothy decided he'd try and outshine them just a little. But, despite hard work, he had the same grades as the average Sloan, and could do little to impress his aging parents.
At age 18, his father died, leaving his mother a large fortune. Toar watched hsi health... his father died of a Heartattack at what was considered to be a pretty young age to do so. With siblings and family members mourning, Toar watched rather then open up to them. It wasn't him to do so.
He considered going into the medical field at first, but always wanted to be a police officer when he was younger. An opportunity close enough to such a job camme up, an investigator, looking for at least 2 years of college at a county school, which Toar had just received.
He got the job at age 21, and was trained very basic police training. At age 26, he was assigned to cases that it seemed like the FBI might handle. Things he had never heard of before were involved. Demons... Vampires... but he didn't believe one bit of it. 2 months into the murder case, the FBI was ordered to take over, but the case had strange effects on Toar. WHile he convinced himself not to believe in such creatures of the dark, a "fear" led him to quit. He now moves to Voletta to look for a new job.
