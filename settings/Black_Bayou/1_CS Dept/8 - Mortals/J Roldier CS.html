Background


Joashua Roldier. Born and raised in a suburb of NewYork. He lived a normal life as a boy. He went to a local school. He had two brothers Kevin which was 2 years older and Michael which was five years younger. His life was normal and fine until one day.
It was around Christmas time and Joshua's dad, brothers, and himself went out shopping. They were going to go to the local mall. They were passing under a tunnel when a car came flying around a turn running from a police car. The car hit Joshua's forcing it into a pillar in the middle of the tunnel. Joshua's arm got crushed, leaving a scar on his arm. That wasn't the worst of it though. Joshua's father went through the window because the airbags didn't go off. his younger brother was knocked unconsious, and his older brother snapped his neck making him paralyzed.
The ambulance rushed to the scene. They got Joshua out nice and easily. They had to rush the other three to the hospital. The doctor's could not save his father. His father's last word's were take this handing him his great grandfather's necklace and he told him to take care of the family. He had sustained to much injury. His older brother has stayed in a wheel chair ever since. As for his younger brother, he had stayed in a coma for about a week when all of the sudden his heart stopped.
This event had made Joshua a different person. He never really talked to people very much. He had decided to go become a doctor so that he could save other people. During his free time he read a lot getting interested in a supernatural being. Psychics.
He has grown up and is now out of medical school at the age of 26. He was working at a loacal clinic. He quit from there very soon since it wasn't what he was looking for. He had heard about an Island called Isle Voletta. He had heard that there were many patients down there and a busy hospital. He had got the first flight in to apply for a job not knowing what was ahead.....
