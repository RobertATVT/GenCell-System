Background


I stand alone in this vast realm we call life.

There are others around me as there are all of us, but I am unnoticed. Please don't confuse this with unwanted. Unwanted would imply that my presence was known to others. This is not a bad thing nor a problem. Actually I prefer it this way.

Who am I?

One of four children born to my parents. The oldest. The forgotten one. The one delegated to chores as the others played. The responsible one. If everything was fine I was unnoticed and underappreciated, if something went wrong it was my responsibility. Am I bitter for this part of my world? Not at all, it has made be stronger.

I am the quiet girl who sat behind you in grade school. The one that would blush if you even looked in my direction. I am the one who knew all the answers in high school but never raised her hand. I am the one that skewed the grading curve, you know, the one everyone silently hated. I am the one that the professors never knew sat in their lectures. I didn't ask questions, didn't talk much... just took notes, did the assignments and aced the tests. I am the one that graduated college with honors but didn't attend the ceremony.

Not beautiful, not ugly. Average is how I could be described. Hazel eyes and brown hair, oh I'd say around 5'5" or so, average build. Nothing spectacular. You would be hard pressed to point me out in a crowd. I have never married, never really dated for that matter. The unnoticed one, remember?

I have been traveling alone for quite a while now. Not heading anywhere in particular, picking up odd jobs here and there. Just observing different cultures within our own country. It might surprise you how varied we all are, some places are almost like a world all their own. Louisiana is one of those places.

Figured it out yet? I am a loner, born to walk alone. Please don't try to find me, I might just disappear before your eyes... that is... if you can see me in the first place.
