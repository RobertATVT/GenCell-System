Background

Vincent Turk grew up Vincent Vincini in New York's Hell's Kitchen, having the typically rough childhood of all those who grew up there. He grew up a juvenille delinquent, in and out of institutions from the time he was 12. Things changed, however, when his older brother, Anthony, joined a gang, the Red Masquers. Not just a typical gang, but a gang of vampires, known for their love of shoot-ups, fearless because of their quick healing powers and superior strength. New initiates into the gang weren't told it's true nature at first, not until they had proven themselves. When Anthony found out, he tried to leave the gang. But he knew too much. He was found two weeks later.
Sick of the violence and chaos that had brought his brother's end, Vincent grew up to join the NYPD. He immediately hunted down the Masquers, and then, in a raid on their hideout, he discovered their true nature. He was nearly killed in that raid, in fact the only survivor. He began to study vampire lore intensely, garnering all the information he could. Searching through countless police records, poring through every library in New York City, he slowly learned the ways of the enemy. He eventually took down the Red Masquers, and his work called the attention of a confidential branch of the CIA.
That secret branch had been created to study paranormal activities in the US. They hired him, and for the next several years he worked on countless paranormal cases, building information on, and fighting, the enemies the government would not officially acknowledge, but could not ignore. Then, accidentally, he discovered some information that even he was not supposed to see. A conspiracy, a silent influence of the vampires that reached high into the government offices. He was sent on a dummy assignment, and disavowed, all records of his existence erased. He was not, however, without skills to forge a new identity. He created a new fabricated history, and took the name Vincent Turk. He now fights the same enemies he has for years, on the sly. His methodical, slow but sure, stealthy and surreptitious. He moves to an area, builds a rapport, lies in wait, and assassinates the enemy one by one. He works various jobs in different cities, sometimes as a policeman or a private investigator. He has built contacts all over the nation, and through them he has just uncovered the location of an intense concentration of the predatoric enemies. It is called Isle Voletta, and he heads there now...
