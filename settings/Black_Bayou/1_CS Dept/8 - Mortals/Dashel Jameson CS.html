Background



Your Approved BackGround: ( Save this Email! )

Dashel Jameson was born in the lush green hills around the Napa valley. His family ran a modest bed and breakfast just outside Novato, just a few miles from Calistoga. After high school, Dash went away to school, majoring in Hotel and Restaurant Management at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. Along with his major, he completed a second bachelor's degree in medeval literature and mythology. Upon earning his masters degree, Dashel returned to Northern California, and openned an Inn overlooking the ocean in Carmel. Within just a few short years, he openned three more, all in Northern California. With profits at an all time high, he has begun an agressive expansion plan, working his way along the SouthWest, and into the Gulf states. He has come to Voletta to select a promising site for his second inn in Louisianna, the Baton Rouge location having been so successful. Dash is looking for a prime piece of real estate on or near the beach for the Inn and the restaurant; as well as a master chef, maitre'd, clerks for the reservation/check-in desk, cooks and servers, and musicians for live entertainment. He will also need bartenders and security, and housekeeping staff for the inn.
