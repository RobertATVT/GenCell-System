Background





Blow softly sweet wind
And take my tears away
ease from my weary mind
And I might find peace again

Born in Columbus Ohio, Leila has always been interested in two things, the supernatural and writing. It is only given that the two things she is most fascinated with might become meshed together to create a form of employment. While not quite Maya Angelou in stature yet, Leila is known as an up and coming writer in the science fiction world. Yet, even her science fiction does not surpass the poetry that haunts her soul to almost obsessive levels. Yet it was not always this way.

Leila was like any little girl of the time, she made plenty of friends with the girls and had even more boy admireres with her chocolate locks painted with hints of strawberry and eyes the color of thunderclouds. As she grew, she tended to dress a little more urban-hippish then her friends, and yet could pass through any of the social crowds because of her fresh looks and friendly personality. In fact her mother lovingly said that -her- daughter could talk the devil out of his pitchfork. It was at the age of fourteen Leila's attitude took a drastic turn.

Find me dear breeze
And carress my burning flesh
That the tortured screams of agony
Might end with your gentleness

Puppy love should be many things, but harnful isn't one of them. Unfortunately for Leila, hers was the most harmful, for she fell for a Vampire stuck in the body of a 17 year old. Bitter at the world, he had made his career in destroying all that was beautiful in life, and could not resist trying to do so with Leila. Through seduction and enchantment, he lulled her into his web before taking away bit by bit everything she held dear. Finally, she was left with only the memories of a family she had loved, now dead, and a lover who had created her into his image, mortal, yet not. She was a slave to his desires as he tried to drain her of all the goodness she had.

But still my gentle lover,
Bring your arms around me.
Hold me tight in your embrace
So, though my soul might be fleeting
When death does come.

Yet Leila was a soul who could find the goodness in any situation, and found it most deftly in a hidden talent brought to the surface. To escape the harsh reality of her situation, a situation she didn't truely understand, she began to write. One journal entry become another, and then another, and when her lover died by the hands of his own chyldren and she found herself free and alone, she turned those journals into a best-selling piece of Fiction named My Story. By the age of 17 she was a best seller. Yearning for knowledge of the reason her parents were dead though, still not quite able to grasp why this creature killed her family, she began researching Vampires, finding a sickening fatuation with learning about the terrors that hurt her for so long.

Find me dear breeze,
Carress my dying flesh.
That tortured screams of Agony
Might end in gentleness

Leads and interviews finally brought the twenty year old Leila Dickenson to Isle Voletta, where a rash of deaths and unsolved murders held firm believement with her insiders that perhaps what she was looking for could be found in that far off distance of a land. And so, with her Editor's Approval and the Publishing Company's heady conmtribution she moved to Voletta to find out the Reality behind the Myth of the Vampires of Isle Voletta.
