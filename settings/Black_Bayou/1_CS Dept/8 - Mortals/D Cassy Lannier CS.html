Background

Darma Cassidy was born to a small family in Jay, Maine. She has long blonde hair that curls slightly in the humidity and flashing green eyes. This seemly frail woman is quick to move and graceful due to her martial arts training, which she had started when she was 5 at her fathers request. Living in an all Catholic home with an ex-marine for a father was rough enough but knowing that you were different was even worse for her. She ran away from home when she was 13, swearing that she would never hear another relative make fun of her religious beliefs again. With pentagram in hand and a backpack slung over her Jean jacketed shoulders she headed out for the great American Road. Her first night out she learned why her father had demanded that she have lessons, with a bloodied eye and a limp she left her would be attackers in the snow. She holed up in a cave system and lived off the lands for the winter. Only once did she think of returning home that first winter. The night that a she wolf moved into her cave. There were a few tense days and after that the two went about their lives. She travels in the any weather and only holes up when the worst of it keeps her from seeing her feet before her. She is most often seen with a leather pack over her shoulders and a faded old denim jacket. She loves her long "hippy" skirts and will stay on extra long in a particulair town if she can get several made in a pattern that she has taken a fashion to over the years. She favors black tee shirts and "granny boots" that lace midway up her calf. She wears her hair loose and sometimes uses the scarf that she always has around one of her wrists to tie it back. She carries a cheap tarot deck that she is familiar with and a small carving knife. It is with these that she makes that small amount of money that she needs to carry on in her little style. Reading tarots to the passer-byes and carving small locket charms out of all types of wood. She answer to either Darma, Dar or Cassy but she rarely gives out her full name.
