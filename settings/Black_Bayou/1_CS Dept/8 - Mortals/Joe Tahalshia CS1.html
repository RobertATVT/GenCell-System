Background

Joe was the oddball of the Tahalshia family. While the rest of the family learned the arts of magic...he...didn't. When the other children were deep in their studies, lil' Joe was betting lunch money over whether a die roll would be odd or even. While loved by all his siblings, he was still, in many repsects, the family's black sheep. Eventually, the siblings split up. Joe spent his time wandering to the best gambling cities he could find. Throughout his travels he made a great deal of money, thanks to his "natural ability to gamble" (meaning not always playing honest).
Joe doesn't believe in having "significant others", due to his belief that it wouldn't go well with him paired with his fear of being hurt. Joe has actually never had a girlfriend in his life. His only love was, and still is, gambling.
While in New Orleans, Joe had heard Nitram and Blackthorne had settled on a small island by the name of Voletta. Deciding maybe he should settle down, Joe moved there too.
