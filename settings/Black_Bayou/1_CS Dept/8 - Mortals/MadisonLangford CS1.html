Physical Description


Her 5'9" form cuts through the air around her as it crackles with the silent electricity of her presence. Background
Madison Juliet Langford, born May 14th, 1979 in Boston, Mass., graduated from Harvard with an English degree. Her first publication, Wandering Thoughts, is due out in paperback this June. She has come to Isle Voletta by way of New Orleans, hoping to do some research into the town's myths and superstitions and in turn be inspired for her next work.
