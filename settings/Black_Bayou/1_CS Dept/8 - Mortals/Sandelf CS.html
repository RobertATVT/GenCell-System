Physical Description
Jakob is 6'1" he is commonly dressed in baggy jeans which hang to his hips, a loose belt is tied to them. He commonly wears brown leather hiking boots. He usually wears a large tan jacket which hangs to his knees. His hair is a dark brown which is usually spiked up. There are two piercings in his ear, one at the top of the ear and one at the bottom. He is rather skinny, slightly muscular but not as to be muscle-bound. He has brown eyes a slight imperfection at the top of one of them. Due to his experience with "Jake-Hate" as he calls it, he carries spiked leather gloves in his pocket. In extreme measure he would have to use the pistol strapped to the inside of the back of his large coat. There is a scar on his right fore-arm from where he was a victim of "Jake-Hate".


Background
Jakob was born on 3/8/78 in Springfield Mass. Jakob is one of those rare kids with a wonderful voice. he also decided to pursue physical activities such as martial arts and sports as a child. he was highly likeable by most due to his charm. Unfortunately this was his downfall. He was quite the player in his teenage years which in turn manifested into many gaining a hatred to him due to the fact that he used them. Jakob then became quite the womanizing, love-a-holic. He left his home town upon the age of 20 after having a steady job as a singer in nightclubs. Many enjoyed his voice but unfortunately you can't live on that much income... so he traveled further.. until he found the most interesting place he had ever known.... Isle Voletta.
