The Gift - 0 Soul to use. (no room command needed)
System/Duration: NA / NA
Effect/Description: Everyone has that little something they just seem naturally gifted at. Whether it be juggling, mathematics, or knife throwing, it's there, and it's just waiting to be found. Should you purchase an Occupational Training type that grants a non-combat skill bonus that you have chosen for your Gift, the bonus levels add to the ones granted by the Gift. You may only use a skill from the non-combat skill list as your Gift if an occupation gives you additional levels in it during character generation. If you ever change breeds, you do not lose the levels of the gift unless they interfere with another rule. For example, if you have Chi it is automatically replaced with the fighting style of the new breed at half its current level. The levels are still halved as normal, even if it was the Gift.

Background


Born and raised in Isle Voletta to William and Vanessa Parker in October of 1970, Scott Parker is a man of few words, unless it's something that is slandering someone else. He is a natural at what he does.

Elementary school and Junior High were a bore for him, but High School...now that was full of good times. He dated Chanson du Geste for those four years..they went to the Senior Prom together, where she spiked the punch and did some other things that he can't remember. After the prom the did the usual tradition of renting a hotel room and having sex.

After graduation, Chanson went her own way, while Scott went to work for the National Enquirer. He met Jake Akison there, and became good friends with him. After a few years, Jake went on to do other things at the Enquirer, while Scott continued in the Western United States.

Almost 30 years after leaving Isle Voletta, Scott returns hoping to find something close to him, and to get a position at the Squealer.
