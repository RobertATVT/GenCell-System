Background

Victor grew up just South of the border in a small Mexican farming town. His parents required that he work on their farm and so he had little time for an education. He grew up knowing only how to speak and write Spanish and do basic arithmetic. Eventually Victor became tired of this life and set off for the states. At the age of twenty he was granted citizenship and he moved into an apartment in L.A. He went to night school to learn how to speak and write English and it was there where he discovered his love for acting. The school he attended offered drama classes,which he occasionally sat in on. One day the teacher invited Victor to participate in the class and he did. He realized his talent and at once began to try out for every part in every play he could find. Victor moved on to star in many local productions and T.V. shows before eventually being handed an audition for a major motion picture. He got the part and became a very well known actor in the movie and stage buissness. Victor most recently bought a house on Isle Voletta and plans on living there almost year round.
