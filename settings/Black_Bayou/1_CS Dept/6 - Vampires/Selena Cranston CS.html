Special Abilities



Bat Form - 0 Soul to use. (##Bat Form##)
System/Duration: NA / Unlimited; takes 1 round to shapeshift.
Effect/Description: A vampire that is attuned to its own inner nature is able to assume its sister form at will. They appear to be rather small and difficult to notice, their bat forms often taking an appearance characteristic of their personality. They still retain their normal eye color, however. While in bat form, they have +2 Quickness and -4 Body (a minimum of 1 remaining). A vampire in bat form effectively cannot see. It uses active sonar to move around. Attackers suffer -4 to their roll when they try to attack the small vampire bat. A vampire can feed in bat form, but cannot use any other masteries while in this form.

(Just a quick note about shape changing (all forms). While a vampire is in another form, if its current Body falls to 0, the vampire will immediately change back to mortal form minus the damage it has already taken, leaving a minimum of 1 Body. It should also be noted that materiel objects do not shapeshift with the character. Any and all clothes and objects the character was holding will need to be transported by some other means.)

Healing - 0 Soul to use (no room command needed)
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: Vampires have an incredible natural healing ability. Any wound caused to the vampire will heal at the rate of 1 Body per hour the character rests. If the character takes any strenuous action, the healing is disrupted and the hour starts over. A Room Host must be notified when you claim your Body point back. Wounds created by faith water cannot be healed by this mastery. Wounds from sunlight or fire heal at the rate of 1 Body per day and only when the character sleeps. If the character is woken up before the day is over, the healing process is disrupted and they do not regain their point (which means they cannot role-play until the damage is healed). The damage from an attack that removes a limb heals at the normal rate, but the player must role-play the limb slowly growing back. (Length of time for the limb to regenerate is determined by the Forum Director.)

Background


Selena Cranston was born into an upper-middle class family in a suburb of Chicago in September of 1971. She had three older brothers and her family was the stereotypical suburban family, absolutely nothing to hide. Except for the fact that they were Satan worshippers. When Selena reached puberty, her family tied to her to an altar and let her bleed while chanting in tongues. This continued, along with physical threats so that she wouldn't tell anyone, until she went to college in Chicago at age 18.

While in college, she majored in criminal psychology and used the psychological skills that she learned to help surpress her memories. On graduation night, she went to a party and got very drunk. She was pulled into an alley by a woman named Garnet, who just happened to be a vampire, and was turned into one without explanation. After suffering for three days, Garnet showed up again to tell her what not to do as a vampire. Mainly...sunlight is bad and blood is good. At first, Selena didn't believe her, but found out soon enough when she had an unquenchable thirst and tried the blood thing and found out it worked.

She became a criminal psychologist and only worked at night, obviously, and learned about a killer named Mark Armeta. She became obsessed with him and found out he lived in some town called Isle Voletta and followed him there.
