 </FONT><FONT  COLOR="#400040" SIZE=2 PTSIZE=8><B>Healing</FONT><FONT  COLOR="#000000" SIZE=2 PTSIZE=8> - 0 Soul to use.  (no room command needed)</B><BR>
 <B>System/Duration:</B>  NA/NA<BR>
 <B>Effect/Description:</B> Angels have an incredible natural healing ability.  Any wound caused to the angel or its host body will heal at the rate of 1 Body per hour the character rests.  If the character takes any strenuous action, the healing is disrupted and the hour starts over.  A Room Host must be notified when you claim your Body point back.  Wounds created by faith water cannot be healed with this mastery.  Wounds from fire heal at the rate of 1 Body per day and only when the character sleeps.  If the character is woken up before the day is over, the healing process is disrupted and they do not regain their point (which means they cannot role-play until the damage is healed).  The damage from an attack that removes a limb heals at the normal rate, but the player must role-play the limb slowly growing back.  Being an energetic child and more than a wee bit precocious,he learned every nook and cranny of the church and surrounding area.Including this awesome and wonderous labrynth and cave system.Just on of the many secrets he'd discovered,including a hdey hole in which he could ease drop on the confessional.<BR>
 As soon as he'd come of age,with his uncle's grudging agreement he spent a tour in the military,the blackwatch to be exact.After serving his stint for god and country he turned his attentions to his hoped for lifelong avocation;that of a priest.Joining the order of St. Michael,he returned home to the parrish he knew and loved in hopes of starting a chapterhouse to the order.<BR>
 That and be near hisself and those he'd known and loved his whole life.While there hisself began a rapid decline.Watching his uncle once vital and full of life to a decrepit and shallow husk,a mere shadow of what he'd been was a strain upon him like he'd ever known.And after his death the parrish had been closed down and the church scheduled for closure and  destruction..<BR>
 Now his goal,the thing that filled his waking moments were only two things...One,to save the monument to hisself's life and service to the community and two;and just as important his home.And then Malcum comes back to claim his inheritance and things were bound to get interesting.<BR>
