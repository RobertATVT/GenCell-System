          Anastasia Midnight is a  vampire with a history that precedes her far beyond her years.  She is 25 years old and she has already learned the ways of her ancestors.  Coming from the dark streets of Paris, she has one thought on her mind.  It is only to survive.  She has never lost the battle of survival.  <BR>
          Even as a new vampiress, she was the strongest of the family.  Coming from a pure vampire bloodline, she has all the blue blood one could handle....literately.  Her mother being a countess of seduction, taught her all the moves to lure her unsuspecting victims in.  Her father's charm taught her how to be smooth, ruthless, and innocent looking.  <BR>
         After the third day and night she awoke in Paris she turned and killed her governess.  Pleased at her skills her family started preparing the training.  Going to the local hangouts, she learned to control her need of blood until she had her victim choosen.  She can be as patient as a tiger on the pounce.  <BR>
          As she was older she saw the need to move away from the family and start a fresh.  Seizing her chance she came to America.  Knowing that Louisana is known for its french quarters she came there.  Lost and confused she somehow made it to Black Bayou.  <BR>
          Now she will learn to survive in the most dangerous place, or will she?<BR>
