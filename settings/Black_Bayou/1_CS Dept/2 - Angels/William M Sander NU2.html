Healing - 0 Soul to use. (no room command needed)
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: Angels have an incredible natural healing ability. Any wound caused to the angel or its host body will heal at the rate of 1 Body per hour the character rests. If the character takes any strenuous action, the healing is disrupted and the hour starts over. A Room Host must be notified when you claim your Body point back. Wounds created by faith water cannot be healed with this mastery. Wounds from fire heal at the rate of 1 Body per day and only when the character sleeps. If the character is woken up before the day is over, the healing process is disrupted and they do not regain their point (which means they cannot role-play until the damage is healed). The damage from an attack that removes a limb heals at the normal rate, but the player must role-play the limb slowly growing back. Background

William Matthew Sanders was born in 1974 in Ireland. His parents were rich and very much respected business men. Until he was sixteen he lived an extremely typical life. He went to parties, dated, got good grades, and was generally a good guy. On his sixteenth birthday as he returned home from a party thrown for him by a group of friends he found his parents waiting for him at the door. There faces an eclectic mix of solemn excitement.
It was then that they introduced him to his lineage. As they explained it there family were descendants of Sandors Ignatius Sanders, the founder of a group known as The Ancients. This group were the watchers of the world the bearers of magics, and protectors of the innocent. With this Wil was shocked unable to even contemplate this, but as they continued the Ancients themselves had no inherent power, instead they had a natural propensity to be drawn to it. As they finished explaining this to Wil they informed him that they were sending him to America, to a small cabin in Pennsylvania where, with the help of his grandfather (Roger Sanders) he would learn all he would need to know to carry on the families lineage.
Three years passed as Wil was trained under the expert tutelage of Roger. He learned the skills necessary to be discreet and retain information. He was also introduced to the texts of the Ancients, which recorded there exploits for centuries. At the sight of what seemed like a library of knowledge Wil was at Awe, unable of even conceiving how all of this information could be written, even more Awed at what he heard next. "You will read, and memorize it all" Were the next words from his grandfather. Needless to say, Wil was stunned.
It took Wil another three years of nearly constant study, reading and rereading this tombs which often times ranged in languages from French to Ancient Greek. This entire time his grandfather began to grow weaker and more sickly. Will had grown into a strong young man, the physical and mental training giving him a new sense of being, and the knowledge of the Ancients giving him a new sense of purpose. Sadly Roger died just after Wil completed his studies, leaving Wil with the knowledge necessary to survive.
Knowledge however came with a price, Wil had become cold, much more aloof than he previously was. His heart remained pure, but on the outside at times he could even seem mean. That's why he found it difficult when he finally returned to the outside world.
He traveled for two years, searching out the magics that seemed to evade him at every turn. Each time he believed he found what he was looking for, he found it to be false and superficial. Being an Ancient, although he did pick up some of this, which he used when he returned to the States to become a world class magician.
He spent only months at this until he felt the calling on the magic returning. He needed, had to find at and he wouldn't rest until he did. He finally, after expending all other choices decided to travel to a place that seemed to him as another dead-end, Voletta. Here he hopes, but sincerely doubts that he will find what he has sought for so long.
