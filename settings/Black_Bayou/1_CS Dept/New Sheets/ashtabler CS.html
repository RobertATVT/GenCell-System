Background


Born in Isle Voletta, his father was a member of the Fire Department, his mother more or less a whore. She was sleeping around with the neighborhood men while Aaron slept upstairs, and when his father found out, all hell broke loose. To put it simply, his mother ended up in the cemetary, and his father was electrocuted on two charges of murder.

Young Aaron was sent to live with his grandmother, a life-long Bayou dweller with a penchant of telling the stories of her native Louisianna. So it became natural for him to repeat these stories. And soon, they found their way into writing.

A shining example of the parish Private School's, Aaron's first book went straight to a bestseller. "Gators in the Bayou" combined several old stories with a single plot-line. Staying true to his forum, he worked in the horror genre.

Right now, he's working on a novel based in the town of Isle Voletta, called "The Isle of Veils". However, with the publisher's breathing down his back, and the deadline getting closer, he has only that....a name. And so, he packed up, moved from his New York apartment, and back to his hometown...for material.
