System/Duration: NA / NA

Background

Kyle Bleeds was born in Trenton, New Jersey in 1974. He had about the same experience as the other Bleeds Brothers when he was a kid, so it's somewhat redundant to say it again. Kyle was the wild one of the bunch. People may say it was Alex...but he was just insane. Kyle's wild, but sensible. He went down to Voletta a few years to help Alex out with something...God only knows what...but disappeared soon after, along with Stephen.
After he had left Voletta, he went to Arizona, where he met his girlfriend, Sara Armitage(which means approval for her to use Kyle in her BG). They fell madly in love and did lots of crazy things together. From Arizona, they moved all about the South West, adventur e seeking and whatnot.
Eventually, Kyle got in contact with Stephen and found out Jon and Alex left Isle Voletta to kidnap Todd McFarlane. Kyle's brain got to work and he figured he would be able to exploit any connections Jon and Alex may have made in Voletta to line his pockets a little more...and off they went.
