Background: Azerin Creed was an angel, a representative of the Light, chosen to join with a human host, Timothy Zerin, to complete a mission in Voletta. However, during the joining, something went horribly and inexplicably wrong. Instead of the usual joining of host and angel, the identity of the host was obliterated, and the mind of Azerin was fused inextricably with the host's body in the same manner that the mortal's mind was. His celestial presence was stripped away, and now he was now just another mortal. However, even more was wrong. A body is not just a handy place to keep one's mind. A mind will quickly change to fit the body it resides in, like liquid poured into a new container. Normally, the powers of an angel would prevent this from happening, but without such powers, Azerin's mind was as malleable as putty. The mind of an angel simply would not comfortably fit into the new body, and nearly all of his memories were lost. Timothy Zerin's body, now occupied by the mind of former angel Azerin Creed, went into a coma for 12 years after the failed joining, before waking up to the doctors' astonishment at age 26. Now he is mortal, with neither memories of being human nor of being an angel. The only memories of his past are distant, removed, and he sees them only as dreams and visions, that he does not believe to be true. He believes that mortal is all he has ever been, even though he doesn't even fully understand what being mortal is.
There were physical side effects, as well. At the time of the joining, Timothy Zerin was 14, and the shorting out of cosmic energies within his body caused severe damage to his pituitary system. Now at 27, his body has not grown, his face the only part of him that has aged. For the past year he has wandered the Louisiana Bayou, until one day he, by strange chance, happens upon Isle Voletta...
