Special Abilities


Adrenaline - 0 Soul to use. (##Adrenaline##)
System/Duration: NA / Instant
Effect/Description: In intense situations, the human body is known to perform above and beyond normal levels. Stories abound of mothers lifting cars to save their children, or people reacting fast enough to cut in front of a speeding car to save a life. Seemingly impossible feats brought on by a simple little hormone called adrenaline. Any one physical roll is made at +8 (must be stated to the ST which roll before use, may not be used to add to mental roll, such as a will roll). This mastery may only be used once per night.

Luck - 0 Soul to use. (##LuckRe-roll##)
System/Duration: NA / Instant
Effect/Description: What is it about mortals that seems to always get them out of trouble at just the right moment? Whatever it is, this is the tendency for things to fall in place at just the right time. The user may re-roll any 1 dice roll. It may not be used to re-roll the same dice roll more than once, and the user must take whatever the second roll is, regardless of the result. The mortal can only enact this mastery twice per night.

The Gift - 0 Soul to use. (no room command needed)
System/Duration: NA / NA
Effect/Description: Everyone has that little something they just seem naturally gifted at. Whether it be juggling, mathematics, or knife throwing, it's there, and it's just waiting to be found. The player may gain 1 skill at level 3 for no cost, except Lore. However, they may not use other points to build upon the skill at generation, only later with updates. This skill must have an * by its name to denote which skill is the Gift. Should you purchase an Occupational Training type that grants a non-combat skill bonus that you have chosen for your Gift, the bonus levels add to the ones granted by the Gift. You may only use a skill from the non-combat skill list as your Gift if an occupation gives you additional levels in it during character generation. If you ever change breeds, you do not lose the levels of the gift unless they interfere with another rule. For example, if you have Chi it is automatically replaced with the fighting style of the new breed at half its current level. The levels are still halved as normal, even if it was the Gift.

Ghoul Special Abilities

Note: due to the unnatural origin of these abilities, they all cost soul to use.

Confidence - 2 Soul to use. (##Confidence##)
System/Duration: roll 2d6 + Mind vs TN: 16 / Scene; takes 1 turn to activate.
Effect/Description: The ghoul is a confident creature in most situations, almost to the point of arrogance. This confidence, when firmly believed in can strengthen the Will of the ghoul, granting it a bonus of +3 Will. This confidence is removed the moment the ghoul fails to avoid an attack or mastery for the rest of the night, or fails any other dice roll, as its confidence is now shaken.

Mindspeak - 1 Soul to use. (>>::master:: message<<)
System/Duration: NA / Scene; takes 0 rounds to activate.
Effect/Description: The Ghoul and their Vampire master have created a blood bond. With this bond, the Ghoul has a very limited form of Mindspeak. They may only communicate with their master, and only when within sight. The Ghoul may only transmit mental thoughts, and only to their master, and only while under the effects of ghouling.

Night Vision - 1 Soul to use. (no room command needed)
System/Duration: NA / Scene; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: The ghoul can see twice as far as a normal human can. In addition, when the lighting is low, or near total darkness, the vision of the ghoul takes on the effects of infrared-vision. Unable to make out detail, the ghoul can discern between animate and inanimate objects, sensing their red or blue glow, respectively.

Recovery - 1 Soul to use. (no room command necessary)
System/Duration: roll 2d6 + Soul vs. TN 10 / Instant; takes 0 rounds to activate.
Effect/Description: A ghoul will recover from damage taken in battle in a most unique way. When taken to 0 Body, a ghoul can summon one last burst of energy. After they spend the point of soul, the player makes the roll listed above. For every point above the target number they score, their character regains 1 point of Body. No character may raise their Body higher than it's normal maximum. This ability may only be used once per night.

Speed of the Cobra - 2 Soul to use. (##CobraSpeed##)
System/Duration: roll 2d6 + Body vs TN 16 / Scene; this mastery takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: The body of the ghoul posses a supernatural quickness which allows it to react faster than normal. This mastery adds +3 to all defensive rolls (i.e. Evasion and Reflexes)
