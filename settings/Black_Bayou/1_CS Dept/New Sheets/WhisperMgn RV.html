Background

Name: Whisper Morgan
Age: 22 Birthdate: March 13, 1977
Sex: Female
Race: Mortal

Personality Traits: beautiful, knows it, and flaunts it; appears cold and detached at first; indignant; skeptical and sometimes sarcastic; a habitual lier. NOTE: all of these traits are simply pieces of a mask that she uses to conceal a deeper, darker part of her life and to hide her true self from those that she fears may hurt her--namely, everyone.

Background: Whisper led a relatively normal life, even for the daughter of the wealthy stock broker, Richard Morgan III. Growing up in the posh Times Square district of New York City, she spent the first half of her life suckling the proverbial silver spoon. Her mother, Danielle Morgan, worked in Richard's brokerage as well; therefore, Whisper was well provided for. She wore the most fashionable clothing and attended the best schools. She was fairly popular and a model student. Surprisingly, she kept her feet planted firmly on the ground and refused to play the part of the spoiled little rich girl--at least in personality. During her sophomore year in high school, she became infatuated with a classmate with whom she eventually fell hopelessly in love. Then, everything fell apart. Richard's brokerage went under due to a poor business decision on his part. Luckily, he had enough money stashed away to pay off his debts, and he owned their home, but he was a ruined man. Danielle was among the unemployed after the fiasco, but she soon found a job working both at home and overseas in Japan. Richard, however, was suffering from severe depression which prevented him from working. Danielle's prolonged absences from home coupled with the anxiety of his lost business played a heavy tune on his heart until one day he just...snapped. One night, in a drunken stupor, he stole into Whisper's room and raped her. Just like that. She was too embarrassed and frightened to leave home, so she crawled behind the mask instead. Two weeks later, she discovered that she was pregnant. Desperate, she ran to her boyfriend for help, but when she arrived, she couldn't bring herself to tell him that the child was her father's...so she lied. Believing it was his, her boyfriend proclaimed his undying devotion to her and pledged his support for whatever decision she chose to make. Unable to bear the thought of a life so beautiful and yet so monstrous growing inside her, Whisper chose to have an abortion. So accompanied by her boyfriend, she stepped through the clinic doors. In spite of this tragedy, he asked for her hand in marriage, and she accepted. She called her mother overseas to tell her the news, and in response, Danielle decided to come home to celebrate. Late on the evening of her arrival, Whisper forgot to be strong and broke down in her mother's arms, blurting out the entire twisted story. Enraged, tears streaming down her cheeks, Danielle lost control. She dashed into the bedroom where Richard's sleeping from lay curled beneath the sheets. She pulled a loaded gun--for protection--from the drawer on her nightstand and unloaded two rounds into his head. Whisper chased after her mother and screamed as the shots were fired, crumpling into a sobbing heap on the floor, where she remained even as the local PD dragged Danielle away in handcuffs. In the aftermath, Danielle was locked away for life, and with Richard pushing up daisies, she left the entire estate to Whisper. Destroyed by the life she was living, Whisper decided that her only hope of survival was to begin a new life, free of the darkness that ate at her soul. So, she sold the house, returned the diamond, basically abandoned her mother in jail, and disappeared into the one place where she thought the past would never follow...the bayou.
