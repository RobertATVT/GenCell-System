Lore

1)Demon Lore 2
You know that the destructive nature of fire can burn and seriously injure demons. Any item embed with light's holy energy causes serious wounds to a demon. When a demon uses a mental mastery with you as the target within line of sight from you, roll 2d6+Mind+2 vs. a TN: 20. If you succeed, you recognize the ability and its effect, giving you a one time +3 to the resistance roll (if any) for that mastery. (cannot be used while in direct combat).
Faith Water: It takes 10 minutes to empower enough water for 1 attack. This empowering must be role-played in the main room for the entire 10 minutes for it to work. The character must put the water in whatever item they intend to attack the demon with prior to the empowering, such as: Balloons, vials, squirt guns, etc. Only 3 attacks worth of faith water may be empowered/time. The empowered water may only be used by the individual that created it. The roll to empower the water is 2d6+Soul+Faith vs TN 22. This roll must be witnessed and approved my either a room host, ST or GM. If an attack is successful, the demon suffers 1 point of damage that cannot be healed supernaturally. The demon must heal the damage as any normal mortal would. That would mean each point of damage would take 3 days.

2) Vampire Lore 3
When a vampire uses a mental mastery with you as the target within line of sight from you, roll 2d6+Mind+3 vs. a TN: 20. If you succeed, you recognize the ability and its effect, giving you a one time +5 to the resistance roll (if any) for that mastery. (masteries requiring the vampire use Will, Mind or Soul rolls only; cannot be used while in direct combat).
Wooden Stakes: To kill a vampire, they must first be brought down to the negative total of their body statistic. Once the appropriate body number is achieved, the attacker must place the point of the stake over the vampire's heart. A full round of uninterrupted concentration is necessary as they summon their faith, empowering it. This is when a roll of 2d6+Soul+Faith vs TN 26 must be rolled successfully. A ST or GM must be present. If the roll is successful, the stake must be driven through the vampire's heart on the round following the roll. Seconds later, nothing will remain of the body as the ashes are extremely thin, disintegrating with any kind of contact. No attack roll is necessary.
