Background

Theo got her ass kicked. A lot. Being an opinionated girl who was unabashed in nearly everything she did, it was difficult for her to make friends. Raised on a shoe string budget and lost as the only girl in a family of 6 brothers, Theo was ambitious. She wanted to get out of the cramped Chicago apartment (they were clearly violating occupancy laws: herself, 6 brothers, two parents, her mother's parents, and her uncle) and do something exciting. School was her window, and she leapt right out. She was fascinated with many things, mostly old relics and the like. She eventually grew to be an archeologist, venturing to distant lands to dig up old stuff. Exciting, right? What did excite her were the legends that went with these artifacts, many times dark and horribly fascinating. She learned quite a lot about demons from all this. She hardly took note of the other stories. Her amulet was discovered at a peculiar sight and was the first and last find that she lifted. There was something about it that would not allow her to let go. She wouldn't give it up to any museum. It was special. After her partner turned against her, she decided to take a break from her profession. She just got in the car and began to drive.
