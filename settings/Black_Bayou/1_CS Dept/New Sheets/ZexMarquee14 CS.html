Background:

Zechs Marquise was born on October 19, 1977, in New York, New York. More specifically, the part of town was called Little Italy, and Zechs family ruled it all. He was the son of Heero Marquise mafia king pin of new york, and being the only male in a family with three sisters made him the sole air to the Mafia rule. So Zechs ,at the mere age of six, already had his life plannned out for him, but somone else had a plan for him and the rest of his family too. It was Jimmy the "butcher" Ventura, family head of the Bronx area, he wanted Heero's territory, and would stop at nothing to get it. That includes hiring a hit man to kill the whole family.
Zechs was late getting home from school and he knew that his dad hated it when he was late, so he hurried home and even prepared an excuse to try to get out of a punishment. When he finally arrived at his house he found more than a angry father, he found a dead family, more specifically his dead family. As he ran from the house littered with bodies he was intercepted by the hit man that did the job, and suddenly Zechs didnt care if he lived or died and took the Man on right there, but this David had no chance against Goliath, and when Zechs realized this he broke down crying right there. He got lucky that the hit man took pity on this newly orphaned child, and took him in as his own.
The hit mans name turned out to be Duo Maxwell, an ex fed with a love for the art of killing, but this killer had a weakness, a want of a companion, someone to share his joy with and that is what he got in Zechs, he also got an apt pupil with a rage in his heart. Over the years Duo taught Zechs all he knew about the art of death, even the teqniques he learned in the FBI, and Zechs became especially talented at distance riflery and information gathering, or in normal terms, sniping and spying.
After a while they became partners, Zechs would use his wit and charisma to line up the contractors and they would both take on the target, it was an unstopable team that made enough money to support a small army through WWIII, but that isnt why Zechs did it, he did it to quench the anger in his heart. That is probally the reason he later killed Duo, just for resolve, a resolve that killing the man that made him into what he was, could only create. After that Zechs looked to begin a "business" of his own, that is how he ended up in Isle Voletta, he wants to make a new beginning for himself in a new place, but now that he has killed the reason for his anger he also thinks that he may find another profession, eventhough what he does best is kill.
