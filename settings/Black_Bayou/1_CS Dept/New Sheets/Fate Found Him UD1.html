Background

Born in Pittsburgh, 1975, Michael was a brilliant child. He often was a schemer, never satisfied with the things normal children enjoyed. Reading Algebra 3 books in the 5th grade Michael was not only very intelligent but very sick. He would be found reading books of hate and torture. Michael didn't have many friends, he always considered himself too goof for them. He was a very quiet person, he was never abused or hurt. Its was just that the chemicals in his brain weren't functioning correctly. That's all.

Michael studied at Carnage Melon and got a bachelor's in literature. He was studying to become a writer, he always loved to write, it was because he was in control of his own little world, and what better place to be in?

Michael always loved the supernatural and mythology, he decided he'd peruse his career in a quite place wanting to get out of the big city. After doing research on the Internet for a month he found the South would suit his needed most, all his needs. He found no more a closed off area with moderate life than Voletta. After making a few calls to a real-estator buddy from college; an apartment was reserved at a reasonable price.
