 The research had been tedious work, they never cared if we slept or ate. They wanted the researched finished. They promised our freedom. and safe return to the world once it was done.
I for one, never believed that they would let us go alive. I mean we had the research and the knowledge in our heads. But that opinion changed when I woke up one morning in a small run down hotel just outside of New Orleans. I had no ID, Just the cloths on my back and a small amount of cash tucked inside my small black handbag. My bank cards, everything that I had all the years I was held captive, gone, But I had my freedom. For how long, that I don't know.
I studied medicine and biological science, mastered in biochemistry and minored in Medicine. That what seemed to catch their eye. I have a mind like a steal trap and I was good at everything I did. I flew through high school and collage. It wasn't easy, but I found out as a young child, that with enough work and determination, I can do anything.
I have a brother, Older by 2 years, and a little sister, younger by 6 years. I call her Ooops, but she's the best thing that ever happened to the family. She is what they used against me to get me to work for them. They wanted my brain as well as that of the skill of a few others. We were all drugged and taken to what seemed to be a very secluded island. We worked like salves for the safety of our friends and family, and for the chance that there would be freedom in the end. For me, that seems to be the small town of Voletta.

Project: Her research was that of the effects of certain chemicals on the brain. Searching for a chemical reaction that would allow one to control memory. Making it possible for one to erase periods of time and even replace them with falsified memory.
Also she was brought into other projects to help or consult on several chemical weapons.. It was not made clear the purpose of these things, but she isn't stupid. The chemical compounds that were teated were clearly for destructive prepossess.
Her part was to research the chemical reactions in the human body. She was shocked as she stumbled across some DNA and the way the accumulation of certain chemicals could almost rewrite the human DNA. She liked to refer to these projects as the "X-Men files".
She was able to pinpoint the correct combinations of chemicals that would allow a human to transform into something UN human. The whole Hulk-David Banner scenario was now a possibility, but with her work and that of the few others. It could be controlled, unlike that in the old TV shows she use to watch as a child.
