Special Abilities



Healing - 0 Soul to use. (no room command needed)
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: Angels have an incredible natural healing ability. Any wound caused to the angel or its host body will heal at the rate of 1 Body per hour the character rests. If the character takes any strenuous action, the healing is disrupted and the hour starts over. A Room Host must be notified when you claim your Body point back. Wounds created by faith water cannot be healed with this mastery. Wounds from fire heal at the rate of 1 Body per day and only when the character sleeps. If the character is woken up before the day is over, the healing process is disrupted and they do not regain their point (which means they cannot role-play until the damage is healed). The damage from an attack that removes a limb heals at the normal rate, but the player must role-play the limb slowly growing back. (Length of time for the limb to regenerate is determined by the Forum Director.)

Mindspeak - 0 Soul to use. (>>::target of Mindspeak:: message<<)
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: By concentrating on a chosen target, the angel is capable of speaking directly to the creature's mind. Mortals are unable to return the thoughts. The target must be within view of the angel attempting to communicate in this fashion. Mindspeak can only be used to speak to one individual at a time.

The Gift of Life - 0 Soul to use. (##GiftLife ##)
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs TN 18 / Instant; takes 2 rounds to activate.
Effect/Description: The angel may transfer their Body points to any willing (or stunned / unconscious) individual through touch. This transfer is almost instantaneous in normal RP, but during combat, no more than 2 Body points can be transferred per round, requiring an re-roll for every additional round. Transferring Body points to a stunned or unconscious individual without permission is perfectly acceptable. This same mastery also has a number of minor effects, causing flowers to bloom spontaneously, or making a starving person feel as though they've just eaten their full. In the end, a Host or Gamemaster has the final say in what can and cannot be performed with this power.

Volange - 0 Soul to use. (##Volange##)
System/Duration: NA/NA; take 1 round to shapeshift.
Effect/Description: Even though an angel's essence is bound the mortal body it inhabits, when confronting the vilest evil, the angel can sometimes extend its true self. By exerting its will, the angel's symbolic self materializes in the real world, temporarily changing the body of its mortal host. This form varies greatly from angel to angel depending on the aspect of the Light they represent. This form does not have to me a mortal-like form. Angels have appeared in many different guises throughout history. The form you choose must be chosen at Character Creation and approved. To get approval, please send your request to ANTEmail 2. There are a few things about this ability that remain the same, regardless of the chosen form. The angel's true form will always radiate a soft, white aura that anyone with the Faith skill will see as obvious. The Angel will also gain +1 Mind and +2 Soul while in this form.

(Just a quick note about shape changing (all forms, except Transcendence). While an angel is in another form, if its current Body falls to 0, the angel will immediately change back to mortal form minus the damage it has already taken, leaving a minimum of 1 Body. It should also be noted that materiel objects do not shapeshift with the character. Any and all clothes and objects the character was holding will need to be transported by some other means.)


Masteries



A Touch of Kindness - 1 Soul to use. (##ToK ##)
Generation Cost: 5 / Experience Cost: 25
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs victim's 2d6+Will / 4 rounds or 2 minutes; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: The angel can fill any character with a brief and overwhelming sense of compassion and amity. If timed correctly, the person may follow through on some action they would not have done otherwise, i.e., giving to charity, befriending a lost soul, lending advice. Using this gift to derive personal benefit is considered a grave sin. This mastery can force another character out of their True forms (i.e., Horus, TVF, TAF, etc.). If successful target is filled with compassion, -3 to Attack for duration.

Unseen - 1 Soul to use. (##Unseen## or ##Unseen ##)
Generation Cost: 10 / Experience Cost: 50
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs TN see below / see below; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: The angel can become the epitome of what it was meant to be: the impartial observer, making unknown subtle changes for good. The angel becomes invisible to all sight, including infrared and all sound, though not imperceptible with touch or scent. As an option, the angel may be visible to one other person, but no more than one. TN is 22 to turn invisible to all and 24 to be visible to only one person. Should the angel exceed a target number of 28, they will be able to touch and otherwise communicate normally with the selected target. The mastery remains in effect until the angel is either touched, attacks or otherwise comes into contact with someone else physically.
