 Special Abilities


Healing - 0 Soul to use. (no room command needed)
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: Angels have an incredible natural healing ability. Any wound caused to the angel or its host body will heal at the rate of 1 Body per hour the character rests. If the character takes any strenuous action, the healing is disrupted and the hour starts over. A Room Host must be notified when you claim your Body point back. Wounds created by faith water cannot be healed with this mastery. Wounds from fire heal at the rate of 1 Body per day and only when the character sleeps. If the character is woken up before the day is over, the healing process is disrupted and they do not regain their point (which means they cannot role-play until the damage is healed). The damage from an attack that removes a limb heals at the normal rate, but the player must role-play the limb slowly growing back. (Length of time for the limb to regenerate is determined by the Forum Director.)

Mindspeak - 0 Soul to use. (>>::target of Mindspeak:: message<<)
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: By concentrating on a chosen target, the angel is capable of speaking directly to the creature's mind. Mortals are unable to return the thoughts. The target must be within view of the angel attempting to communicate in this fashion. Mindspeak can only be used to speak to one individual at a time.

The Gift of Life - 0 Soul to use. (##GiftLife ##)
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs TN 18 / Instant; takes 2 rounds to activate.
Effect/Description: The angel may transfer their Body points to any willing (or stunned / unconscious) individual through touch. This transfer is almost instantaneous in normal RP, but during combat, no more than 2 Body points can be transferred per round, requiring an re-roll for every additional round. Transferring Body points to a stunned or unconscious individual without permission is perfectly acceptable. This same mastery also has a number of minor effects, causing flowers to bloom spontaneously, or making a starving person feel as though they've just eaten their full. In the end, a Host or GM or ST has the final say in what can and cannot be performed with this power.

Volange - 0 Soul to use. (##Volange##)
System/Duration: NA/NA; take 1 round to shapeshift.
Effect/Description: Even though an angel's essence is bound the mortal body it inhabits, when confronting the vilest evil, the angel can sometimes extend its true self. By exerting its will, the angel's symbolic self materializes in the real world, temporarily changing the body of its mortal host. This form varies greatly from angel to angel depending on the aspect of the Light they represent. This form does not have to be a mortal-like form. Angels have appeared in many different guises throughout history. The form you choose must be chosen at Character Creation and approved. To get approval, please send your request to BBSTMail. There are a few things about this ability that remain the same, regardless of the chosen form. The angel's true form will always radiate a soft, white aura that anyone with the Faith skill will see as obvious. The Angel will also gain +1 Mind and +2 Soul while in this form.

(Just a quick note about shape changing (all forms, except Transcendence). While an angel is in another form, if its current Body falls to 0, the angel will immediately change back to mortal form minus the damage it has already taken, leaving a minimum of 1 Body. It should also be noted that materiel objects do not shapeshift with the character. Any and all clothes and objects the character was holding will need to be transported by some other means.)

Physical Description


Angus is a quiet, unassuming Scotsman in his mid 30's - a rare breed indeed. A tall, lanky man [6'0" -- 190lbs] he nonetheless is very robust, and enjoys hiking. He sports shoulder length auburn hair, and a fine beard etched along the line of his jaw. A moustache sprouts from under a sharp nose, upon which rests a pair of wire frame glasses. Thick glasses. He likes to dress in jeans and plaids, matched up with soft leather boots, if indoors, and working boots if outside
Volange form appearance:Uriel ("Fire of God"): Identified as both a seraph and a cherub, Uriel is most well known for his role as Regent of the Sun in the book of Revelation where he calls forth the birds of the air to feast upon the fallen. As one of the most faithful and dedicated members of the host, Uriel was also placed in charge of Tartarus (another name for Hades), warned Noah of the impending flood (although Raphael is credited with teaching the building of the ark), and attacked Moses for failing to circumcise his son. Accepted as an archangel by the Church for many centuries, he was finally removed from the records in 745 CE as the Church became increasingly concerned with the prominence the public was placing upon angels.

Background

Born on Christmas day in 1969 in the Parrish of Ardlui,at the morning mass.Being born to a traditional family he was brought up in the ways of the clans and the Catholic Church.Having shown a early aptitude towards the clerical life his uncle 'hisself' the parrish priest took him in to follow in his footsteps.
Being an energetic child and more than a wee bit precocious,he learned every nook and cranny of the church and surrounding area.Including this awesome and wonderous labrynth and cave system.Just on of the many secrets he'd discovered,including a hdey hole in which he could ease drop on the confessional.
As soon as he'd come of age,with his uncle's grudging agreement he spent a tour in the military,the blackwatch to be exact.After serving his stint for god and country he turned his attentions to his hoped for lifelong avocation;that of a priest.Joining the order of St. Michael,he returned home to the parrish he knew and loved in hopes of starting a chapterhouse to the order.
That and be near hisself and those he'd known and loved his whole life.While there hisself began a rapid decline.Watching his uncle once vital and full of life to a decrepit and shallow husk,a mere shadow of what he'd been was a strain upon him like he'd ever known.And after his death the parrish had been closed down and the church scheduled for closure and destruction..
Now his goal,the thing that filled his waking moments were only two things...One,to save the monument to hisself's life and service to the community and two;and just as important his home.And then Malcum comes back to claim his inheritance and things were bound to get interesting.
