Background:

Wolf von'Darkmoor born a werewolf in 1969, and moved to New Orleans with his parents when he was 14. He wanted to be a police officer like all the rest of his family, but instead disappointed them when he went the other way about it and became a mercenary. When he was 19 he parents left to go back to Scotland and disowned him. He traveled getting jobs all around the US. When he came to Isle Voletta he met Vortex, owner of CMA (Central Mercenary Agency). He joined up with Vortex and met James Mag through a job he did to protecting one of his pack members, and was soon after made a pack member. Vortex was killed and Wolf left CMA, he was then supported by the pack, in which he was Omega, Beta, then Alpha. After the pack died off, he and Kali went back to Scotland to get married and live there. He became a bartender in his own bar. After while things started going bad with Kali, his wife, and they separated. After living on his own a very powerful Lamia beat him and made him mortal again. After that he forgot he was a werewolf and forgot nothing else of his past but that which dealt with his wolf self. After sometime he and his wife divorced because she had become heartless again. And then he returned to Voletta to live and remember his happier times.
