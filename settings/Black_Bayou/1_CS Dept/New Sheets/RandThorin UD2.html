Background

Rand Thorin began life as just your average American male. Born into a middle income family in Los Angeles, he was an only child. He attended school and graduated in the upper divisions of his class, then went on to the University of California at Los Angeles to study towards a history major. Rand spent many years working hard, trying to achieve the best life he could.
It was after all this running around that Rand grew tired of it all. He longed for some way to get away, but the means to do so eluded him.
Rand graduated college and found dull job in retail. Shortly after his twenty-third birthday, however, he got his break. A rich uncle suddenly died of a massive heart attack. Though the event caused much grief for Rand, because he truly had loved that uncle, it was also through this death that he inherited a small fortune of half a million dollars, chiefly due to the fact that the uncle had held Rand just as dear.
Possessing $500,000, it was tempting to splurge it. Rand, however, sought professional help, and within a few years had invested most of it in farm land and apartments/housing. Through money paid to him as rental and leases, as well as percentages of the crops grown on his land, he was able to maintain an income that allowed a comfortable lifestyle. And of course, as time went on, that level of comfort rose.
With his relatively new income and freedom, Rand traveled across the nation, searching for an interesting place to make his home in. While such places as Colorado, Texas, and Oregon intrigued him, he just didn't feel that they were "the place."
Finally, his travels brought him to Voletta. Rand surveyed the area and the inhabitants, and knew instantly that this would be an exciting place for him to spend the remainder of his life.
He had a home built not far from downtown, and concentrated on living the life offered by this strange city. He's met many strange people, some of the chief players in his life being Ms Syn Blaidd, Special Agent Andy Trask, and Ms. Lydia Thorne.

He has had two great lost loves. The first was a high school girlfriend named Amanda. They were together for two years; however, just before graduation, she and some of her friends celebrated a little too much and subsequently rolled their car, killing everyone inside.
It was only in his fourth and final year of college that Rand met his next romantic interest. Her name was Carrie and they met in a history class and soon began seeing each other outside the academic life. No accident or injury claimed her, but after a few months the relationship broke down, and she began seeing another man.
Shortly after moving to Isle Voletta, however, he met his third love interest. Bryn Monroe, daughter of a wealthy Georgian banker. Though they had their ups and down in the relationship, they were finally married on February 14, 1998.

Rand has four other passions in life. He is an avid book collector, searching high and low for novels, histories, and informational texts. In his house he spends most of his time in the library, a massive room with floor to ceiling shelves to hold the collection he has accumulated through the years.
Classic cars are another great interest. Though he drives a 4x4 Chevrolet around Voletta for practical purposes, he does own a 1969 AMC Javelin and a '69 Chevy Camaro. He loves all his cars and delights in keeping them in top form to show off to friends.
The third reason for Rand's continued life is a desire to explore the unknown- this is the driving force behind coming to and staying in Voletta. He knows that there is much that is strange in this area, and this catches his interest. In fact, he's made a special interest of learing about the "nightlife" in the bayou, and is somewhat of a scholar on all the creatures and beings running lose in the swamps.
The final thing Rand is attached to is his cat Amber. He practically worships this cat, seeing to its every needs and spoiling it rotten. The surest way to rile Rand would be to insult or injure his cat.
