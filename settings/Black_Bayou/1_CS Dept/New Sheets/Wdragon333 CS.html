System/Duration: NA / NA

An only child, life had grown very boring for Bryant, and he jumped at the chance to go to a Christian college for 4 years. There, he studied on Demons and Angels, though his faith in Angels slipped. However, he strongly believed in Demons and feared them greatly.

When Bryant returned from college, he found his parents ripped limb from limb. Deep down inside, he knew that Demons had done the job, though he wasn't for sure. He vowed to slay each and every one of the creatures. In his quest to destroy Demons, he ventured to Voletta. That brings us to now.
