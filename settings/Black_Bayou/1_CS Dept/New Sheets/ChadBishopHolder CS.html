Background


During his childhood, he remained a pretty laid back Character. He's more of the strong, silent type than the fierce grizzly type. Since Childhood, he and his brother, Clay Holder, have been clueless as to why their names were changed at birth. It seems as though their entire family's name was changed from Bishop to Holder. Chad's full name was Chad Clay Bishop but was changed to Chad Bishop Holder for reasons that he does not know. During his younger years, he graduated from FBI School and became a federal agent. The training tought him to cover emotions and other such things to make him a stonger member of the force. He trained extensivly to make his body into a feirce weapon but uses his strength only to help. But Why Voletta? Together, Chad and Clay, have been investigating the mysterious murder of his Aunt, Uncle, and two cousins. While on the investigation, they discovered one of their cousins had escaped and they've been trying to find him ever sense to see how he's been doing and to hopefully shed some light on the situation. When in Texas, both brothers met with a Bar Owner named John Kyle. Kyle told the men that Matt Holder, their cousin, had gone off to Isle Voletta, LA to search for his past. With incredible joy, the team of men rushed off to Voletta.
