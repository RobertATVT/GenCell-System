Background


Jillian Elizabeth Craft, born May 28th, 1973 to Arthur and Lydia Craft in Melbourne, Australia. She stands 5'9", 128 lbs., long curly blonde hair and stunning aqua blue eyes that have made more than one man stop in his tracks and stare. The youngest daughter of two, Jill always looked up to her older sister, Miranda, wanting to be exactly like her and her best friend, Alicia Monterey. Jill was devestated when Miranda died of the AIDS virus in the winter of 1994, leaving home and the reminders of her sister behind to begin a new life in the Outback. A natural love of animals and nature gained her a job as a Safari guide for Aussie Tours, guiding small groups of tourists around the country and showing them the beauty and wonder that is Australia. In her off hours, which were few and far between as the legend of Jill Craft spread through the countryside of being an amazing guide, painting glorious pictures in her charges minds as they sat around campfires at night and listened of her plans for the next days adventure which always turned out to be so much more incredible than she'd described, she served as a Tracker for police departments across the continent, her skills unmatched by even some of the most experienced Bushmen.

In the Spring of '95, she met another Tracker, Marshall Wright, and dispite the warnings from other women he'd been with, Jill fell head over heels. Life was perfect for Jill, she and Marshall living in the small home she had built with her own hands in a quiet and stunningly beautiful spot among massive wild flowers and a small natural waterfall. Unfortunately, Marshall turned into exactly what his former ladies said he would; a controlling, vicious man that would beat Jill into a bleeding, unconcious mess if she didn't do what she was told. She stayed in this relationship until early March of this year, 2000. She left after planning and scheming for months, working on the fool proof way to escape Marshall. Saddened she had to leave the Outback she loved so much, she has come to Isle Voletta to begin her life over, for the third time.

Naturally a joker, Jill is graceful and lilth, her smile ever present. Extremely quick witted, a one liner always perched on full lips and ready to sting. Her clothing is usually faded jeans, Doc Marten boots and t-shirts.
