Meditation - 0 Soul to use. (##Meditation##)
System/Duration: NA / NA; takes 30 minutes to activate
Effect/Description: In order to center themselves and replenish their mental energies after utilizing the power of the mind, a psychic needs to shut themselves off from the world around them and meditate. If they are interrupted (legitimately) for even a moment, the meditation must begin anew. 1 Soul Point may be regained each half-hour, going no higher than the original level.

For the Psychic to keep this mastery in effect, they must continue to concentrate. This means no movement quicker than a slow walk, and no other masteries may be performed. The Psychic can speak, but if physically attacked, the concentration is broken.

Background


April 21st, 1988.
Carnegie High School
Pittsburgh, PA.

Chris Lederman begins the long walk home with a black eye. It's 1988 and the smart kid with glasses always gets beat up. Upon reaching his house he quickly heads up the steps into his room. An IBM 80286 with a brand new Hayes 2400 is waiting for him. He'll show them, he'll show them all.

November 18th, 1996
Aiken Labs
Boston, MA.

An employee of Aiken Labs, a computer science center, is fired for violating usage agreements. His use of Aiken computers to decrypt unlawfully obtained data had him not only fired but banned from campus and redlisted. That same day a call is recieved via the Arpanet dial-ins and every computer in the lab crashes. The data is not recovered.

April 5th, 1997
Carnegie Mellon University, Margaret Thacks Hall
Pittsburgh, PA

A man wanders on to campus and into MTH. He sits down nonchalantly and begins using the network. Administrators assume he is a student and students assume he is an administrator. After a week of his mysterious computer usage, a supervisor approaches him and asks that he identify himself. He claims to be a student, Michael Vapor, and that he must be leaving. School records show no signs of anyone even applying under that name. Mike Vapor is never seen again.

Roanoake, VA

He's thinking of heading for Georgia or maybe Florida but neither appeal to him. Then, a thought crosses his mind...what about Louisiana?
