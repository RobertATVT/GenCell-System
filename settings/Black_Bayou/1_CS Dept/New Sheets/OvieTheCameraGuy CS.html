Background

Born and raised in New Hampshire, Lord Charles Oswald Madeson is the sole heir to the Madeson estate, son of Lord James Killian Madeson, who earned his fortune through the production and distribution of fortune cookies. Basically from a rich family he does practically nothing in school but passes with straight As because his father bribes all of his teachers. Moving onto college, he spends the majority of his time drinking and partying, and reading old comic books. After seeing the movie "The Blair Witch Project" he gains interest in the occult and hears of a similar area, Ilse Voletta, who's bayou is said to be inhabited by a species of super-animals responsible for several mysterious attacks in the town. A minor in journalism, he joins up with three other curious idiots like himself and travels to Voletta in hopes of documenting and proving the existence of the "Bayou Wolf".

Note: This character has been created for the sole purpose of furthering the "In Search of the Bayou Wolf" SL.
