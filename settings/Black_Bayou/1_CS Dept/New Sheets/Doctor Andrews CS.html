Background


Born June 4, 1974, Dr. Michael Andrews is 24 years old. He grew up in Los Angeles, California, with divorced parents who were both very rich. His father was the owner of a multinational corporation, and his mother was a fashion designer. He and his younger sister Melissa were spoiled, always getting whatever they wanted. Despite this, he developed a keen sense of caring and became very worried about the world. He excelled in school, and went on to get a PhD in Emergency Medicine at Harvard. Dr. Andrews moved to Miami, FL, and headed up the Surgery team at Miami Children's Hospital. He was very skilled as a surgeon, and almost never lost a patient. Part of this was due to his strong religious beliefs, his entire life being lived Jewish. He moved to Isle Voletta, LA, picking up a job at Voletta Parish General Hospital.
