The Gift - 0 Soul to use. (no room command needed)
System/Duration: NA / NA
Effect/Description: Everyone has that little something they just seem naturally gifted at. Whether it be juggling, mathematics, or knife throwing, it's there, and it's just waiting to be found. If you ever change breeds, you do not lose the levels of the gift unless they interfere with another rule. For example, if you have Chi it is automatically replaced with the fighting style of the new breed at half its current level. The levels are still halved as normal, even if it was the Gift.

Background


Growing up in Las Vegas is not a normal childhood. The city doesn't come alive until the night. She was always fascinated with the nightlife. It was more exciting than what was happening during the day. When she was old enough she went to California and soon started to work for Club Raven. Unbeknownst to her it was a haven for more than the nightlife. It was a haven for vampires. She came under the protection of the owner. She had been ghouled by him for years, her loyalty ran with him and she soon became indispensible in his business. He used her to do his daytime work, and used her as her knowledge increased to kill the ones he felt didn't offer society the gift. She became knowledgeable in the world of vampires and once out of the hand of Gregory, she preyed on them. Her mission was to kill them. They stalked the innocent and they ran the power of the country. She got to where she could find them in even the darkest crevices of society. She set up a one man operation under the name of R.I.P. Pest Control. She was hired to exterminate those pests that just wouldn't go away. Until a phone call from a friend, she was content to stay in California. Now she is on the trek to Voletta, Louisiana. Seems they have a pest problem.
