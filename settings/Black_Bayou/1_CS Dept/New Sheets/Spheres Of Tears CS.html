Background


Franz was usually good at whatever he did. He was particularly good at hurting people. A fiendish temper and not knowing what to do with it he searched for the un-doer of his parents. Traveling to America from his home in Germany after giving up a life as an underground street fighter his life was one big bet and he has a battle scar from every bet mand on his head. He's an easy person to get along with. Although he is known for making a few paitings with the victem's face as his canvas if provoked.

Franz grew up as a fighter, and remains as a fighter. His father and uncle taught him everything he knows about his craft. Franz is a calm person most of the time, but if his temper does flair he usually has little control. He started making money on petty street fights against neighborhood children, he realized, by crusing them all, he should look into this as a professions. Thats exactly what he did. Franz fought the underground for 15 years, he started when he was 17. 32 now he's ready to settle down and take his money to America and star repairing the life he left, and healing the scar he created.

His manager arraged an apartment for him from a friend in America. Franz would be staying in Voletta. He was excited, with his parents recently killed he thought he'd find peace in this place. Him going to Voletta was no mistake, infact he wanted to go there. Seems the murderer of his parents dropped a piece of paper; it had a phone number on it with the area code of Voletta on it. Franz knew he would have to find his parents' killer. Then his plans at peace would eventually fall into place.
