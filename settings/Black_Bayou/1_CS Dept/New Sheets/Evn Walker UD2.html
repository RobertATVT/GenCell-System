Healing
0 Soul to use. (no room command needed)
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: Werewolves have an incredible natural healing ability. Any wound caused to the werewolf will heal at the rate of 1 Body per hour the character rests. If the character takes any strenuous action, the healing is disrupted and the hour starts over. A Room Host must be notified when you claim your Body point back. Wounds created by bane water cannot be healed with this mastery. Wounds from fire heal at the rate of 1 Body per day and only when the character sleeps. If the character is woken up before the day is over, the healing process is disrupted and they do not regain their point (which means they cannot role-play until the damage is healed). The damage from an attack that removes a limb heals at the normal rate, but the player must role-play the limb slowly growing back. (Length of time for the limb to regenerate is determined by the Forum Director.)

Horus Form
0 Soul to use. (##HorusForm ##)
System/Duration: NA/Until target(s) is destroyed; takes 1 round to shape shift
Effect/Description: Tall, muscular, and extremely strong, this raging beast is terror incarnate. The massive head of a deranged wolf sits upon its massive shoulders. Thick matted fur covers its body as four inch claws tip the fingers on their enormous hands. Their eyes are slits like animals, but still retain their human color. Their reasoning is that of a maddened beast; pure instinct. Impervious to logic, they seek only to destroy the target of their fury . It will go to whatever means necessary to kill the target of its rage, whether it is loved one or foe. Once the target has been killed, reason begins to fight its way back. How the Horus reacts depends on the individual it is. Some flee while others fall to their knees sobbing. While in Horus form, the werewolf (currently) gains +3 Body (if the rules of the curse are not adhered to, we will change this bonus to +1). Due to its enraged state, the Horus is immune to the following masteries: Terror, Hypnotic Suggestion, Mind Scrye, Illusions, and Twisted Visions. Since the Horus is unable to think rationally, it may only use the following masteries: Dulled Pain, Rending, Agile Wolf, and Terror.

Mindspeak
0 Soul to use. (>>::target of Mindspeak:: message<<)
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: By concentrating on a chosen target, the werewolf is capable of speaking directly to the creature's mind. Mortals are unable to return the thoughts. The target must be within view of the werewolf attempting to communicate in this fashion. Mindspeak can only be used to speak to one individual at a time.

Wolf Form
0 Soul to use. (##WolfForm##)
System/Duration: NA/Unlimited; takes 1 round to shape shift
Effect/Description: A werewolf who is attuned to its own inner nature is able to assume its sister form at will. They appear in many different shapes and sizes, their wolf forms often taking an appearance characteristic of their personality. They still retain their normal eye color, however. While in wolf form, they gain +1 Body.

Summon The Beast
0 Soul to use. (##SummonBeast##)
System/Duration: N/A; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: Even the wisest and most peaceful of beings understands that use of force is sometimes a necessary thing. The "next step" in self discipline, the ability to summon forth the beast during even the most relaxed situations. By way of this mastery, the Horus form can be called forth whenever the Alpha desires. When they willingly bring their beast to the surface, they are treated the exact same as any other Horus except for a limited intelligence that remains. While they are unable to communicate or use mental masteries, they are capable of discerning who is an enemy and who is not, combine their attacks to those they choose. As stated above, it takes 1 round to summon forth the beast within. It takes and additional round to actually shape change to the Horus form. No target is required when the Horus is brought about in this manner.

Taming The Beast
0 Soul to use. (##Tame ##)
System/Duration: 2d6+Will vs victim's 2d6+Will / Scene; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: Although rare, it is not unheard of for a werewolf to get out of line within the pack. Most Alphas have realized the death of their fellows serves absolutely no purpose. Therefore, some Alphas have learned alternate ways to handle to disobedient. During a total eclipse of the moon, all werewolves temporarily "loose the wolf". Meaning, they lose all of their masteries, shape changing abilities and for all intents and purposes, become mortal until the light of the moon once again graces the sky. An Alpha who has studied this phenomena can attempt to duplicate it's affects upon a single individual by sheer force of will. Should the Alpha beat the victim in the test of wills mentioned above, the victim will effectively loose the use of all their masteries for the rest of the scene. This includes everything from shape changing to Mindspeak. If the victim is in wolf or Horus form, they will immediately revert back to their mortal form. The use of this mastery is often considered a great insult by many werewolves and if used without good reason, could result in an overthrow of the Alpha.

(Just a quick note about shape changing (all forms). While a werewolf is in another form, if its current Body falls to 0, the werewolf will immediately change back to mortal form minus the damage it has already taken, leaving a minimum of 1 Body. It should also be noted that materiel objects do not shapeshift with the character. Any and all clothes and objects the character was holding will need to be transported by some other means.)

Agile Wolf
1 Soul to use. (##AgileWolf##)
Generation Cost: 5 / Experience Cost: 25
System/Duration: 2d6+Body vs TN 16 / Scene; takes 1 round to activate
Effect/Description: The body of the werewolf possesses a natural animal quickness , which allows them to react faster than normal. This mastery can be used in all forms of the werewolf, as the animal within them has turned their instincts to animal based, not human. This mastery adds +3 to all defense rolls (i.e. Evasion and Reflexes).

Dispas
1 Soul to use. (##Dispas##)
Generation Cost: 10 / Experience Cost: 50
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs TN 20 / Indefinitely; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: The cunning wolf can sometimes blend in with its surroundings when danger is near. For all intents and purposes, the Werewolf is considered to be totally invisible to the eye and all waves of the light spectrum, including infrared, etc. However, even while not visible, the wolf -can- be heard and smelled, should those methods be available to others. The werewolf will remain in this state until it drops the cover or attacks its prey. Attacks made while under the cover of Dispas are granted a +5 bonus for surprise. Werewolves under dispas cannot be targeted for attacks, as they cannot be seen. Werewolves also cannot target others for attack as that would be too strenuous. In this case, 'targeting' is the ability to use skills or masteries to enhance damage or hit a certain spot on someone. No Aiming, Rending, Sure Shot, etc. If a character has a Mastery that allows them to recognize hidden things (Heightened Awareness, Enhanced Senses, etc.) and they SAW the werewolf change into Dispas, they can roll their Mastery to 'see' where the werewolf is, provided the werewolf has not yet left the area.

Dulled Pain
1 Soul to use. (##Dulled Pain##)
Generation Cost: 10 / Experience Cost: 50
System/Duration: NA/Scene; takes 1 round to activate
Effect/Description: Due to the werewolf's innate healing system and mind set, its body is able to handle the stress of physical attacks better than any other creature. They are more resilient to the damage they can take before pain begins to affect their abilities. A werewolf with Dulled Pain ignores changes to their modifiers that would normally take effect through the loss of Body for the first 3 points of damage.

Extended Mindspeak
1 Soul to use. (as mindspeak)
Generation Cost: 5 / Experience Cost: 25
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs TN 20 / Scene; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: The werewolf has learned to seek others out by manipulating their natural ability: Mindspeak. This allows the werewolf to communicate via Mindspeak to a target within one mile. For every mile after the first, a modifier of +3 is added to the target number. The communication is only one way, unless the target also has the mastery: Extended Mindspeak. No roll is necessary unless the werewolf is in combat. If combat is initiated while this mastery is in effect, the werewolf must reactivate it due to the stress of combat.

Night Vision
1 Soul to use. (no room command needed)
Generation Cost: 5 / Experience Cost: 25
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: The werewolf can see twice as far as a normal human can. In addition, when the lighting is low, or near total darkness, the vision of the werewolf takes on the effects of infravision. Unable to make out detail, the werewolf can discern between animate and inanimate objects, sensing their red or blue glow, respectively.

Scent of the True Form
1 Soul to use. (##ScentTForm ##)
Generation Cost: 5 / Experience Cost: 25
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs target's 2d6+Will / Instant; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: The werewolves' olfactory senses are very sensitive, sometimes to the point where they pick up on the presence of supernatural beings. They will instantly know what the individual's breed is if they have Lore level 3 of that breed. The individual must IM the werewolf's player with the truth if the werewolf wins the roll. *Demons using Soul Mask, will reflect the breed being masked and not the nature of the Demon.

o00o Physical Description o00o
O O


Evan stands 6’2", and weighs in around 220 pounds. He is 21 years old. His skin is of a deep tanned color due to his Indian heritage. His hair is a dark black and runs past his shoulder to the middle of his back. Usually he will keep it banded together with a rubber band to keep it off his back in the heat of the south. His eyes are a straight brown but tend to turn a greenish tint, almost a hunter green, as anger sets in, as rage grows inside. Just recently he has gained a scar to the right side of his head and his ear. A large jagged outline, set in white scar tissue sits on the right side of his head from where a silver bullet ripped him open and blasted some of his brain apart. The top of his ear has been severed and is no longer there, along with another white scar tissue line running from the top where a small piece of his ear is missing about ½ in. down. On his legs there are a few other long straight scars that run the length of his leg. These he has put on himself with a silver blade of his own.
When taking on his wolf nature he stands taller and larger then many others. Standing 26 inches high, 34 inches long and weighing in at 160 pounds. His fur is of a tanned color; his underside is a misty white while his back has a smoky black cover.
When anger and rage get the best of him the beast rips free. When this happens he takes on the form of a beast. Depending on what form he was in before the shift he will either look more human or more wolf. When he has been angered in his human form he will shift into a large human like creature with some wolf like characteristics. When taking on this form he stands over 9 feet tall. His fur turns to an all black covering except for the top of his head. On the top of his head he has a white line that runs from between the eyes to the back of his neck, about ½ inches thick. His hands become strong powerful deadly claws that will swing at its prey without a second thought. His teeth are razor sharp and will rip into your flesh if given the chance. When he has been angered in his wolf form, he takes on a more wolf life form, with some human features. He continues to walk on his hind legs, his back arched more then the human like form. He can stand to his full upright position but can move with greater speed on all fours.

o00o Background o00o
O O


Born in the midwest of America Daniel grew up as a country boy. He and his brother Evan were born identical twins, his brother being the first born and always a tep ahead of Daniel in physical traits, mental abilities and parental love. Neither of the two attended pubilc schools past the age 10. Their family required their help on the small farm they live on. The Walkers were a Native American family. For generations their ancestors cultivated the land they now lived on. In one point in history the farm was the location of their tribes village.
Along with living on the land for so long the Walkers also possessed the spirit of the wolf within their life. The wolf inside had been past down for generations. All members of the house had the innate ability to transform into a wolf. Along with that their healing was remarkable. They were not invulnerable to everything, but they had a pretty good immunity to almost anything that might normally threatened them. At times of rage they would go off their own ways and let out their anger away from others. Their biggest fear was the ability that they could cause death within the blink of an eye.
Evan spent much of his life helping on the farm, sitting with his mother for small lessons on reading and writting, or hunting the wooded land around the houst. Daniel on the other hand enjoyed sneaking off to the near by towns and causing trouble when he could. He never really cared for his mothers lesson's or helping to much around the house. He did what was required and no more. Over time Daniel began to grow jealous of his brothers strength, intelligence, and closeness with their parents.
As with any one born wolf they never recieve their shifting abilities until they reach adolecsence. Evan and Daniel were no exception. Remarkably the two shifted for their first time on the same exact night. One of the shiftings was the cause of the other. Neither had ever experienced so much rage and hate releas in one day as they had that night.
All things seemed to point to tonight for their first shift. The night was a crisp clear night, very little clouds hung in the sky. The moon was full and showered the farm with a beautiful natural light. The twins had just turned 13 today and were now into their teen years. After the two had managed to get into the small usual brawl Daniel thundered off quite upset and angered over his brothers win once again. After only a few moments or the rapidly building rage it was all released in the blink of an eye. In one instant Daniel had gone into full horus. Picking up a scent he quickly followed it without any hesitation. His claws slashed at tree trunks and his teeth glimmered in the moonlight. Breaking free of the wooded are he rushed into a large clearing where a small fire was roaring. Within seconds blood curdling screams were being released as blood and flesh began to drip own his claws. The memebers of the small wolf pack dropped one by one to the ground in a mangled mess of rage. Once the whole lot of them had been left to nothing more then claw shreddings he took flight once more, vanishing into the night.
Evan heard the erie screams from off by the farm. Taking flgith fast he rushed out to find what had happened to cause such a cry. Once arriving in the clearing, the fire now dieing away slowly, he saw the forms of the massacred. Falling to the ground fast and hard he too snapped into a horused raging machine. The sight of his bloody and torn open parents haveing sent him over the edge.
Evan hunted his brothers for years, it took Daniels crimes to bring the two back together again. Finding himself falling from a cliff he knew the only reason for the scene that had gotten him into this mess could be cause of Daniel. From a distance Daniel managed to catch the whole scene as it unraveled. Once the body had slammed into the ground he made his way towards it, to see that it was who he had thought. Looking down over the mangled body of Evan he let out a low chuckle. Fleeing from the town quickly he began to use his brothers name where he visisted.
Since arriving in Volleta Daniel has worked his way into a pack and all the way to Beta before leaving it and wandering lone wolf once more. Not until he came to Voletta was he ever with a woman. Since here he has married once, then was shortly divorced, and dates only 2 others. Having nowfallen for his first made pup Skye Heart the two have started the beginning stages of forming a pack. Evan having not yet declared Skye as his official mate he is waiting for the unfolding of project Luna. What the future holds for Daniel can only be answered as his life is played out...
