Special Abilities



Mind Shield - 0 XP - 0 Soul (##MindShield##)
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs TN 18 / 4 rounds or 2 minutes; takes 1 round to activate
Effect/Description: It is difficult for a Psychic to keep their wits about them, and even more difficult when the creatures of the night try to penetrate their mind. By blocking everything out, they can prevent anyone from manipulating their mind, thus adding +3 to their defensive Will rolls. For the Psychic to keep this mastery in effect, they must continue to concentrate. This means no movement quicker than a slow walk, and no other
masteries may be performed. The Psychic can speak, but if physically attacked, the concentration is broken.

Mindspeak - 0 XP - 0 Soul (>>::target of Mindspeak:: message<<)
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: By concentrating on a chosen target, the psychic is capable of speaking directly to the creature's mind. Mortals are unable to return the thoughts. The target must be within view of the psychic attempting to communicate in this fashion. Mindspeak can only be used to speak to one individual at a time.

Meditation - 0 XP - 0 Soul (##Meditation##)
System/Duration: NA / NA; takes 30 minutes to activate
Effect/Description: In order to center themselves and replenish their mystical energies after spell casting, a psychic needs to shut themselves off from the world around them and meditate. By sitting alone and relaxing, concentrating on a candle flame, or some sort of talisman - perhaps chanting. They must meditate in the main room. If they are interrupted (legitimately) for even a moment, the meditation must begin anew. 1 Soul Point may
be regained each half-hour, going no higher than the original level.

Empathy - 0 XP - 0 Soul (##Empathy ##)
System/Duration: 2d6+Mind vs. TN 16 / Instant; takes 1 round to activate
Effect/Description: Psychics have learned to read the facial expressions and body language of others. These insightful individuals can occasionally pinpoint someone else's emotional state just by observing them for a few seconds. The victim must IM the mortal, describing their emotional state. Angry, aroused, excited, and distraught are only examples.


Masteries


Extended Mindspeak - 1 Soul to use. (as mindspeak)
Generation Cost: 5 / Experience Cost: 25
System/Duration: 2d6+Will vs TN 20 / Scene; takes 1 round to activate
Effect/Description: The psychic has learned to seek others out by manipulating their natural ability: Mindspeak. This allows the psychic to communicate via Mindspeak to a target within one mile. For every mile after the first, a modifier of +3 is added to the target number. The communication is only one way, unless the target also has the mastery: Extended Mindspeak. No roll is necessary unless the psychic is in combat. If combat is initiated while this mastery is in effect, the psychic must reactivate it due to the stress of combat.

Mental Blast - 1 Soul to use. (##MentalBlast ##)
Generation Cost: 15 / Experience Cost: 75
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs victim's 2d6+Will / Instant; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: A few Psychics have found another way to use mindspeak. By increasing the telepathic flow toward a subject, they can over load their mind. If this is kept up, or if it is powerful enough, then the target may eventually fall unconscious. This buildup causes intense feedback, like white noise dealing 5 points of Mind damage. Due to the great mental force and Psychic energy put into the attack, a Psychic may not perform any masteries the round following a Mental Blast. The victim must be within line of sight.


Physical Description



Roland Deschain is one big, impressive looking Rasta. Black, 6'3", 230#, with dreadlocks cascading down over his shoulders to mid-back. He wears a mustache and goatee, and is often dressed in jeans or fatigue pants with tant top or t-shirt. His Father's beloved guns, custom .45's, are always holstered at his hips. Heavy combat-type boots cover his feet, and occasionally, when the weather is cold, he can be found in a jacket as well.


Background

Roland Deschain was born in Trenchtown, Jamaica in November of 1972. He was born into a family of vampire hunters known as "The Gunslingers" who, throughout the ages, have protected humanity from the vampire threat. At the age of 19 he and his friend and fellow gunslinger Aurelianus Ambrosius were recruited by the Jamaican Intelligence Division for a special program. The Commonwealth of Jamaica and it's island neighbors formed a joint task force to control the growing vampire threat in the area. With the prevelance of the AIDS virus in the area and other concerns the task force was given the mission of eliminating the vampire population in the Carribean.
In 1997 Roland's partner, Aurelianus Ambrosius, came to Isle Voletta, Louisiana in search of a vampire family that he suspected murdered his wife Julia. Agent Ambrosius lived in Voletta for six months and sent regualr reports and photographs of his life there to Roland back in Jamaica. Agent Ambrosius died in a reactor accident at the Syntec Biomedical Corporation, a company he believed to be a front for the vampire family.
Nearly mad with grief at the loss of his oldest friend Roland requested permission to travel to Voletta to investigate his death. As the JID had no authority to place an agent on American soil Roland was placed on indefinate administrative leave and attached to the Jamaican Embassy as an associate consulate.
The Jamaican authorities have sent letters to the Voletta authorities informing them of Roland's mission and his diplomatic status and have requested whatever cooperation can be given to him. He has applied for and received the relevant permits for his weapons.
Roland has enough money to survive on his own for about a month and must then look for suitable employment to finance his investigation.

Mandatory Role-play Requirements

1) All Psychics have something which they focus their energy through. This could be anything from a lucky penny to a crystal. This item must be chosen at character creation. If this object is ever lost, stolen or destroyed, the psychic will temporarily loose all of their masteries and special abilities. A 48 hour meditation and attunement ritual is needed for the psychic dedicate themselves to a new
focus object. If the item is found or recovered, all of their abilities will immediately return.
2) Psychics are very superstitious, the character will react to items or signs which are considered to bring good or bad luck - i.e., a raven could symbolize a bad omen.
3) Once a character becomes a Psychic, they may never learn the arts of spell casting, as these two philosophies contradict each other heavily, even if the character should lose their Psychic ability permanently.
4) If the character ever becomes another type of breed, all Psychic ability is permanently lost.
