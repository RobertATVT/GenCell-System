Background



Background: Conviction was born as a good, healthy baby. With very religious parents and family members. He attended regular school, turning out as a higher than average student. Though never wanted to take any real action. He enjoy the more barbaric qualities of life. Mostly sports. But he read up on major wars and battles withen the battle or faith related. This affected him psychologicly. Believing a fist was the way to convert, to teach the ways of god. Though his parents denied of his violent and almost psychotic behavior as a teenager, and never took any action. He graduated with high honors and his GED in highschool and took away immediatly toward college. If not by choice, by force of his parents. When he was done, he returned home with his bachelors degree. His consiousness was growing more active and violent.. he eventually killed his father. Which, as he thought, was a message from God to pursue this action. He fled from his sin, toward the Isle of Sleepiness. Isle Volleta. "His destiny lies in this island", he dreamed. He is still searched of the murder, though he keeps low. During his stay at Volleta, he had strange occurancences with the "supernatural". One incident for example, he was on his way home at night when he heard of a old man in need. He thought he saw the old man despertly run his way into the woods, following him a large "bear". He didn't mind about this at first. But he got into closer situations, until he himself was close to death by one of these wolf/bears. He did not fully understand these creatures that he had the incredible luck of commonly seeing. He had a dream, to slay these creatures who stalk the innocent. To "lay them in blood on their own soil". He followed this dream, as by command of God himself.
