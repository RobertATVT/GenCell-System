Background

1977. The end of a turbulent time in American history. The beginning of Lauren M. Green.
Lauren Green was born in Wichita, Kansas to Karen and Elijah Green. Her parents has been flower children. They'd been to Woodstock, dropped acid and shot heroin. They'd driven around the country in a VW van. They'd made love under the stars, high on every drug they could get their hands on. And out of that had come Lauren and after that, her 5 brothers and sisters, Lucy, Larry, Linda, Laine and Lorne. L had meant loneliness in an ancient culture and each child symbolized a little less loneliness for the others.
Her parents had also managed to attend college. They retained mediocre jobs. Her father worked in an auto plant and her mother became a secretary. On the ouside, her family looked fairly normal, but on the inside, it was far from it.
Her parents had never actually kicked their drug habits and spent hours, drikning and partying. Lauren had raised her brothers and sister virtually on her own, also playing a nurse to her often drunk and high parents.
This went on until Lauren was 11. On night, one of 7 year old, Larry's teachers came by the house after failing to reach Mr. or Mrs. Green despite trying for 2 weeks. She discovered the house in a squalled state. 11-year-old Lauren cooking and helping her younger brothers and sisters with their homework, feeding 1-year-old Lorne. The police were phoned immediately and the children were sent to live with their childless aunt and uncle.
Within the courts raged a custody battle for the next 2 years and on the night before her 13th birthday, permanent custody of the Green children were awarded to her aunt and uncle, after her parents failed to complete their 4th and final round of treatment. After spending so much time in the presence of cops, Lauren decided to become one and entered college for a 2-year Criminal Justice program. Then, she entered a local police academy and spent the next 2 years completing there. Upon graduation, she heard of the Isle Voletta Police Department and decided to head down to check it out, despite the fact that she'd have to go through another round of training, she needed out. And out she went.
