Background


 Phillip Verne Thronnus was born in a huge castle on the border of Germany and Netherland near the north sea. His parents owed the castle for quite sometime because the house was passed down from generation to generation. His family is one of the last royality that still lives in Germany and it was hard for him to grow up there. His mother was always trying to make his life as easy going as possible while his father ran the farm land and things of that kind. Phillip worked hard to make something of his self, even though he had a royal rank...he still continued talking to anyone that was willing. He was well liked by everyone in the village and in the surround areas. As time went on, he continued to grow in his education and soon had to leave his home to study in Berlin, over a 1000 miles away from his home. With great sorrow and disappointment he left home to head to a college in Berlin. He worked extremely hard trying to get the best grades and some of the college's great achievements. He graduated from college at the age of 25 and soon was sent to America to finish learning some new ideas of medicine from a ER in New Orleans. After training for a long while he finally got the highest degree he could get here in america....so he decided at the age of 29 to head to a small town known as Isle Voletta, hearing that they are in need of some good doctors there. He arrives here in Isle Voletta unsure what he will be coming upon, but with all his being and so much more..... he sure has one hell of time ahead.
