Special Abilities


Healing - 0 Soul to use. (no room command needed)
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: Werewolves have an incredible natural healing ability. Any wound caused to the werewolf will heal at the rate of 1 Body per hour the character rests. If the character takes any strenuous action, the healing is disrupted and the hour starts over. A Room Host must be notified when you claim your Body point back. Wounds created by bane water cannot be healed with this mastery. Wounds from fire heal at the rate of 1 Body per day and only when the character sleeps. If the character is woken up before the day is over, the healing process is disrupted and they do not regain their point (which means they cannot role-play until the damage is healed). The damage from an attack that removes a limb heals at the normal rate, but the player must role-play the limb slowly growing back. (Length of time for the limb to regenerate is determined by the Forum Director.)

Horus Form - 0 Soul to use. (##HorusForm ##)
System/Duration: NA/Until target(s) is destroyed; takes 1 round to shape shift
Effect/Description: Tall, muscular, and extremely strong, this raging beast is terror incarnate. The massive head of a deranged wolf sits upon its massive shoulders. Thick matted fur covers its body as four inch claws tip the fingers on their enormous hands. Their eyes are slits like animals, but still retain their human color. Their reasoning is that of a maddened beast; pure instinct. Impervious to logic, they seek only to destroy the target of their fury . It will go to whatever means necessary to kill the target of its rage, whether it is loved one or foe. Once the target has been killed, reason begins to fight its way back. How the Horus reacts depends on the individual it is. Some flee while others fall to their knees sobbing. While in Horus form, the werewolf (currently) gains +3 Body (if the rules of the curse are not adhered to, we will change this bonus to +1). Due to its enraged state, the Horus is immune to the following masteries: Terror, Hypnotic Suggestion, Mind Scrye, Illusions, and Twisted Visions. Since the Horus is unable to think rationally, it may only use the following masteries: Dulled Pain, Rending, Agile Wolf, and Terror.

Wolf Form - 0 Soul to use. (##WolfForm##)

While in wolf form, they gain +1 Body.

(Just a quick note about shape changing (all forms). While a werewolf is in another form, if its current Body falls to 0, the werewolf will immediately change back to mortal form minus the damage it has already taken, leaving a minimum of 1 Body. It should also be noted that materiel objects do not shapeshift with the character. Any and all clothes and objects the character was holding will need to be transported by some other means.)

Background

Born to Norma and Maxwell Kingston in the Fall of 1976, Gabriel Kinston was a gift child. His mother thought barren and his father on the verge of adoption, Gabriel has come without warning or expectaion. Filled with joy his parents named him after Gods most favored and beloved angel Gabriel, his voice and courier thought the heavens. Gabriel was a beautiful child with a peaceful temperment and seemed a child of miracles. His eyes spoke volumes of mystery and past, and his talents lied in both leadership and the arts. In church his voice carried high and wonderous, and at schoold he excelled in all of his subjects. By the time Gabriel was in High School he was an up and coming sports star, most likely to succeed. He led his fellow youth in prayer and conviction, and spent his life studying his faith in god and his obsession with angels. By the time he graduated he was lined up for scholarships for everything from his sports to his grades, his community achievements and the things he did in the private sector to help out those less fortunate. All around Gabriel had been a wonder to his parents, to those whose life he touched. One day however his faith would be put to test. When he had turned 21 Gabriel had decided to go to New Orleans during Mardi Gras to spread the word of God to the lost, it was a pilgramage of sorts for him, a calling. He didn't know why Mardi Gras, he didin't know where, he just knew to go there. And he did. The realization of the decadence and horrors that he saw drove him crying into the night, lost in the small winding backroads of New Orleans. And he fell and cried to the heavens in anguish, afraid that some dark seed was to be opened in him. And he was lost, and afraid for the first real time in his life, hunkered down in the filth of all that is Mardi Gras. But a light shined on him, and slowly he looked up, expecting to see a policeman to cart him off to the drunk tank. This light however came to him in a warmth of love and care, this was the light of heaven shed from the wings on an angel. She looked down upon him and touched the sweaty and filth covered crown of his head, and her voice rang forth like the silver drops of a waterfall. "Stand Gabriel, you must be strong... you must not fear those that are to be saved, their iniquity shall not touch you. You are a follower of the light, and must go forth into the darkest corners carried by your own faith and guided by your inner strength. Now go and free them." And suddenly she was gone... his Angel. all that was left was an ever warm, ever glowing feather that fell from her wings... perhaps left to give him strength. And go he did, and to Voletta he has come.
