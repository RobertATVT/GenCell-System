Background

Background:

Amiee Renee Glass was born in the rural area outside of Burlington, Vermont. Raised by loving parents of the Wicca religion, she had two brothers and one sister (Raymond, Jacob, and Sarah, respectively). Childhood was a merry time for all, and though her family was a bit different, they were happy.

All of her siblings were much older than she, and by the time Amiee was 14 years old the only one left living in the household was Sarah. It was nice, at times, to have someone around the same age to talk to when high school started. Her family's beliefs and practices didn't win her much popularity. But when Sarah left for college in Amiee's Junior year, things went haywire.

In order to 'fit in', Amiee rebelled against her parents and all that they stood for. This, of course, was heartbreaking to them. To see their little daughter go through so much...the drugs, the late nights, the numerous 'boyfriends'. Amiee did graduate, but it certainly wasn't easy. It was about that time that she started getting things straight. Almost too late.

After taking a break for about a year to live with her parents and get things back together again, she went to college. In order not to have to deal with a lot of flack about her upbringing, she threw herself into her studies and graduated with all A's and B's. A short time after, she ended up working for a large security company developing their computer software.

One thing remained the same, however. Ever since regaining her dignity shortly after high school, she decided never to get involved with men (or boys) again. They had been nothing but trouble in the past, and she doubted they would ever be anything else.

Still, she wanted to get out of Vermont. ANYPLACE was better than Vermont. She quit her job and took a small-time position in Baton Rouge, LA. She's currently looking for a place to reside in Volletta...
