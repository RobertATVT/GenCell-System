Background

Background: Daniele grew up just outside of Genoa, Italy with his parents, Eduardo and Marcella, a pair of practicing Lamia. He was always vaguely aware that his parents were different from those of his friends, but they did not think him ready to begin learning. They were also practicing Catholics and faithfully attended St. Matteo's near their home. He showed a natural affinity to music at a young age and his parents, knowing that the sooner he began the better he would be, set him up with violin lessons after his third birthday. He hoped to attend the Music School at the Università di Genova while his parents wanted him to move farther away to a more prestigious school.

The point became moot when they were visiting relatives in a backwards rural area of northwestern Italy. Marcella and Eduardo instinctively used their power to fight a fire at the local church of St. Luca's. Despite the use to which they put their talents, the locals accused them of starting the fire in the first place. They were lynched and did not receive Last Rites nor a proper burial, but Daniele was not given time to do anything about it. His cousin Gianni immediately took him back to his home near Genoa to escape any guilt by association. The family decided it was best to get him out of the country. His family home was to be taken care of by his relatives while he was packed off to stay with a cousin in America.
