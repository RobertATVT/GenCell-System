Special Abilities


Empathy - 0 Soul to use. (##Empathy ##)
System/Duration: 2d6+Mind vs. TN 16 / Instant; takes 1 round to activate
Effect/Description: Psychics have learned to read the facial expressions and body language of others. These insightful individuals can occasionally pinpoint someone else's emotional state just by observing them for a few seconds. The victim must IM the mortal, describing their emotional state. Angry, aroused, excited, and distraught are only examples.

Meditation - 0 Soul to use. (##Meditation##)
System/Duration: NA / NA; takes 30 minutes to activate
Effect/Description: In order to center themselves and replenish their mental energies after utilizing the power of the mind, a psychic needs to shut themselves off from the world around them and meditate. By sitting alone and relaxing, concentrating on a candle flame, or some sort of talisman while chanting, the psychic can slowly restore their soul. They must meditate in the main room. If they are interrupted (legitimately) for even a moment, the meditation must begin anew. 1 Soul Point may be regained each half-hour, going no higher than the original level.

Mind Shield - 0 Soul to use. (##MindShield##)
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs TN 18 / 4 rounds or 2 minutes; takes 1 round to activate
Effect/Description: It is difficult for a Psychic to keep their wits about them, and even more difficult when outside forces try to penetrate their mind. By blocking everything out, they can prevent anyone from manipulating their mind, thus adding +3 to their defensive Will rolls. For the Psychic to keep this mastery in effect, they must continue to concentrate. This means no movement quicker than a slow walk, and no other masteries may be performed. The Psychic can speak, but if physically attacked, the concentration is broken.

Mindspeak - 0 Soul to use. (>>::target of Mindspeak:: message<<)
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: By concentrating on a chosen target, the psychic is capable of speaking directly to the creature's mind. Mortals are unable to return the thoughts. The target must be within view of the psychic attempting to communicate in this fashion. Mindspeak can only be used to speak to one individual at a time.

Masteries

1) Pyrokinesis - 1 Soul to use. (##Pyro ##)
Generation Cost: 20 / Experience Cost: 100
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs TN 26 / 4 rounds or 2 minutes; takes 2 rounds to activate
Effect/Description: By focusing his energies on a specific object, the Psychic is able to manipulate it on an atomic level. Through this practice, the Psychic can cause the atoms of nonliving objects to speed up at an amazing speed, generating intense heat, and causes a highly combustible material (i.e. gasoline) to catch fire. The target need only be within sight to use this mastery. The object will catch fire, or begin to turn to cinder, or melt. If metal is heated, it will cause 2 points of Body damage to anyone who comes in contact with it. After 2 rounds after the object was heated, the metal can be touched again without harm. The material must be stated prior to this mastery being used. Any material may be used, however, there must be a direct line of sight to the material. You would not be able to set a gas tank ablaze, simply because you cannot see the gas. Bullets (if the gun itself is heated) or other explosives heated in this manner will explode causing the normal damage +2 to anyone holding the item.

2)Vertigo - 1 Soul to use. (##Vertigo ##)
Generation Cost: 10 / Experience Cost: 50
System/Duration: 2d6+Will vs victim's 2d6+Will / 4 rounds or 2 minutes; takes 1 round to activate
Effect/Description: The Psychic sends out mental impulses that disrupt the equilibrium of a target, throwing them off balance and rendering them unable to concentrate. While under the dizzying effects of Vertigo, movement for the victim is slowed and difficult. The victim cannot use any masteries which require any amount of concentration (mental masteries). The victim receives a penalty of -4 to all physical rolls. Eye contact must be established in order for this mastery to take effect, though once the victim is affected eye contact is no longer needed to continue the effect. The Psychic must continue to concentrate to keep this mastery in effect. They may not do anything other than move at a normal, walking pace, they may not enact any other masteries, nor can they speak. If they do, the effects of this mastery immediately end.

3)Accelerated Healing - 1 Soul to use. (##AccHealing##)
Generation cost: 10 / Experience Cost: 50
System/Duration: 2d6+Body+Basic Medical Skill vs TN 22 / Ends play for the night
Effect/Description: Some Psychics learn to control their bodies through extensive training of the mind by entering a trance like state. They have learned how to increase the body's metabolism, thus causing them to heal at a quicker rate. For every point above 18, 1 point of body is regained. This cannot exceed the psychics max body score. Due to the draining effect of this mastery, and the state the Psychic must enter, this must be the last thing the Psychic does for the night. If the Psychic is interrupted in ANY way, then the effects for the night are lost, and the mastery cannot be performed again until the following night. A room host must be present for this mastery. The TN is 18 if used in conjunction with the Major Medical Skill

4)Laying On Hands (requires Accelerated Healing) - 1 Soul to use. (##LOHealing ##)
Generation Cost: 10 / Experience Cost: 50
System/Duration: 2d6+Will+Basic Medical Skill vs TN 24 / Instant; takes 2 rounds per point healed to use this mastery.
Effect/Description: The Psychic ability to heal through the placement of hands over the injured part of the body. The hands usually grow very warm. In some instances, the healer exhibits the wounds or symptoms of the disease or wound, until they sleep it off. This mastery can be used to heal wounds during combat, however, the player's actions for the necessary number of rounds (see above) is the "laying on hands". If successful, the target will regain 1 Body point per point over 24. For every 2 points the target receives, the Psychic will receive 1 Mind damage as the healing drains his mental faculties. This mastery can only be used on another person, the Psychic cannot perform it on themselves. If the Psychic takes another action during combat, before duration is over, healing has stopped. The TN is 20 if used with the Major Medical Skill.
