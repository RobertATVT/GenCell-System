Background


She was a straight "A" popular student in St.Louis, Missouri. She was loved by everybody and had almost a perfect life until her older sister Jeannette left. Nisa was greatly disturbed by this but kept going on with her life. After her senior year she decided she wanted to major in dance and modeling. Nisa's parents told her what she wanted to do and that was become a doctor. She had earned a scholorship to go to Harvard, but she turned it down. She finally realized why her sister left, she was being cruched under the supression of their parents. Nisa wanted to get on her own and see how she would do alone. Nisa told her parents that she'd go live with her sister in Louisianna and study becomming a doctor. Instead Nisa studied what she wanted, choreography. Nisa is a successful drummer, violin player, basketball player, and choreographer. Nisa left home to prove she wasn't a little girl anymore. When she came to Voletta she was a quite girl. She started to meet people and became well aquanted with Ty Landers. She made Ty her role-model and wanted to be like him, her finding out he was a wolf made her want to become a wolf. She met a new person named Roma and Roma made Nisa a wolf. Nisa and her sister Jeannette decided to start a family and slowly made their name in the bayou. Nisa was Beta of the Knight family and alpha of the Knight wolves. Nisa had to leave for college in another state and came back to find out Jeannette was dead. After Jeannette's death, and many other Knights took place, it had a major impact on Nisa and made her feel obligated to start acting like an adult and take charge of her life. Nisa had been through rehab for drugs and alcholism and has successfuly overcome her problem. She has become a very well known dancer for music videos, but the death of her sister and neice has changed her. She usually walks around town after college until late night and sometimes stop at the bar. She's lost and looking for help, but doesn't know where to look.
