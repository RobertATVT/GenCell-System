Physical Description


Crimson tendrils of silk--more like blood than liquid fire--lap over rounded shoulders to fall in a glimmering cascade to curved hips. A delicate, heart-shaped face sets off lazy, sleepy-lidded eyes of purest topaz gold, a lush crimson mouth, a tiny nose straight as an arrow, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. Del can usually be found wearing jeans and a variety of black t-shirts with different logos (NiN, X-Files, etc.) She smells of patchouli, cloves, and cinnamon, and moves with the lazy, sleepy grace of a panther that has just fed.


Background


 Delilah's parents were hippie holdovers from the '60's at San Francisco. They tuned in, turned on, and dropped out...until the late '70's and early '80's, that is, when they cleaned up their act, finished college, and became drug researchers for Eli Lilly. They'd had plenty of experience, after all. Delilah, or Del, as she prefers to be called, grew up the spoiled little rich kid by the time she hit kindergarten. For Del, money meant the time to do whatever she wanted and a near-total lack of responsibilities...as well as all the books she could get her hands on. She made excellent grades through school, more through natural talent than any real effort, and won a scholarship to Tulane University in New Orleans when she was 18. She attended for three years, majoring in English, and dabbled a bit in poetry, publishing some pieces in small, prestigious magazines around the country, but school was too much like work for her, and she dropped out in her last semester. The next year and a half were spent in the French Quarter, smoking dope and drinking too much espresso with her friends, a clique of mixed goths and writers and musicians there before someone told her about this weird little town called Isle Voletta. Curious, bored, with too much time and money on her hands, she decided to take a trip down to see what the town had to offer.
