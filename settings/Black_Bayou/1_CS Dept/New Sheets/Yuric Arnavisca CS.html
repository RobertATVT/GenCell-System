Background

Born in beautiful Los Angeles, California, Yuric was originally from a high class society of people. His father was the Chief Executive Officer of a major corporation while his mother was the Vice President of the same company. Yuri was a decent student, and seemed like he was on the right track. Though he was sent to a rich private school, rather than the public high school, he always seemed to get into trouble. He fought constantly with the other students, smoked on campus, and was eventually caught dealing illegal drugs, heroin and marijuana. He stayed out late at night, usually "borrowing" one of his father's cars and driving into the city. It was at this point in his life in which he made contact with the gangs, though he was first viewed as an outsider, he was eventually accepted on the grounds that he had access to large amounts of cash, funds necessary to fuel the gang's "propositions". After getting busted on drug charges by the cops, Yuric's father decided to straighten out his son and pulled him out of school and put him to work at the company. He underwent what seemed like a total change, he got into no more trouble, was home on time, everything was back to normal. But it wasn't, he'd faked the whole thing, once he'd earned there trust he hacked into the company account and embezzled over $200,000. With a steady cash flow, he earned a high spot in the gang. Finally, at age 24, his past caught up with him and he was sentenced to 10 years in prison. He returns to the gang to find it disbanded and reformed under new leadership. With the change, he is put back at the bottom, though he deserves more from the gang. He is sent to Voletta to kill a rogue member, though a part nags at him, about getting back at the new leaders and claiming his rightful place in the gang.
