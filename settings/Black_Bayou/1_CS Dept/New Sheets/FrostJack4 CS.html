Background

Jack was born to parents that were werewolves, in a small town in Nebraska. When he was still an infant his parents gave him up for adoption early one due to the fact that they felt they were too scared for what may come of his birth. So he was given up for adoption and placed in a foster home with lots of other kids who had been given up for adoption. Early one Jack exhibited traits of his werewolf status. He seemed to have more strength then the other boys, and a much more agressive nature. When he was around the age of 9 he was adopted by a nice couple, the Frosts. He was soon taken out to a Farm where he had many blissful years up until the age of 18 when his first transformation occured. He ended up killing both of his foster parents in a blind rage brought on by the beast within him. Horrified by what he had done he ran. He ran not understanding what had happened back there. By the time he was 22 he had learned how to control his temper much better and learned how to store it and release it only when he needed to, almost like a rain check. He drifted around from place to place. When he was about 23 he began learning the Art form known only to his own breed. He quickly learned upon it and became exceptionally good at it. He was around the age of 28 when he stumbled upon Volletta and its strange mythical prescence. Deciding this place may hold some answers he ambled on in and decided to see what this place was all about.
