Background



Isaac Levine, born in 1968, was raised in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, for the majority of his early years. A surprise to his parents, a frequently unemployed drunk and a waitress, he grew up in a home of mixed emotions. With the death of a younger brother, divorce soon followed and a move south to Baton Rouge was made with his mother at the age of thirteen. He was sucessful in high school, earning high marks and athletic honors, but problems with friends and long time girlfriend Laura Delmonty would find him favoring alcohol as a cure all to his problems. Years and the suicide of a close friend only made the problem worse for him and not even a seemingly happy engagement in the August of 1989 seemed to help for long. Later that year, problems with work and his subsequent dismissal were the last straw. In November he was hospitalized with severe, though non life-threatening injuries, after a tragic car accident in which five innocent people were killed when he attempted to drive home one night from a bar. Found guilty of vehicular manslaughter and DWI, he spent 8 long years in jail, a time in his life which, oddly enough, brought some clarity to his mind. Now sobered up, or so it seems, and free at long last, he comes to Voletta to stay to find work in the attempts of regaining his life and the love of his ex-fiance.
