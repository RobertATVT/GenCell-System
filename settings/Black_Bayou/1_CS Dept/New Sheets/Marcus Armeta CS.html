Background

Mark was born in Atlanta...he's not sure when since he was kidnapped right out of the hospital by a male nurse. It seems the guy wanted a child of his own...since he had a habit of not being able to keep his women alive. They just bled so much. Anyway...the man took Mark to his studio apartment in downtown Atlanta...which contained nothing but a couch and a broken crib. When Mark learned to walk, his "father" put razor wire of the top of the crib to keep him in.

As Mark grew, he saw "daddy" bring more and more women home...and watched every horrid thing he did to them, not really realizing that it was wrong. When Mark was too big for the crib, his father got him a mattress, but still used the razor wire to keep him in it.

One night, when Mark was 13, "pops" was busying doing his carving and Mark had had enough. He sat up through the wire, not making a sound, barely feeling it at all as he had all of his life. He took the wire and walked over to his father and decapitated him with the wire. The woman was still alive...looking at Mark thankfully. Mark just looked at her stoicly and proceeded to whip her to death with the razor wire.

He then took to the streets and lived on a corner. One day, a missionary approached him offering help. Mark pulled the hidden razor wire from under his shirt and beat the man with it in broad daylight. He was arrested and put into an asylum. He was released at age 18, faking being rehabilitated. He was good at that sort of thing.

After being released, he immediately returned to the old apartment to find someone else living there. He gave them some sappy sob story about how he grew up there to get himself in. He gained entrance...and when the new tenant's back was turned, he wrapped an arm around his throat and snapped his neck. He found the panel where his dad kept his extra toys. A curved switchblade and another length of wire in case the other got too dull. Mark then went on a long walk west.

When he arrived in Isle Voletta, he met a woman named Celene deep in the woods who captured his heart...by offering to have sex with him in return for him letting her live. This convinced him to stay. One day, Celene was gone. No note. Nothing. Poof. He shrugged it off and took over her little shack for his own.
