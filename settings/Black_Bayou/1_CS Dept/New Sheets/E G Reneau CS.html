Physical Description

Maturing into a very attractive woman, she finds little need to taint her face with makeup. Wearing only a thin layer of gloss upon her bow tie lips, she retains a natural blush and her pale azure eyes need no enhancement.. they are disarming. A tiny sprinkling of cinnamon freckles dot the bridge of her nose and the apples of her cheeks. Long, thick, chestnut hair hangs straight, with one wave that tends to fall side long into her features. She often wears it back in braids or a pony tail to shows off the unmarred and fair features of her face. Most comfortable in fitted jeans, bare feet or work boots, and halter tops, denim jackets or plaid. She moves with spirit and an undeniable femininity. She is non judgmental, strong willed, stubborn, devoutly kind and quickly urged into genuine laughter.

Eve, as she likes to be called, has one obvious quirk is her love of junk food, twinkies or pretzels or jelly beans.. whatever she can munch on. Oddly enough she is a mere 5'2 and only 92 lbs. Small hands and feet and plenty of wit.

Background


Evening Grace Reneau was born 25 years ago, on the last day of April. The firstborn child of a humble, and middle class couple in a small community in Peecavo, Idaho. Shortly after her birth, her parents were involved in a fatal car accident in a sudden, and serious snow storm. Left in the care of her only living relative, she was reared by her very religious and kind Grandmother, Rose, in a small farm house in the country. Helping out as best she could, and spending much of her time in the company of her aging and widowed mother figure, she learned empathy and hard work. Gaining a tomboyish air, she declined the life of a teenage socialite and enjoyed a much simpler existence within the flowing grains, and crystalline creek waters that ran through their property.

A four year degree in Journalism from Boise State University, aided her in landing an editing job, which she does on her laptop and scanner. The type of career that can be done at any location, and sent to the publishers via overnight mail . Born with a gift for music; she sings like an angel, plays the piano and her Grandfather's fiddle with the vivacity of the ages. She carries a pearl handled ruger in the glove box of her jeep for security; licensed and trained to use.

After her Grandmother's final passing, she packed up a singular duffle bag of items. Including a locket with a snap shot of her parents inside, her fiddle, and Grammies wedding band- she placed it along with the office equipment in the passenger side seat of her White, 1990 Jeep Wrangler. Tossing a dart at a map of the United States, she marked it, highlighted the highways and byways, and filled up the gas tank. One sighing glance back to the recently sold farm she drove away and never planned to look back. A sum of money in her pocket, to help her settle in her new destination. Top down, and lively tunes accompanying, she began her exciting journey to the Isle of Voletta, Louisiana. On the way she was not discouraged by a flat tire. Grateful for the readily offered help from the undeniably handsome gentleman, she was completely unaware of what plans he had in mind for her; seemingly endless abuse and bondage. Months later he released her, and though she had no memory now of who she was, and having no ID and only a few hundred dollar bills in her sweatshirt pocked, she once again finds herself with one name on her mind. Voletta.
