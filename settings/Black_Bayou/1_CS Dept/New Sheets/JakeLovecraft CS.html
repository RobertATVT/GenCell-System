Physical Description


 Jake is a shortish man looking to be about five years older than his twenty eight years due to his short cropped white hair. He bears the breeding marks of his pure German heritage - Square jawline, pale eyes, and hawkish nose. He dresses in good, though not ultra expensive, suits but has horrendous taste in ties(Hula dancers, clashing colors, etc...).


Background


Born to a second generation German family, and fourth of four boys, Jake was constantly in trouble to gain attention. His friends and teachers learned early to be wary when that irrepressable evil smirk came to Jake's lips. Though this tendency could have led to a life as a career felon for the young man, it was actually his saving grace. At the age of thirteen Jake got caught shoplifting by an off duty police officer by the name of Gordon James. Gordon felt an instant link for the boy, and made him his special project.
Jake finished high school after getting his life under control and applied to the police academy - and failed miserably. Not to be deterred, he took a correspondence course in the fine art of private investigation. Six months later, PI license in hand Jake went into business for himself. He quickly earned a name for himself as taking any and all jobs no matter how sleazy. It was on one of these jobs that he had his first run in with the supernatural - and earned his first shock of white hair. After that he spent his career taking cases that dealt with the supernatural. Debunking the false cases, and looking for answers in those that proved to be real.
Which brings us to the present. Taking a runaway case on for a friend he finds all the trails lead to a small town in Louisana. One with a particularly large incidence of missing persons and runaways.
