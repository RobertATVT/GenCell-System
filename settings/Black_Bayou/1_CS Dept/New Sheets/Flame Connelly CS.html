She was raised in a very strict, military fashion, so she is well versed in disapline and control. After High School, she joined the U.S. Army ( following in her fathers footsteps, so to speak) and took her college courses while serving. While serving, she became very well trained in Special Ops. and was soon asked to join the elite force of Green Berets, where she became an Lt. of her squad. On her last mission, Flame was wounded and due to her injuries, given an Honorable Discharge.
