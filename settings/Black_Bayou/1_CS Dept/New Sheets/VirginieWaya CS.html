Background




Virginia Andree` Waya was born in a cabin deep within the forest of Washington. Her mother was Isabelle Vanhorn a french actress that came down from Canada because she fell in love with her father BlueSky Waya. They builts the cabin to hide away from the world, plus it brought her father peace to be in the world his ancestors once lived. They were married two years before Virginia was born, but during childbirth a few problems occured. By the time Virginia was presented to the world her mother was already dead because during the child birth her mother tore something and she died from blood lose. Her mother was buried near the beautiful cherry blossom tree- that her father planted when the house was first built. The tree was still terribly small , but that was the perfect place for her to rest in peace. So Virginia was left with her father, the only living relative left in her life. He showed her everything about her Native American background and taught her how to listen to nature. He showed me how to hunt for food, though I should only take what I need to survive with. With each passing day she began to grow and look more and more beautiful. When she finally reached the age of 21 her father became very ill, due to a certain kind of cancer. She tired everything to make him go to a hosptial, but her father told her that she must not worry about him, that he shall soon be with her mother. Within a week he died and was placed near the huge cherry blossom that he planted over 21 years ago. She lived in the cabin for most the following year and continued the nature walks that she used to do all the time with her father. But one day as she was walking through the forest a man came into view- one she had never seen before. She moved through the woods following him like a mountain lion as he seemed to move to her place. She moved into the path just ahead of the house as the man comes to a dead hault infront of him.

She soon found out that the man that came to her house was a near by neighbor coming to give his sympathy to her. As the days passed they seemed to be seeing one another more and more. By the end of spring he was going to leave and the feelings that were between them were extremely strong. He had to go away to a college in New Orleans, there he would start to work with his father and go to college. So the day before he was suppose to leave they had a beautiful dinner out in the forest near lake indawin. When he brought her to the clearing a huge fire was a blaze and the blanket was set off to one corner but had no food upon it. She was so utterly confused as to why the blanket was set out upon the ground and where the dinner was. But soon she heard chanting as she glanced back over at her love- his shirt flung to the side as he started to paint symbols along his chest. He danced around the camp fire the pantings smeared upon his body as I just watch him, moving back from the area. A howl is heard from his lips as his body started to change in a man wolf- something my father use to tell me, but I always thought were myths. His clothes shredded infront of me as a scream echoes from my lips. I start to run but before I get too far the beast was upon me tearing up my back. I soon passed out into a a deep hole not knowing exactly what happen.

The next morning I awoke to the rays of light flitering through the trees, with his body next to mine curled naked in a ball. I touch my back coming away with blood as I try to hold back the tears. I stand slowly trying to get as far away from him as possible afraid of what he was or yet what he did to me. When I got home I got what few objects that I held dear and wrapped my back up so that I could manage not to go to the hospital. Then with the few objects I headed off to the main highway to hitch hike myself out of this place.
I hitched my way across the many states holding dearly to what little money I had, as I finally arrived in a small town. I read the sign reading Isle Voletta as we entered the small town. I shrugged to myself my back almost healed as I entered the town - this town is as good as any...
