Background

James was born in New York in 1974. He lived a comfortable and quite normal life until he was 18. One mid-winter night in 1992, James found himself in the wrong place at the wrong time. He had been snooping around where he shouldn't have been, in an old warehouse that he'd thought to be abandoned. As it turned out, there were people there--two gangsters and a woman. The woman was tied to a chair, and the two men didn't seem to be her friends. They both had guns, and though he knew better, he couldn't just stand by and watch it happen. He stayed hidden behind several old, empty crates, grabbed a rock from the floor, and threw it across the room. Both men looked up, and one instructed the other to go check out whatever the noise was. One of them turned to walk the other way, and James took out his 9mm. He aimed carefully, and put down the one who'd walked away. The other walked toward his wounded friend, with his gun drawn, and James shot him, too. He rescued the woman, called the police, and quickly left town.
He lived the next few years of his life in New Orleans, living a fairly normal life. He met and fell in love with a woman named Bella, and then with her sister. Then he met yet another woman when he turned 24. And then his life got really crazy.
The woman's name was Kayla. She was a werewolf. James learned all about werewolves from her. About keeping them away, about killing them, about how their true form came out during times of extreme agitation and during a full moon. And then he learned about the packs. There was terrible competition among the packs. They were constantly at war with each other. This seemed all well and good to James until he saw the destruction that could be caused by these wars. Innocent people could be caught in the way. As Kayla's lover, James was in danger himself. Lives could be lost, and the property damage was devestating. When Kayla was murdered by another wolf, he swore he'd had enough. He loaded his gun with silver bullets, and began to slay as many wolves as he could. And when he thought he'd done enough there, he decided to go to a town Kayla had spoken of where other wolves could be found. And that is when he came to Isle Voletta.
