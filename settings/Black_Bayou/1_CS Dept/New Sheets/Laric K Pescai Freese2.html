Background:
Laric (named for his grandfather) was orphaned at the age of seven when his parents were killed by a drunk driver. His uncle, a small town preacher from Macon, Georgia took him in out of respect for the boys father. Laric, confused by his recent loss, sought answers to his multitude of questions in the Holy Bible. He soon was convinced the entire ordeal was God guiding him into the ministry and accepted the task at hand with no further thought. 16 years later, Laric graduated from Bob Jones University in Greenville, South Carolina and answered the Lords call for a man of faith in the hamlet of Voletta, Louisianna. Today, he stepped off a bus onto Bayou Coast Parkway and felt as if his soul cried out for mercy. Now he is more determined than ever to discover what exactly is so interesting in Voletta that it got Gods attention.
