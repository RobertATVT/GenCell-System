Physical Description

Hunter comes into the bayou with dirty, stuck up hair, resembling a homeless man. He has a brown trenchcoat and black pants which go down to his boots that cover the rest of his leg. He is well-built through his travels and has a more grizzled appearance than most.
He walks through the streets wearing a kevlar vest and has a katana strapped to his back while a well hidden wooden stake is at his hip.


Background
 Hunter Steele was born in a small village off the West coast of Canada, where he had a standard childhood. He had a mother who was a kindergarten teacher and a father who was a police officer. This should have gave him a reasonable life, but it didn't end up that way. When Hunter was fourteen he was arrested for breaking and entering into a fellow students home. This made his parents furious and made him go to a private school for two years. Over those years, Hunter got into selling drugs to the students and made a lot of money over those years. The only problem was that his father was a cop so they were on the appeasing sides of the law. Hunter had made a lot of money, but one night Hunter's father was supposed to make a drug bust on a big dealer, this happened to be Hunter, and they had a shootout. Hunter had shot and killed his father. He was eighteen. Having no place to run he had found his cousin, Rick, who was an assassin, he had made a lot of money with hunting down people wanted by big companies, so Hunter wanted in o the deal. Rick had taken him in, but knew that he was in high risk of being caught. Over that one year that they were training Hunter got skilled at the katana and learned about many of the supposed myths that ended up being true.
Six months before Hunter's 20th birthday Rick had gotten word about a big money deal in a swampland that they never heard of. But they decided to go. Soon enough, they were knee deep in Vampire County, this is where Hunter had lost Rick. They were traveling deep in the marshes when out of nowhere comes a hungry vampire. The vampire had jumped upon Rick and bit him in the neck. As he was feeding off of Rick's lifestream Hunter had tried to get him off, but it was to late. As the vampire ran off with Rick he could here his cousin say, "Sadly.... I'll see you again..." Hunter never thought in one million years that he would ever come head-to-head with a real vampire or even lose his cousin to one, but after that night Hunter had ran off into the depths of the marshes until he reached his destination... The Black Bayou.
