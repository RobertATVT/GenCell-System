Physical Description

A middle-aged man, looking quite good for his age due to his military history. His hair is short and brown, his eyes are blue. He's 6'1" and around 200 lbs.


Background

Sean was born in Memphis, Tennessee, in 1975. He married Elise Monroe, a girl he'd known for only three months, directly out of high school. They lived together in New York until 1989, when Sean decided he wanted to join the military. He signed up for two years of service, putting him in there just long enough to be shipped off to the gulf. He faught in the war, came home in 1993, and lived for just over 4 more years with Elise before the event which forever changed his life occured.

One night, the night before Independence Day, while Sean and Elise were minding their own business in their apartment, three gothic teenagers broke in. Wearing what Sean thought to be, at the time, fake fangs and white make-up, they attacked in a vampiric fashion, biting both him and his wife. A hardened war hero, Sean wasn't about to let three punk kids get the better of him, so he battled them like they were Arabs with machine guns. Only this fight, much different from those with the Arabs, he lost.

He woke up in the hospital on the fifth of July, feeling sick and weak. He had lost alot of blood, but they'd been able to save him with a transfusion. His wife, who he also discovered to have been pregnant, was not gotten to in time.

Sean vowed revenge, promising that he would find the kids who'd done this to her. But he did not have to find one of them, for this one came to him instead. Realizing that he and his friends were in trouble, this school-teacher's nightmare said he would tell Sean where the others were hiding in exchange for immunity. He lied to him, really just wanting enough time to get out of the country before Sean came back to New York. The punk, who called himself Nigel, said that the other two could be found in a small town called Isle Voletta, in Louisiana. This was a place he'd been to before, and knew there were enough things there to keep Sean looking for a while. Sean, of course, tried to kill Nigel even after being told, but of course he had not been able to, as he did not know the correct means.

This lack of knowledge he took as a warning. Before he left for Voletta, he found his friend Cody, from the army, who had always talked about vampires and such things. Cody taught him to defend himself from vampires, but was unable to teach him the method for killing one. However, undiscouraged, Sean headed for Voletta, determined to destroy Elise's killers one way or another.
