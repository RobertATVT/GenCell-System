Background


Sebastian was raised in Melbourne, Australia until the age of 10 so he has an accent. After his parents died in a car wreck outside of Melbourne, he inherited the $500,000 they had earned in investment, and an antique silver pocket watch. He was sent to America to live with his Aunt Beth and Uncle Matthew in McAlester, Oklahoma. The money was put away in a savings account.
His uncle was a vampire hunter who took him on as an apprentice while his aunt taught him computers and continued teaching him to be a gentleman. After high school his uncle and he had a long talk and they decided it was time for him to move out. He moved to New York City in his new car to see the "Big Apple." There he fell in love with Claire Adams- a woman who turned out to be a vampire. He couldn't believe that he had fallen for an evil creature- or that the knowledge of that evil had not changed the way he felt for her.
He left NYC to stay with Beth and Matthew again, but his relations with his aunt and uncle had grown strained when they found out about his feelings for the vampiress. Eventually he transferred the money and left McAlester heading for a New Orleans, but he got lost and found himself in Isle Voletta...
