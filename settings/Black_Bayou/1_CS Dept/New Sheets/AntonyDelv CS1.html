Background


Born from the steriotypical Italian immigrant family, Antony Delvechi has not only the attitude, but the look too, of a sleezy lawyer. His facination with the law began when one of his close friends was beaten to within an inch of his life by two Police Officers. At the age of seventeen, it was pretty traumatic, and their reason, later heard, was even worse. 'Self defense within regulated accepted force.' They claimed that Rico had tried to fight back, but Antony had seen it all. Ever since that time, he held a grudge against anyone in a police uniform. On numerous occasions, he's actually had to have been held back to stop from striking someone in the course of their duty.
In 1997, at the age of twenty eight, he opened his own law firm, having until then worked under a company that dealt specifically with accident claims. Based in New Orleans, his reputation grew when the case of Rico Arman was brought up again, and won by the defence lawyer, Mr. Delvechi.
Now he accepts cases from as far afield as Mexico, and as close as Voletta. But his grudge against police brutality is always at the front of his mind, and he never refuses a case where there is a chance of such an event.
