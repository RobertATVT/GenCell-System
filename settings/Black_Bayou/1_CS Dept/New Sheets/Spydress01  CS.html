Physical Description
Corisica is of average height and above average build. Her muscles are very toned but she still has a feminine side. Her eyes are a Blue gray color with small piercing pupils. She has long black hair which she pulls back in a bun. She always wears a brown trench coat and often wears jeans.



Background
Corsica Lanheart was born on April 13, 1978. Her mother died while giving berth to her and Corsica grew up with her alcoholic father. She lived in a small suburb of Washington, DC where she attended public schools and at the age of 13 got her first job as a bag girl at a local grocery store. She got decent grades and attended jujitsu classes every other day after work. She got her first boyfriend at the age of 16. His name was Troy and he was a Senior. Corsica's father disapproved of her dating but let her go out only on the weekends. One night Troy brought Corsica home past her curfew. Corsica's father was furious and took himself, his daughters boyfriend and a wooden baseball bat out into the woods. Corsica was very angry and embarrassed so she secretly followed them. When corsica caught up with them Troy was on the ground dead and her father was on the ground with a wolfish creature feeding upon him. Corsica took the bat and beat the wolf unconscious. Corsica spent every day for three months trying to find as much information on werewolf's as possible. She learned how to use weapons and how to kill her enemies. Now she has come to a city in Louisiana to hunt down the creatures just as they hunted her loved ones. Corsica carries a Glock 17 on a concealed hip halster and a barretta 9 mm in a concealed side halster. She also has a 12 gauge shotgun that she carries in a back case. She conceals this with a trench coat.
