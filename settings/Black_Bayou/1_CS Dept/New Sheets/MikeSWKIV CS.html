Background



He was born in Louisville, Kentucky, a country boy at heart, but not in practice. Born the son of an attorney and a high school history teacher, they raised him in the best manner possible. Of course, that doesn't count for a whole lot when the kid you're raising is in public school. He was never much of a ladies man, but found his niche on the local public access channel in the 80's broadcasting a TV show that found a small cult-like following among small groups of students. It was this show that eventually got him hired at WQST, And the nickname Mad Mike.

His show at WQST, which ran from midnight to 4 am became a success, his smar-aleck antics and rough way of speaking attracting callers. However, one night he botched up, and a viewer took him literally....committing a murder. Mike was fired and shunned. After two years of blaming himself he moved to Voletta, grabbed an opprotunity to voice out at a small local fm station that broadcasted mostly to idiots at 6 am, and is determined to turn it all around.
