Appearance:

Catherine stands approximately 5'6" tall, with waves of strawberry blonde hair that falls past her shoulders. She has deep green eyes that border on fiery that strike her independent spirit. She is 26 and has a lithe slender body that is very toned due to her atheletic ability.


Background


Background:

Grew up in Atlanta, Georgia in a very political family. Her father George Mason was the governor of Georgia for two terms. Her mother ran the family and Catherine was taught all the proper etiquette of a nice southern girl. Even at this young age, her natural analytical mind and interest in law enforcement was seen. President of her class in High School, Valedictorian, she attended George Washington University in Washington DC. with the blessing of her controlling father who had hopes she would meet and marry a Senator or his son and play political liasion for him. She had her sights higher than that and much to his dismay she studied, criminal law, political science and investigative law. During her summers she interned at the Department of Criminal Justice in Washington DC. She graduated in the top 1% of her class and received a Masters Degree in Criminal Law with a minor in Political Science.

After graduation she returned back to Georgia. With help from her father, she entered a predominately male work enviorment and landed a job in the Internal Affairs Department of the Georgia State Police. With long hours, her skills and in depth analytical ability on cases involving cops, soon proved her worth to the department. Having achieved an award for excellence for convicting a group of Georgia cops that skimmed drugs from their busts and money that was suppose to be turned over to their departments. Wanting to move from the big city and hearing of an opening in Isle Voletta, she put in a transfer request to transfer to the Internal Affairs division there. While awaiting notice of an interview, she has taken a paid leave from the Georgia State Police to visit Voletta. Currently she is the Internal Affairs Director of the Plaquemine County Sherriff's Department.
