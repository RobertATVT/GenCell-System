My bank cards, everything that I had all the years I was held captive, gone, But I had my freedom. For how long, that I don't know.

The whole Hulk-David Banner scenario was now a possibility, but with her work and that of the few others. It could be controlled, unlike that in the old TV shows she use to watch as a child.
