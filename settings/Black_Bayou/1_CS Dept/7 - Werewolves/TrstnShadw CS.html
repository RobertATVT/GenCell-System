Healing - 0 Soul to use. (no room command needed)
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: Werewolves have an incredible natural healing ability. Any wound caused to the werewolf will heal at the rate of 1 Body per hour the character rests. If the character takes any strenuous action, the healing is disrupted and the hour starts over. A Room Host must be notified when you claim your Body point back. Wounds created by bane water cannot be healed with this mastery. Wounds from fire heal at the rate of 1 Body per day and only when the character sleeps. If the character is woken up before the day is over, the healing process is disrupted and they do not regain their point (which means they cannot role-play until the damage is healed). The damage from an attack that removes a limb heals at the normal rate, but the player must role-play the limb slowly growing back. Background


 Most people have meaningless lives, living day to day with pointless lives. They have no ambition, do little in the way of improving society, and generally make a nuisance of their pointless existence.
I have a purpose, a goal. I am driven by a burning desire for justice. There is one person out there who has eluded justice for too long. His name is Rane Shadow. He is my brother.
I shall kill him.
Ever since we were children, he was always considered better than I. Throughout our childhood, I was in his shadow. He was better at sports, better in school, better with relationships. Even our mother, our sole parent, always loved him more.
That's why I left home. I couldn't endure the constant comparison to him. It was always, "What's wrong with you? Why can't you be more like your brother Rane? You are identical twins, why are you so different?"
Blah. Talk about sickening.
I left home, but I couldn't leave town. Money was a factor. Sonya was the reason.
Sonya, a beautiful red headed lady of genuine upbringing. Classy, lovely, I needed her. I was never brave enough to tell her . . .the entire story. She would always listen to me, always comfort me when I approached her. She understood me. We would have been great together, she and I.
But Rane always got in the way. He couldn't leave well enough alone, and always had to upstage me with her. What was it with him? He wasn't happy enough I let him have everything else. He wanted her too.
He knew she was mine. I had her affections, he just had her pity. She told him one night, and in a jealous rage he killed her. He went on a rampage, killing several people that night. When the smoke cleared, he had ran, left town. The mob that followed went after me.
I barely survived what happened. The man hunt had decided it was my fault, and tortured me in place of him. I escaped, and had to run myself. He had killed the only person in the world I cared about, and left me to deal with the aftermath. He destroyed my life, so I dedicated mine to destroying his. I became a hunter, a bounty hunter. I don't want to bring him to justice. I want. . . Revenge.
It's been almost 2 years. That dear brother of mine had disappeared of the face of the earth. I was ready to give up the chase. Until, that is, his name suddenly resurfaced. Rane Shadow, proprietor of one Moiriarti Hotel in Isle Voletta, Louisiana. Now how would he get a hotel? It's probably far fetched, but it's the only lead in so long. If it's Rane, he'll pay. If not, he'll die for having such a sickening name.
