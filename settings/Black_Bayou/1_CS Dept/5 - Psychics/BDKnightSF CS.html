Meditation - 0 Soul to use. (##Meditation##)
System/Duration: NA / see below; takes 15 minutes to activate.
Effect/Description: In order to center themselves and replenish their mental energies after utilizing the power of the mind, a psychic needs to shut themselves off from the world around them and meditate. If they are interrupted (legitimately) for even a moment, the meditation must begin anew. 1 Soul Point may be regained each quarter-hour, going no higher than the original level.

Because the psychic is so mentally adept, no concentration is necessary to keep this mastery active. It may only be used once per Scene, but psychics can use any other mastery they own or act as they wish while this one is active.

Your Approved BackGround: ( Save this Email! )

Born and raised in El Paso, Texas, Bruce spent the majority of his childhood years on his parents' farm. After graduating highschool, Bruce was unable to go to college as his parents' financial status was poor at the time. Needing money for college, Bruce enlisted in the Military. Having worked as a farmhand all through his childhood, Bruce had become athletic and could use guns well from his days of hunting with his dad. Four years in the army further enhanced those abilities, and his drill sargeant recommended him for the Special Forces division. In the SF division, Bruce was put in the assassination group and trained in the art of ninjitsu. After 3 years in training, he was ready for real world situations and sent all over the world to work with various governments, coalitions and rebelions. His life was more that of a mercenary than of a Special Forces operative. He was always working with other SF operatives like himself, but he became more and more upset about his life. In 1996, after 15 years of military service, Bruce left the Military and returned to his hometown El Paso. It was there that in 1997, SF operatives and his former friends were sent to kill him as to make sure none of the information he'd gained from his service would be released. His house was destroyed, and he was shot twice in the chest, but not before he succeeded in killing his assailants. He was hospitalized for 6 months. Now, 2 years later, he comes to Voletta in hopes of escaping his past.
