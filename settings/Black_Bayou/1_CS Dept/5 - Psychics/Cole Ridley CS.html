Angry, aroused, excited, and distraught are only examples.

Meditation - 0 Soul to use. (##Meditation##)
System/Duration: NA / see below; takes 15 minutes to activate.
Effect/Description: In order to center themselves and replenish their mental energies after utilizing the power of the mind, a psychic needs to shut themselves off from the world around them and meditate. By sitting alone and relaxing, concentrating on a candle flame, or some sort of talisman while chanting, the psychic can slowly restore their soul. They must meditate in the main room. If they are interrupted (legitimately) for even a moment, the meditation must begin anew. 1 Soul Point may be regained each quarter-hour, going no higher than the original level.

Mind Shield - 0 Soul to use. (##MindShield##)
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs TN 18 / Scene; takes 1 round to activate.

Because the psychic is so mentally adept, no concentration is necessary to keep this mastery active. It may only be used once per Scene, but psychics can use any other mastery they own or act as they wish while this one is active.

Background


There isn't a lot to tell. He has always been this way: quiet; almost sullen, from the time he was born in London, from where the family immigrated to America before he could talk. He extracts people from their shells; picks them apart; analyzes - and neglects, always, to do the same with himself. He has never quite been anyone's fantasy.
Cole was hospitalized at the age of seventeen for psychiatric problems (undiagnosed). Not only was he potentially suicidal (read the report); he may have been hallucinating. He claimed he could do things. He learned, there, to keep his mouth closed. Cigarettes and liquor did nothing for his reputation, minor as they might have been. He went to school with the intention of staying along for his doctorate, and dropped out a year later; took up the piano and doesn't play (though he does, actually, quite well; prodigy, they whispered). He wrote, though, and continues to write; this is a constant force.
As is his infatuation with a certain old friend. Unbeknownst to the girl, Ridley fell in love with her - not that he would ever say anything, no. He isn't so stupid. Besides - it would be hard enough to say it; how could anyone -possibley- expect him to -sign- it? He did learn the language for her.
This time, he intends to become a writer.
