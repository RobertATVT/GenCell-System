These insightful individuals can occasionally pinpoint someone else's emotional state just by observing them for a few seconds. The victim must IM the mortal, describing their emotional state. Angry, aroused, excited, and distraught are only examples.

Meditation - 0 Soul to use. (##Meditation##)
System/Duration: NA / see below; takes 15 minutes to activate.
Effect/Description: In order to center themselves and replenish their mental energies after utilizing the power of the mind, a psychic needs to shut themselves off from the world around them and meditate. By sitting alone and relaxing, concentrating on a candle flame, or some sort of talisman while chanting, the psychic can slowly restore their soul. They must meditate in the main room. If they are interrupted (legitimately) for even a moment, the meditation must begin anew. 1 Soul Point may be regained each quarter-hour, going no higher than the original level.

Mind Shield - 0 Soul to use. (##MindShield##)
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs TN 18 / Scene; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: It is difficult for a Psychic to keep their wits about them, and even more difficult when outside forces try to penetrate their mind. By blocking everything out, they can prevent anyone from manipulating their mind, thus adding +3 to their defensive Will rolls. Because the psychic is so mentally adept, no concentration is necessary to keep this mastery active. It may only be used once per Scene, but psychics can use any other mastery they own or act as they wish while this one is active.

Masteries

Mental Blast - 1 Soul to use. (##MentalBlast ##)
Generation Cost: 15 / Experience Cost: 75
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs victim's 2d6+Will / Instant; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: A few Psychics have found another way to use mindspeak. By increasing the telepathic flow toward a subject, they can over load their mind. If this is kept up, or if it is powerful enough, then the target may eventually fall unconscious. This buildup causes intense feedback, like white noise dealing 5 points of Mind damage. Due to the great mental force and Psychic energy put into the attack, a Psychic may not perform any masteries the round following a Mental Blast. The victim must be within line of sight.

Forces (requires Telekenesis) - 1 Soul to use. (##Forces ##)
Generation Cost: 5 / Experience Cost: 25
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs victim's 2d6+Dodge / Instant; takes 1 round to activate.

If the psychic wins the roll contest by more than 5 points, the victim is knocked to the ground. Any character on the ground suffers -4 to all physical rolls unless they have the Wrestling fighting skill. This mastery may not be used on inanimate objects.

Background

At the ripe old age of fifty-three Gunner Allricson has spent his life, like his father before him as a servant for the Kington Manor. After years of working for them his most respected master summoned him into his study and as he read from a rather thick book he told Gunner of his son who was staying in the brutish city or town known as Voletta in the what he described as beatnik state of Louisiana.

Going down state side from upper main did not appeal to him but he lived for his work, he lived for perfection and Maxwell could not select someone more loyal to do such a dead. With two heavily arched eyebrows Gunner nodded and asked when he was to go. My Kington told him right away and that is how he found himself in this town.

Standing at around five foot seven and rather thin and aging badly because of years of hard work or possibly heredity he wasn't well suited in the arts of combat but he did have the latent abilities of his father who was a psychics. Gunner never really practiced this art but in one fit of anger which was on the outside completely calm he did make a maid faint.

On the Prophet home, seeing the door was in need of cleaning. As Gabriel Opened the door he looked at the house which he thought to be at least livable but by no means Kington clean, However this would change very soon. He handed Gabe the note from his father on why he was sent and that no child should grow up without a butler to raise them proper like. Gabe being mostly brought up by Gunner greeted him with a smile and an Open Arm. Gunner made a list for cleaning supplies.
