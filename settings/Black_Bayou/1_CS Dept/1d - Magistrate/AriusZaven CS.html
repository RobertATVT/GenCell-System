Special Abilities



 Empathy - 0 Soul to use. (##Empathy ##)
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Mind vs. TN 16 / Instant; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: Psychics have learned to read the facial expressions and body language of others. These insightful individuals can occasionally pinpoint someone else's emotional state just by observing them for a few seconds. The victim must IM the mortal, describing their emotional state. Angry, aroused, excited, and distraught are only examples.

 Meditation - 0 Soul to use. (##Meditation##)
System/Duration: NA / see below; takes 15 minutes to activate.
Effect/Description: In order to center themselves and replenish their mental energies after utilizing the power of the mind, a psychic needs to shut themselves off from the world around them and meditate. By sitting alone and relaxing, concentrating on a candle flame, or some sort of talisman while chanting, the psychic can slowly restore their soul. They must meditate in the main room. If they are interrupted (legitimately) for even a moment, the meditation must begin anew. 1 Soul Point may be regained each quarter-hour, going no higher than the original level.

Mind Shield - 0 Soul to use. (##MindShield##)
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs TN 18 / Scene; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: It is difficult for a Psychic to keep their wits about them, and even more difficult when outside forces try to penetrate their mind. By blocking everything out, they can prevent anyone from manipulating their mind, thus adding +3 to their defensive Will rolls. Because the psychic is so mentally adept, no concentration is necessary to keep this mastery active. It may only be used once per Scene, but psychics can use any other mastery they own or act as they wish while this one is active.

 Mindspeak - 0 Soul to use. (>>::target of Mindspeak:: message<<)
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: By concentrating on a chosen target, the psychic is capable of speaking directly to the creature's mind. Mortals are unable to return the thoughts. The target must be within view of the psychic attempting to communicate in this fashion. Mindspeak can only be used to speak to one individual at a time.

Masteries



 Isolation - 1 Soul to use. (##Isolation ##)
Generation Cost: 10 / Experience Cost: 50
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs victim's 2d6+Will / 4 rounds or 2 minutes; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: How would a Psychic react if they suddenly lost the ability to communicate, but didn't know it? If they just thought everyone was ignoring them? How would other people react if they thought the person was ignoring them? This works the same way, but with Mindspeak. The victim has no knowledge they have been blocked. The victim loses all ability to Mindspeak. You must posses at least a level 1 lore of the victims breed and know for a fact that they are of that breed before this may be used.

 Mental Blast - 1 Soul to use. (##MentalBlast ##)
Generation Cost: 15 / Experience Cost: 75
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs victim's 2d6+Will / Instant; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: A few Psychics have found another way to use mindspeak. By increasing the telepathic flow toward a subject, they can over load their mind. If this is kept up, or if it is powerful enough, then the target may eventually fall unconscious. This buildup causes intense feedback, like white noise dealing 5 points of Mind damage. Due to the great mental force and Psychic energy put into the attack, a Psychic may not perform any masteries the round following a Mental Blast. The victim must be within line of sight.

 Mind Scrye - 1 Soul to use. (##MindScrye ##)
Generation Cost: 10 / Experience Cost: 50
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs target's 2d6+Will / Instant; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: Gives the psychic the ability to understand thoughts, even pictures of what is in a person's mind. The psychic may eavesdrop into the surface thoughts in a person's mind. Surface thoughts are basically what the victim is thinking about at that moment. The psychic may not add, change or delete these thoughts. They are only a spectator. The psychic may ask 3 questions in an instant message of a specific nature. The answers must be as specific as the questions. The victim must be touched for their mind to be read. This mastery cannot be used to discover another's breed.

Pyrokinesis - 1 Soul to use. (##Pyro ##)
Generation Cost: 20 / Experience Cost: 100
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs TN 26 / 4 rounds or 2 minutes; takes 2 rounds to activate.
Effect/Description: By focusing his energies on a specific object, the Psychic is able to manipulate it on an atomic level. Through this practice, the Psychic can cause the atoms of nonliving objects to speed up at an amazing speed, generating intense heat, and causes a highly combustible material (i.e. gasoline) to catch fire. The target need only be within sight to use this mastery. The object will catch fire, or begin to turn to cinder, or melt. If metal is heated, it will cause 2 points of Body damage to anyone who comes in contact with it. After 2 rounds after the object was heated, the metal can be touched again without harm. The material must be stated prior to this mastery being used. Any material may be used, however, there must be a direct line of sight to the material. You would not be able to set a gas tank ablaze, simply because you cannot see the gas. Bullets (if the gun itself is heated) or other explosives heated in this manner will explode causing the normal damage +2 to anyone holding the item.
