</FONT><FONT  SIZE=3 PTSIZE=9><B><I>Law </FONT><FONT  COLOR="#000000" SIZE=3 PTSIZE=9></I>(Mind)<BR>
System: </B>roll 2d6+Mind+Law Skill Level vs TN (see below)<B><BR>
Description: </B> If arrested and the character goes to court (in email), a Storyteller will be made available to witness the roll above.  If this roll is needed against more than one charge at a time, the player must roll for each charge.  Each charge after the first takes a cumulative -5 penalty.   Listed below is the level of each charge a character can be brought up on (their descriptions are listed below this skill for ease of reference), with it's respective TN.  For every three points the player beats the TN by, the charge is dropped a level.  For instance, Eric Johnsn is brought up on charge of attempted murder which is a 2nd degree felony (TN: 22).  Rolling 2d6+Mind+Law, he scores a total of 29.  He beat the target number by 7, which drops the charges by two.  He is instead found guilty of a Class A Misdemeanor.<BR>
