</P><P ALIGN=CENTER>Physical Description</FONT><FONT  COLOR="#000000" SIZE=2 PTSIZE=8></B></I></U><BR>

</P><P ALIGN=JUSTIFY></FONT><FONT  COLOR="#800000" SIZE=2 PTSIZE=8>Standard Senior Deputies in Isle Voletta are charged with enforcement of the laws and protection of the Peace and Dignity of the Parish and are the rolemodels for their younger Deputy counterparts. There are about 5 of them on patrol, one usually in each section of the city with a new Deputy. Their gold star gleams from the left chest of a Kakhi Uniform while mirrored shades are perched on their nose. They patrol in black and tan Ford Crown Victoria sedans, sometimes in the drivers seat, but mostly they let the rookies drive. Each Senior Deputy carries the same Sam Browne utility belt as their counterpart, though the Senior Deputies is usually a little more worn.  It contains his Baretta F14 Sidearm, as well as two 9mm clips for the pistol, each containing 15 rounds. Two sets of handcuffs, a cannister of pepper spray, a walkie talkie with lapel mic, and a MagLite Flashlight also hang from the Senior Deputy's waist. Under the kakhi of the uniform tunic is a Kevlar vest. (-2 damage against Small Arms. -1 damage against Large Arms.Wearer receives a -1 to their Dodge modifier.) In the cruiser's front seat is a locking rack for a Benelli Super 90 Riot Shotgun. The crowd pleaser packs nine shells in the magazine. In the event of a serious disturbance, the trunk of the patrol car contains equipment for Riot Control, including tear gas grenades and launcher.(2D6+Will Vs TN 24, Werewolves are -4 to the resistance roll because of their enhanced senses.  If successful, victim is -4 to Attack and Quickness for 4 rounds or 2 minutes ~This affects ALL breeds except Vampire, Demon and Angel~). There is also a Taser (NonLethal Weapons) and equipment for blocking off a crime scene, including flares, evidence baggies, drug identification kit, crime scene tape, and a gas mask. There is also a utilitarian tool kit, usable for minimal roadside assistance. Senior Deputies are authorized to detain, arrest and transport to the Sheriff's Station anybody suspected of breaking the laws of Voletta Parish. Use of deadly force is always a last resort, but self defense is never denied.  Unlike a code 5 for general backup in which Deputies arrive, the Senior Deputy will only arrive for Code 6 calls from Deputies already on the ground and in desperate trouble.<BR>
