Assistant Storyteller Advisor Job Description


Breed Coordinator ~ This job includes the tracking and managements of Angels and Demons. This management includes the following responisbilities:
#1 Tracking the amount of Angels & Demons in the game.
#2 Recieving/Answering requests for open Angel/Demon spots.
#3 Following the Guidelines for Angel/Demon Approvel or Generation.
a. First a spot must be requested through ANT BB Mail
b. Should a spot be available, a Storyline needs to be submitted explaining the
breed change in detail. (How, Why, Who, etc...)
c. If the player is genning the character as an Angel/Demon this is when they
send in the Background. If they are Breed Changing, then they must wrte
up three posts (Having to do with the Storyline) and include them on their
BC CS.
d. The Character sheet is sent to the BreedCo to be approved.
#4 Approving all Angel/Demon Character Sheets.
#5 Answering questions pertaining to Angels and Demons

Storyteller Trainer ~ This job includes the training of new Storytellers using a standardized curriculum prepared by the ST Advisor. The duties also include looking over the trained ST's to see if retraining is needed.
