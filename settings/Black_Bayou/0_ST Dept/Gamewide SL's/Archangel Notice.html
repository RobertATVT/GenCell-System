GAME WIDE SL - Silver Future - Africa - July 1st


A few weeks ago a horrible blow was dealt to the Angel Breeds. Six Archangels were slain throughout the world, 53 total Angels killed in the process.

Sudan, Central African Repulbic and Cameroon had been engulfed in flames as an out of control border raid from Eritrea into Ethiopia fails. A not so nice branch of Hunters, had managed to worked their way into this War and a destructive attempt to destroy an encampment of soldiers (Angels) turns into massive kill zone. The fighting between the two groups caused fires to dot along the dry grasslands, and spread west into theh three above mentioned countries.

Four Arch Angels are to die in this massive destruction. This supposidly inconsequentail strip of land is actually the location that the body of Ramses was deposited upon centuries ago after he gave chase to Moses upon the parting of the Red Sea. Cursed by God, the remains held about them an unholy taint that spread about the desert and has caused nothing but stride and striff for anyone or anything that has ever lived there. The Four slaughtered Angels had been attempting to Bless this blemish for centuries - their attempts had not gone unnoticed and the Hunters attacked.

Little is known about the Hunters that slaughtered many of your kind. One can assume that there is a group that has struck out against the Angel Breed. There is whispered words along the gossip network that the name Bii is one to be feared.

You and your Flock may not have any connection to those across the waters. But word of mouth does travel and the above information has been dropped in your ear.
