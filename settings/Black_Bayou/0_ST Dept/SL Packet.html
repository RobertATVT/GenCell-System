Section One
Occupations
I'm going to list each Occupation and the reasons for needing an SL to get this occupation started.

Chief Executive Officer
Generation Cost: 33 / Experience Cost: 99
These are the leaders of any major business. They are the ones that make the major decisions, doing so for the greater good of business, regardless of what many people think about them. They are paid a small fortune, but that's only because the amount of responsibility they hold. They must manage things so that one little thing will never tip the balance of the company and cause the stock prices to crumble. To purchase this occupation, you must have an approved SL. Get one at BBST Mail.

Wouldn't it be wonderful if we all could be millionairs? There wouldn't be any world hunger and everyone would live in peace and harmony. Unfortunately, that isnt' realistic. Granted this is a fiction game and reality has little bearing on it. Or does it? The element of conflict is needed for all things.
Therefore, we have this set up with a storyline requirement. What types of things would we need listed in this SL. The Company your a CEO of, why did they send you to Isle Voletta, what will you be doing in the Isle, are you setting up a new branch in the Isle.
Those are the main bits of information that is required.

Section Two
Age
Age can play a very important part in the life of a demon, angel and vampire. With age comes power. How can you make an old character with the current guidelines for generation?
It's very simple. You can't. There isn't a way for a new character to gen at an older characters rate, you have to earn your points.
However, with a storyline you can create an older character. How? Simple. Give us a good reason to allow you to be old. Were you sleeping from wounds done to you? Were you watch the world from on high? Maybe you were trapped in hell waiting for release to march the earth again? Or perhaps you walked the earth several times but were forced into deep hiding making you unable to learn to be very powerful?
All of these are valid options for an SL of age to be approved.

Section Three
Breed

There are only a few reasons why a SL would be needed to become a breed, and that is to become an Angel or a Demon, or to leave an existing breed to become Mortal. In the event of what breeds can do what I will bring up the small chart in the rulebook that shows what a character can change to.


Current Breed May Change To:
Mortal Psychic, Lamia, Vampire, Werewolf, Angel or Demon
Psychic Mortal, Vampire, Werewolf, Angel or Demon
Lamia Mortal, Vampire, Werewolf, Angel or Demon
Vampire Mortal, Angel, Demon (Only with an Approved Storyline!)
Werewolf Mortal, Angel, Demon (Only with an Approved Storyline!)
Angel Mortal
Demon Mortal

 All of the Highlighted sections above need a Storyline to be passed, with the exception of a Vampire and Werewolf becoming a mortal, all of them need to be approved by the Breed Coordinator and the SL Dept., which means that you will have a minimum of two people that will be looking over your SL. The things that are looked for overall is creativity and depth. Just saying that you pray to the dark and a demon is sent to you... doesn't cut it. If you have a very well written storyline and it's all dark and vile and you get to the end and you have the Demon of Bugs Bunny sent into you... well that won't work either. Angel and Demon storylines should be more than I just wanted to become one, they should include motive and some amount of intrigue. Demons and Angels don't decide to meld with a host on a whim, it's a very long process of choosing who is right for the job. Why would a Demon or an Angel want to use you for a host? These answers should be in the Sl and it will not be approved without them.
