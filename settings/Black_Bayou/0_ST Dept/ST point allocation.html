Table for allocating ST Points (Effective July 1, 2000)


Witnessing rolls ~
Standard rolls (Anything taking less then 30 minutes) : No points.
Extended Rolls (30 minutes to an hour) : 1 point.
It should never take over an hour to witness rolls.

ST Events:

If there is a log turned in, and they actually went to Inits. : 1 Point
Fights between 1 and 2 hours : 1 Point
2 and 4 hours: 2 Points
Over 4 hours: 3 Points

Anyone STing a fight that lasts more then 4 hours should also be placed in for A&B points.
