^v^ What you are and what ^v^
you might become...


What your character is right now, we would call a mortal. Mortals are just like you and I. The world your character lives in, however, has its subtle differences. The creatures of folklore are not myths. With any luck, they might leave you alone, but don't count on it. If one of them happens to curse you with their gifts, you will immediately loose all of your current masteries and special abilities (you may keep the skill levels from the Gift, but lose Phenom, for instance). Also, if the new breed has a mastery that you have already purchased under another breed, you may keep them. In return, you gain the temptation of the breeds power. This includes changes to your statistics and the new special abilities. All of these are listed below. You must have had an approved character sheet for 30 days before you can change breeds. You may not change breeds more than once in one update and you may not update your character sheet more than once in a 30 day period.

Note: The following descriptions are what you might hear from the old guy who lives in the alley or in hushed conversation. Some of it is true, some of it is not.

Psychics: What are these strange people? Controlling their mind more than any of us could hope. They perform all kinds of tricks with their so called "sixth sense", but are they still one of us? Are they people or just another freak?
Should you ever decide to search for this answer yourself, you must study the power of the mind through role-play over a period of no less than 30 days or meet the requirements listed in the Psychic Breed area.

 Requirements for Change: Should you ever decide to search for this answer yourself, you must study the power of the mind through role-play over a period of no less than 30 days. In your Character Sheet update in which you change breeds you must include the screenname of the one training you in the Psychic Arts and a link of one post of the training.

Stat Changes: -1 Body, +2 Mind, +1 Soul
Special Abilities: Mind Shield, Mindspeak, Meditation, Empathy


Lamia: Spell weavers, whether by incantation, ritual or sheer force of will. These mortals wield magnificent powers, being able to shape consciousness at will. Though every mortal has the capacity for spell weaving of one form or another, a dedication of study and the right mentor is required.

Requirements for Change: Should you ever decide to study the arts, you can do so through role-play over a period of no less than 30 days or meet the requirements listed in the Lamia Breed area. In the Character Sheet update in which you change breeds to Lamia you must include the screenname of the one training you in the Lamia Arts and a link of one post of the training.

Stat Changes: -1 Body, +2 Mind, +1 Soul
Special Abilities: Animal Mimicry, Nightvision, Healing, Meditation


Vampires: These are the creatures of the night. Shunning the sunlight for it's deadly rays, the vampire stalks the city and the swamps of the bayou, searching, hunting for its next living meal. They are deadly hunters, resembling humans in almost every way. Being drained of all your blood turns you into one of them.

Requirements for Change: To become a vampire you must be bitten in role play by an approved vampire after a period of no less than 30 days or meet the requirements listed in the Vampire Breed area. In the Character Sheet update in which you change breeds you must include the screenname of the one siring you and a link to the post of the ST'd blood rush roll (Must be a registered ST who witnessed the roll, not another player subbing in for the roll).

Stat Changes: +1 Body, +2 Mind, -1 Soul
Special Abilities: Healing, Nightvision, Mindspeak, Bat Form


Ghouls: Slaves; the vampires' caretakers. Created by a vampire feeding the mortal its blood. The mortal's body is empowered with supernatural strength for a short amount of time. In return, the newly created ghoul is entranced by the vampire, totally willing to do it's bidding. Ghouls often take care of a vampires business during the day, ensuring that the bills get paid, the house is taken care of, the deliveries are received and the suspicious turned away.

Requirements for Change: To become a ghoul you must be fed by an approved vampire through role-play after a period of no less than 30 days. In the Character Sheet update in which you change breeds you must include the screenname to the Vampire ghouling your character and a link to posting of the ST'd rolls for the success of the ghouling (Must be a registered ST who witnessed the rolls, not another player subbing in for the roll).

Stat Changes: +2 Body, -1 Mind, -1 Soul


Werewolves: The ultimate hunters. These man-beasts prowl the countryside and the bayous, ravaging those that cross them. A ritual and a mere bite of one of these monsters could transfer the curse of lycanthropy, turning a human into a ravaging monster.

Requirements for Change: To become a werewolf you must be bit and a ritual performed by an approved werewolf through role-play after a period of no less than 30 days or meet the requirements listed in the Werewolf Breed area. In the Character Sheet update in which you character changes breeds you must include the screenname of the one who bit you and a link to the required ritual that must be posted before the biting.

Stat Changes: +2 Body, -1 Mind, +1 Soul
Special Abilities: Healing, Mindspeak, Wolf Form, Horus Form


Angels: These holy messengers walk the earth, valiantly fighting sin and evil in all it's forms. They speak the gospel, spreading the truth and the word of the light to those that will listen. They help those in need when it matters the most. An angel is a spiritual force that will join itself with that of a mortal at one of two times. Either when a person lives such a righteous life that they gain an Angel's attention, or when a person is truly living darkly, if they renounce their sin completely, an angel will sometimes join with them to help restore their soul.

Requirements for Change: To become an Angel you must give up your body to the light as a vessel with which to spread it's rays after a period of no less then 30 days and receive approval from a request to LDRS ANT BB Mail to play the breed. In the Character Sheet update in which you change breeds you must include a copy of the approval letter for the breed change and one post involved in the possession of the mortal body to serve the light.

Stat Changes: -1 Body, +1 Mind, +2 Soul
Special Abilities: Volange, Gift Of Life, Healing, Mindspeak


Demons: The work of evil is never done, and the sinister "powers that be" use these foot soldiers to cause havoc and destroy lives. Contracting souls for their master is their specialty. Demons will possess the bodies of mortals, taking complete control of it's host. From this guise, the demon works it's evil, manipulation - it's weapon.

Requirements for Change: To become a Demon you must give up your body to the Darkness as a vessel with which to spread corruption after a period of no less then 30 days and receive approval from a request to LDRS ANT BB Mail to play the breed. In the update in which you change breeds you must include a copy of the approval letter for the breed change and one post involved in the possession of the mortal body to serve the Darkness.

Stat Changes: +1 Body, -1 Mind, +2 Soul
Special Abilities: Locust, Liar's Ears, Healing, Mindspeak


The Second Breed Change:
If your character changes breeds for the second time since you created the character you must remove the first breed bonus you got for the second breed's bonus. For instance if your character started with 5, 5, 5 for statistics as mortal and you became a Psychic those statistics would become 4, 7, 6. Now say a resilient Vampire resisted the hallucinogenic effects of your character's blood long enough to sire your character. Statistic wise on your CS you would remove the previous bonus to set your Statistics back to 5, 5, 5 first then add the Vampire breed bonus making your final statistics 6, 7, 4. You may not stack breed bonuses on top of each other.
