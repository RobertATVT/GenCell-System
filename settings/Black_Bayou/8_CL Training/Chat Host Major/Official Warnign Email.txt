<B>Notes on the Official OSW Mail</B><BR>
<BR>
 * The e-mail format must be followed <B>exactly</B> to insure the mail is compatible with CAT's tools.<BR>
<BR>
	- The subject line must be the letters OSW, colon, space, member's screen name.<BR>
<BR>
	- The screen name in the "Screenname" field does not have to be lowercase; the preference is for a copy/paste to avoid errors.<BR>
<BR>
	- Do not use the <I>Tab</I> key to create space between field names and their respective entries. There can be no space or more than one space, but no <I>Tab</I> characters. Tab characters are only permitted in the chat clip where they occur as part of the copy/paste from the chat room window.<BR>
<BR>
	- The "Location" field does not have to be a path. The forum/area/category name and the name of the room, as indicated in the example e-mail, is the correct format.<BR>
<BR>
	- The chat clip should be pasted in immediately following the "Example" field as indicated in the example e-mail. Do not press the <I>Enter</I> key and paste the chat clip on the following line.<BR>
<BR>
	- "Example:" can be "EXample:" but no other variations in field names are supported at this time.<BR>
<BR>
 * Use a new e-mail every time you submit an OSW mail (do not use forward or reply when resubmitting a corrected mail).<BR>
<BR>
 * If you need to provide further explanation of the violation, use the "Notes:" field. Do not add anything to the actual chat clip.<BR>
