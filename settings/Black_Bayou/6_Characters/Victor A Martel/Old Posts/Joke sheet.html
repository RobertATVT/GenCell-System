Special Abilities


Healing - 0 Soul to use. (no room command needed)
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: Demons have an incredible natural healing ability. Any wound caused to the demon or its host body will heal at the rate of 1 Body per hour the character rests. If the character takes any strenuous action, the healing is disrupted and the hour starts over. A Room Host must be notified when you claim your Body point back. Wounds created by faith water cannot be healed with this mastery. Wounds from fire heal at the rate of 1 Body per day and only when the character sleeps. If the character is woken up before the day is over, the healing process is disrupted and they do not regain their point (which means they cannot role-play until the damage is healed). The damage from an attack that removes a limb heals at the normal rate, but the player must role-play the limb slowly growing back. (Length of time for the limb to regenerate is determined by the Forum Director.)

Liars Ears - 0 Soul to use. (##LiarsEars##)
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs TN 22 / 10 minutes; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: A demon, being a part of the lord of lies is capable of picking out any attempts of deception directed at it. This is effective against anyone who attempts to lie to the demon. They must immediately IM the demon's player with the truth behind their lie.

Locust - 0 Soul to use. (##TDF##)
System/Duration: NA/NA; takes 1 round to shape shift.
Effect/Description: A demon is capable of forcing its true form upon its mortal host body. A demon that assumes the locust form will rip free from its host body, physically. Their skin will crack and split, falling off of the demon in a macabre display. A typical demon in its locust form stands about 9 feet tall. Its skin is a deep reddish tint with long, stringy black hair. The body is covered in a hard carapace that helps protect it from physical harm. Its black lips curl up from the sickly yellow fangs as its eyeless sockets stare on. Black, leathery wings sprout from its back, enabling it to fly at a speed of 25 mph. Once the demon releases its locust form, the beast will shimmer slightly as the host's flesh slowly begins to reappear over the creature's features and it returns to its moral form. Locust form grants the demon +3 body and gives it the ability to fly (as described above).

(Just a quick note about shape changing (all forms, except Transcendence). While a demon is in another form, if its current Body falls to 0, the demon will immediately change back to mortal form minus the damage it has already taken, leaving a minimum of 1 Body. It should also be noted that materiel objects do not shapeshift with the character. Any and all clothes and objects the character was holding will need to be transported by some other means.)

Mindspeak - 0 Soul to use. (>>::target of Mindspeak:: message<<)
System/Duration: NA/NA
Effect/Description: By concentrating on a chosen target, the demon is capable of speaking directly to the creature's mind. Mortals are unable to return the thoughts. The target must be within view of the demon attempting to communicate in this fashion. Mindspeak can only be used to speak to one individual at a time.

Masteries


Soul Mask - 1 Soul to use. (##SoulMask ##)
Generation Cost: 5 / Experience Cost: 25
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs TN 20 / Scene; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: This is the ability to hide a demon breed from those with the ability to Scrye such things. Whether it is a Lamia's spell, a werewolf's scent or even an angel's true sight. The demon chooses the breed it wants to be detected as, as long as it has Lore level 1 of that breed.

Terror - 1 Soul to use. (##Terror ##)
Generation Cost: 5 / Experience Cost: 25
System/Duration: roll 2d6+Will vs 2d6+Will / 4 rounds or 2 minutes; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: By making eye contact, the demon can exert its demonic will over a victim, paralyzing it with fear. If successful, the victim will be at -4 to Attack and Quickness for the duration of the mastery. The victim snaps out of this stunning fear as the instinct for survival kicks in when the duration expires.


Alpha Masteries

Summon The Beast - 0 Soul to use. (##SummonBeast##)
Experience Point Cost: Innate
System/Duration: N/A; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: Even the wisest and most peaceful of beings understands that use of force is sometimes a necessary thing. The "next step" in self discipline, the ability to summon forth the beast during even the most relaxed situations. By way of this mastery, the Horus form can be called forth whenever the Alpha desires. When they willingly bring their beast to the surface, they are treated the exact same as any other Horus except for a limited intelligence that remains. While they are unable to communicate or use mental masteries, they are capable of discerning who is an enemy and who is not, combine their attacks to those they choose. As stated above, it takes 1 round to summon forth the beast within. It takes and additional round to actually shape change to the Horus form. No target is required when the Horus is brought about in this manner.

Taming The Beast - 0 Soul to use. (##Tame ##)
Experience Point Cost: Innate
System/Duration: 2d6+Will vs victim's 2d6+Will / Scene; takes 1 round to activate.
Effect/Description: Although rare, it is not unheard of for a werewolf to get out of line within the pack. Most Alphas have realized the death of their fellows serves absolutely no purpose. Therefore, some Alphas have learned alternate ways to handle to disobedient. During a total eclipse of the moon, all werewolves temporarily "loose the wolf". Meaning, they lose all of their masteries, shape changing abilities and for all intents and purposes, become mortal until the light of the moon once again graces the sky. An Alpha who has studied this phenomena can attempt to duplicate it's affects upon a single individual by sheer force of will. Should the Alpha beat the victim in the test of wills mentioned above, the victim will effectively loose the use of all their masteries for the rest of the scene. This includes everything from shape changing to Mindspeak. If the victim is in wolf or Horus form, they will immediately revert back to their mortal form. The use of this mastery is often considered a great insult by many werewolves and if used without good reason, could result in an overthrow of the Alpha.

Physical Description
Victor is 6'-7" tall with a bald head and a tribal crest tattoo that goes around his head from widows peak to occipital lobe and bright green eyes. Victor wears mostly Black clothes with high tunnel neck shirts and well made steel toed boots. When in a bar Victor is nice and charming with plenty of good things to say to all, however the callous uncaring look in his cold eyes seems to have softened unless dealing with an enemy to his family.

Wolf Description
Victors wolf form is that of a large Brendel striped black wolf with green eyes. The stripes are a greyish silver color and Victor has his earings large enough that the show when he is in wolf form, his tattoos also show up in his wolf form as silver markings on his black skin. Victors Horus form is more lean and lithe than usual and his head has an almost pit-bullish look to it, as does his legs.

Background

Victor Martel was born in Barcelona, Spain in 1972. He has worked in Bars from Spain to Los Angeles. Bars are his life, bars house all kinds of interesting things, interesting people....Especially interesting people, Victor loves meeting new people, Interesting people. Victor doesn't have a steady long time love, his job won't allow it. He likes to travel and go from Bar to Bar, if he can work in some other bar in some new city with new people, he'll go in a heartbeat.
