Technology

Magi: As explained in a previous SL, Dominus Magi granted permission for Fairlight to use any and all resources they possessed. Facing going bankrupt, Fairlight now pays for their building, personnel, etc. all under the table. Managed by Banhood.

Imagica: As explained in a previous SL, Milan deNoir has full control over his corporation, with Fairlight reaping the profits, and engineering's efforts of the company all under the table. Managed by MilandeNor.

Jameson Technologies: As explained in Jameson's own SL, he is an up and coming technology giant who has just created a technology firm. He has been merged in Fairlight as a subsidiary. Managed by: AlecJamesn.

Treen Telecommunications: In Cooperation with AT&T, Treen Telecommunications was named in honor of Jim Treen. Serving the better Louisiana Area to give users another alternative rather than just the one monopoly baby bell in the area. Treen Telecommunications operates using a small satellite leased from AT&T over the Louisiana area. They are a cell phone opperator and builder along with one of the first to join TCI Cable with the TCI@Home Digital Internet service. Managed by: NPC.

Entertainment.

Mythic Press And Books: The European leading seller, publisher and maker of all gothic books. Currently in line with the American Press agent Ballentine Books to get rights to publish Anne Rice over sees and split the profits. This publisher oversees Arius Zaven's best selling novels. Operated from London, England. Managed by Arius Zaven.

Nite Time Fantasy Magazine: Unsure of what to do with this fantastic magazine, he saw it's advertising start to go downhill and has taken it upon himself to save it. Contacting Nite, he has given enough money for the magazine to stay afloat, in fact, he is even thinking of having it as a Sunday insert into the Bayou Bugle or more. Managed by niteangeli.

The Voletta Symphony: Aware his best friend and confidant has fallen ill, and may even be approaching death. Zion immediately purchased the ailing Voletta Symphony in the conductor Doctor Sinclair's honor. Ensuring, that it would survive, he awaits for Sinclair's health to improve so he may give the man this gift. Managed by NPC.

(Also the Bayou Bugle and WKRV-NBC can be included but are more defined in other SLs)

Other Businesses.

Dark Shipping: Bought out by Fairlight, Angeleen Dark's shipping company is definitely smaller that the Billaud Fleet, but serves it's purpose by supplying travel and raw materials to Fairlight from afar. Not only owning a small barge fleet, but at least 3 cargo planes as well. Managed by Angeleen.

Dark Pharmaceuticals: Once upon a time, this business produced drugs for Angeleen's empire. Now, swept clean from that corruption, Dark Pharmaceuticals is a needed asset to Fairlight's medical powers. Managed by Angeleen.

Dark Construction (now titled Lighthouse Construction Company ): In honor of the first thing the renewed construction force built, this construction company was owned by Angeleen Dark, and now serves as Fairlight's main contractor. Managed by NPC.

Rental Cars R Us: Using Zion's vast collection of cars purchased over the years, through several means and using various aliases, Zion, often times, uses this corporation to rent himself and his employees out the cars of choice at a discounted Buyer's rate (to purchase the cars in the first place). Not really serving the general population, this resource grants Fairlight with many different cars for many different occasions. (festivals, balls, holidays, etc.). Managed by Zion St. Croix under unknown alias Enrique Fe Rodriguez.

Chapaev Bank & Trust: Opperating also the shadow corporation Mercy Point Bank and Trust (under assumed identities, its own staff, etc etc), the Chappy is in a league of their own. ALthough not technically owne dby fairlight, Fairlight works very closely with them. Managed by maialndmt.

Eclipse Security: Taking over the fledgling security company, Fairlight plans to make them high-tech and bursting with glory. Owned 60% by Fairlight and 40% by the manager, himself, this security corporation will help people install their own systems into homes and will work with anyone whenever necessary. Managed by VicMartel.

Omsford Investigations: A Private investigations firm funded exclusively by Fairlight. Omsford isused to test out technology devices for field work and the like in an investigative atmosphere. A bunch of specialists in various surveilance and investigation fields work there. Land, building, etc. is all owned by Fairlight but managed exclusively by Seth Omsford. Managed by Soulreaper12.

All managers, in one way or another, are independant. If information is requested on them, Zion recieves the notice and does the contacting, himself.
