It was a typical day for Vic. Meditation and self sacrifice had become the staples of his life. He was torn between two women that he would readily give his life for. One was his creator, the Golden haired goddess that gave him his animal. The other a raven haired mystery that tantalized him and brought that animal to the fore. The first was fully alive, vibrant and breathing life every moment. The second was a creature of night and shadows who slept cold and unbreathing.


A world of Violent rage

having never seen the color of my fathers eyes.

They both touch his soul in places that have been locked and closed for over a decade, and it hurts. How they have managed and when it started is shrouded in the fog of lost memory. He knows what he feels... he is a man of emotion.


Yes I dwell in hell
but it's a hell that I can grip
tried to grip my family but I slipped

When Victor got into Voletta and began meeting people he met Skye Heart in the first couple weeks. From that day on he was smitten by her likeness to his long dead love, and was enraptured by her gentle loving kindness. She was fair haired and appeared like an angel to lift the sorowful burden from his shoulders. Only to be replaced by the weight of his unreturned love. In a fit of pain he went as far as to shoot himself to keep from hurting her, to keep his rage from taking over. boy did that hurt.

Victor met Cleo in the Symposium and offered her a job since she was leaving town to find one. He also saw something else in her, from that first moment he knew that he would see a lot more of her. More than that, he knew that he wanted to see more of her.
