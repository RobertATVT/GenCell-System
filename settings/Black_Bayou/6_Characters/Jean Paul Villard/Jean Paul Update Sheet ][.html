Physical Description
 Jean Paul was born in Paris, France on February 10th, 1977 to Anne Marie and Jacque Villard. He is 6 ft /8inches tall and 335 lbs. Lithe, muscular build with Long Black hair and Grey eyes that almost look silvery. He wears simple comfortable clothing, of which he has only three changes, and very thin wore out boots, he always wears a pair of odd old looking bands of cloth wrapped up his forearms, these are the Sacred Wraps of the Northern Dragons, given to him by his former mentor Shao Pei Tung. He is quiet and introspective, when he speaks he does so softly like an old Oriental wise man but with an odd accent that is mostly French with a little Chinese mixed in...


Background

Jean Paul grew up in the Temple of Light in China as a Shaolin Monk. He has just begin to see the outside world and is troubled with the corruption and shadows that lie across the world. The spirits are tormented and distraught, and this saddens Jean Paul (and Fei Ling). He knows only the arts taught him by his Shaolin Masters and is travelling the world to learn new things to bring to his fellow monks and to help weary souls where he can.
