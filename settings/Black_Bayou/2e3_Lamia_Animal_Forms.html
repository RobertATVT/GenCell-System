Animal Forms

When a Lamia is first created, they are allowed to chose one form that they can use as the basis of an illusional shapechange. This is more of a cosmetic change than a physical one. That means, while a bird form may enable the Lamia to fly and a form with claws may allow the Lamia to use the natural weapons skill (if the player has purchased it), they are unable to use the special abilities associated with the animal. A dog form does not enable the Lamia to see or hear better, a snake form does not allow the Lamia to inject poison into a victim, etc.

When a Lamia undergoes a shapechange using this ability, they succumb to the statistic / modifier changes associated with the type of animal they are changing to. There is a specific order that these statistic / modifier changes come in. As soon as the Lamia changes back to their mortal form, they reverse the changes the animal had granted them, taking into account any damage the Lamia may have received while in their alternate form.

First of all, the Lamia changes their Body statistic to the one associated with the animal. This Body change affects the Lamia's modifiers as normal, reducing all the modifies associated with the Body statistic. Secondly, the animal form changes the Lamia's Quickness modifier using the formula associated with the animal. This change to the Quickness modifier adds to the Lamia's Initiative and Dodge modifiers as normal. Again, both the statistic and modifier changes revert back to normal (subtracting any damage the Lamia may have sustained while in the animal form).

Here is an example:
If the Lamia's stats are, "Body: 6 Mind: 5 Soul: 6," their relevant modifiers would be, "Attack: 11 Quickness: 12". If the animal form they chose happens to be an alley cat (Small Feline), when the Lamia shapeshifts into this form, their statistics become, "Body: 3 Mind: 5 Soul: 6," and their modifiers also become, "Attack: 8 Quickness: 15".

If you have chosen your animal form prior to this list being published and your animal does not appear on this list, please write BBST Mail and we will inform you to which group your animal form will belong to. If you choose a form after this list has been published, then you must choose a form from the list below.

(Just a quick note about shape changing (all forms). While a Lamia is in another form, if its current Body falls to 0, the Lamia will immediately change back to mortal form minus the damage it has already taken, leaving a minimum of 1 Body. It should also be noted that materiel objects do not shapeshift with the character. Any and all clothes and objects the character was holding will need to be transported by some other means.)
