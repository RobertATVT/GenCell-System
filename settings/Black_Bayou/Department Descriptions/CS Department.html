Character Sheet Department
Position Descriptions

The Department:

The Character Sheet Department is a integral part of the flowing of the game. As all characters generated have to have an approved Character Sheet, this department has a hard responsibility to maintain records on Character Sheets, Check the math on all sheets, tally experience point records and to make sure all is correct on character sheets for the entire game.

Department Manager:

*Responsible for Character Sheet Reactivations of 6 months or longer
*Answers player mail regarding Character Sheets
*Oversee the entire Character Sheet Department
*Keep up with any delays with the department and notify the FD
*Generate ideas, suggestions and improvements to help the Character Sheet Department.
*Answer player complaints regarding Character Sheet Department
*Write mail to players that sheets are being pulled or investigated because of XP problems
*Handle departmental problems as they arise

Assistant Manager:

*Overview all Character Sheets that are sent out.
*Distribute character sheets that are sent from the game mail service
*Review all Staff Sheets
*Support the entire team answering questions and helping where needed.
*Generate new ideas, suggestions and help streamline the department

Character Sheet Department:

*Review all characters sheets assigned for non-updates, updates, activations and
reactivations.
*Make sure that the backgrounds have been approved for new sheets
*Review the math on all sheets for correctness along with XP records
*Submit discrepancies to the Character Sheet Manager
*Make sure that the turn around time on all sheets are within the specified guidelines.
